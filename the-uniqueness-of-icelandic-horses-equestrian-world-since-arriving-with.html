Since arriving with the first settlers over a thousand years ago, Icelandic Horses have been isolated on their little Island without any genetic input from other breeds. Thanks to strict regulations, the Icelandic Horse remains pure breed and due to the geographical location, very few equine diseases occur.

But this also means - no other horse can enter the country and once a horse leaves Iceland, it can never return!
