This recipe from grandma stunned everyone! I've never eaten such a delicious cake! Unsurprisingly, This is one of my all-time favorite recipes. Creamy cream, delicate cakes. Everyone loves this recipe and is very happy when I make it for the whole family. I do this a lot for friends and family because they love it. Very easy to do for everyone. Just follow the simple steps and voila, the delicious cake is ready and everyone is happy. Try this easy and delicious recipe and let me know in the " How do you like it?" Good day friends!

INGREDIENTS
VANILLA CREAM :
All-purpose flour ( 1 tbsp )
Milk ( 500 ml )
2 eggs
Sugar ( 135 g )
Cornstarch ( 30 g )
Butter ( 170 g )

CAKE PASTRY :
Milk ( 80 ml )
Water ( 80 ml )
Butter ( 60 g )
Sugar ( 2 tbsp )
Salt ( 1/2 tsp )
All-purpose flour ( 95 g )
3 eggs

2 SPRINGFORM PANS ( 20 CM )

BAKE AT 190 DEGREES
FOR 25-30 MINUTES
