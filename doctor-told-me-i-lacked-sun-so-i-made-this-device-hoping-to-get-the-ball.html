Hoping to get the ball rolling again with this Sunshine Dispenser device! Using the micro:bit platform it can communicate with a previously installed motor in my blinds to open them with morning sun, dispense vitamin D supplements, and shut the blinds by yelling.
Many of the electronics in this video were provided by the nice people over at STEM Education Works. Check their kits out on their website! https://stemeducationworks.com/
