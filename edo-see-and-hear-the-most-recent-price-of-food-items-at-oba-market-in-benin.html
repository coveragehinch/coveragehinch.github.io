In today's update on the price of food items in Benin city, our focus is on Oba Market In Benin city which is one of the biggest and most popular markets in Benin city.

During our visit to the market, it was observed that business activities were currently ongoing as traders and buyers were seen doing negotiations and transactions.

During a chat through sections with some of the traders regarding prices of foodstuff like rice, beans, melons, pepper, tomato, onion, carrot, vegetable and others. Their response was that things are very expensive and they blame it on the fuel price and the current exchange rate of the dollar to naira.

Some also said the reasons for the hike in price is as a result of the bad roads in the state while some also said is a result of the issues between farmers and herders in the bushes.
