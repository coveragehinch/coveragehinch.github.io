There are three components to make a progressive overload.

1: More weight than the previous week but foem

2: When You can’t do more weight, do more reps, again keeping tight form, your form should never vary.

3: If you can’t go up in weight and you can’t do more reps didn’t slow down your rep speed while keeping it tight form.
