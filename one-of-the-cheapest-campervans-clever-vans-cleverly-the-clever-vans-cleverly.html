The Clever Vans Cleverly is amongst the cheapest campervans in Europe - although please remember that the word cheapest is not at all cheap, and furthermore if something is cheap then there is a reason for it! This van is based on the Citroen Jumper platform, and contrary to widely published claims, cannot be purchased for under EUR30k.
