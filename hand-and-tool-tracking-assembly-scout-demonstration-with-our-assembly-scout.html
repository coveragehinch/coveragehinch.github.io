nexonar - next generation tracking technology - Components and solutions for 3D motion tracking and coordinate measurements. The nexonar product series offers technical options as a cost-effective alternative to building a dedicated 3D position capturing and measurement application.

nexonar Assembly Scout is our system that provides precise tool positioning and/or positioning of hands and fingers.
