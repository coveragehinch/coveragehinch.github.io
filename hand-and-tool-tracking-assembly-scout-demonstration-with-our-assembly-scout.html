nexonar - next generation tracking technology - Components and solutions for 3D motion tracking and coordinate measurements. The nexonar product series offers technical options as a cost-effective alternative to building a dedicated 3D position capturing and measurement application.

nexonar Assembly Scout is our system that provides precise tool positioning and/or positioning of hands and fingers. It can accurately capture and control movements in large measurement areas within millimeters. With the realtime capabilities workers can be clearly guided through the assembly process.
