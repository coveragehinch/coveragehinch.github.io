For one of the Ariana Grande "Monster Hunter" challenges in Fortnite Battle Royale, you need to "Reveal the Command Symbol"

There's a few spots around the map where you can do this, but we shall go to the one at Corny Crops. Simply land where I do, and you need to emote, jump and swing your pickaxe in the respective corners to reveal the command symbol and complete this challenge / quest.
