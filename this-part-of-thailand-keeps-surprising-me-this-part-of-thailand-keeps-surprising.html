I made it to Nong Khai, a small town right on the Mekong River in Isan Thailand. On the other side of the river, we can see Laos. Also, the capital of Laos, Vientiane isn’t far away from Nong Khai. Just a 40 minutes ride. So, I thought it would be interesting to jump on the bike and ride from Nong Khai to Vientiane, at least to the point where we can see it from the Thailand side.
