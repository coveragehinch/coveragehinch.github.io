While we cannot prepare to take on a multi national war on all fronts, we can prepare for the secondary events that do affect our families. From the UK to Canada, Japan, and Australia, we will begin seeing a falling off of product availability that will continue to affect modern life.

In the example noted insulin is the most important item that needs stop gap measures.
