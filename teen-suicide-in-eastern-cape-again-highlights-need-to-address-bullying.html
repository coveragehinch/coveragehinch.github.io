The suicide of a 16-year-old schoolgirl this week has again highlighted the problem of bullying. Lathitha Nako, a grade 10 learner at Bisho High School in the Eastern Cape, swallowed pesticide and died in hospital on Wednesday. Her devastated family is demanding that bullying at schools must urgently be addressed to prevent similar tragedies. Before her death, she spoke openly about how she was targeted on social media platforms.
