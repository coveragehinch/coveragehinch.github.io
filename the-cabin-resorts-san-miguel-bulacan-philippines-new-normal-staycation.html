The Cabin Resorts is located in San Miguel, Bulacan, Philippines. This must visit new normal staycation is just 2 hour drive from Manila.

The Cabin Resorts Bulacan offers a unique staycation as it is in the middle of a rice field! Their cabins are beautiful and perfect to those who want a unique stay near Manila!

Bulacan is also home to countless resorts with pools but The Cabin Resorts stands out due to its high-end feel and vibe!

Things to note:
- They have 2 types of cabins/accommodation (Deluxe for couple and Loft for families). See below for the prices.
- If you are bringing a car, sedans can not go directly to The Cabin Resort's property due to the rough road going to the resort. There's a parking area in Gulf Gas station where you can park your car and The Cabin Resort's service will pick you up.
- They don't have day tour
- They have a restaurant. You can eat there or have your food delivered at your cabin
- Check in is as early as 1 PM and check-out is at 12 PM
- Cabin Resorts Bulacan is a pet-friendly resort!
- They allow kids in the resort :)
- They follow safety and health protocols

Published rates:
Deluxe - 6,500 Php / night
Loft - 12,500 Php / night
