He wrote a book of his generation and brands at his age of 14. He has a YouTube channel about sneakers with thousands of followers, where he talks about exploring stories behind brands. He is a top athlete, he studies in Austria as an Alpine ski racer, and spends his summer vacations at the campus of University of Oxford.
