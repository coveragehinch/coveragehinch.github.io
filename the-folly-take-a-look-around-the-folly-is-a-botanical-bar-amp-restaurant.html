The Folly is a botanical bar & restaurant close to Cannon Street & Bank and moments from Monument station. From our garden-inspired lounge to the private dining room seating up to 35, The Folly is perfect for all-day drinking, dining & unwinding. Take a look at our gorgeous bar & restaurant in this video.
Part of the Drake & Morgan collection, The Folly serves a seasonal menu, gorgeous cocktails and fine wines ready for to indulge in. Get together with your friends, family or colleagues & escape the ordinary. Find yourself at The Folly.
