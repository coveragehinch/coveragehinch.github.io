Fifty year-old Australian mountaineer Lincoln attempted to climb Everest in 1984 but didn't reach the summit. Now, twenty-two years later, he has a second chance. Despite his age, Lincoln fulfils his lifelong dream, but on the descent is struck by a deadly form of altitude sickness known as a cerebral oedema. Unable to climb and suffering from hypothermia, Lincoln is left for dead at twenty-eight thousand feet. His survival is nothing short of a miracle.
