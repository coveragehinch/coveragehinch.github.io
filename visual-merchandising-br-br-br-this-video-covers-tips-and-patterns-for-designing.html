This video covers tips and patterns for designing a store effectively so as to be able to direct customers correctly and easily. In addition, it tackles uses of colors and their effect on creating an impression on customers. It will also cover the importance of the display window and its impact on the customer’s decision to enter the store and the product display skills to increase their interest in purchasing
