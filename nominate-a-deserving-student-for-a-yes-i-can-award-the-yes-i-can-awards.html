The Yes I Can! Awards were established to honor children and youth with disabilities who have excelled. More than 30,000 children and youth have been recognized since the programs inception in 1982.



Each year, CEC selects approximately 27 winners for their outstanding achievements in one of nine categories: academics, arts, athletics, community service, employment, extracurricular activities, independent living skills, self-advocacy, and technology.



For more information, including eligibility guidelines and the nominations packet, visit www.cec.sped.org.
