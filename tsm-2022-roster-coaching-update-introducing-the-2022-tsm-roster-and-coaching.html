Introducing the 2022 TSM roster and coaching staff:

Joining Spica, Huni, and Tactical on the TSM LCS roster are two little-known players hailing from the LDL: Keiaduo and Shenyi.

Keiaduo dominated TSM's month-long scouting combine. He stood out as the top-performing mid-laner, bringing an aggressive look and crisp teamfighting.

Shenyi was the first choice at support from the moment he entered TSM's scouting combine. He showed unparalleled proactivity, map control and flexibility, making him a key component of the team.

Taking the helm at head coach is a familiar face to longtime LoL fans: former world-class midlaner Chawy. With experience coaching both western and eastern teams, Chawy is the natural fit to lead the 2022 TSM roster to domestic and international success.

Bring on '22.
