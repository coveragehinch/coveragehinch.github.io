Development is a branch of geography which refers to the standard of living and quality of life.



Measures of development (lots of different measures)

Life expectancy
Literacy rates
Access to clean water
GNI
HDI - Human Development Index - Life expectancy, number of years in education and GNI per head

Development Gap and reducing the gap.

The development gap refers to the widening gap between the richest (most developed) and poorest (least developed) countries of the world

Investment (industrial)
Tourism
Aid
Fair trade
Debt relief
Loans (microfinance)
