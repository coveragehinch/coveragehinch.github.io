I made this video back in November 2019 when I visited Wuhan for the first time. I’ve been thinking for a while now about when would I should post it. Actually, I can’t think of a better time than right now. There’s a lot of misinformation going around about what kind of city Wuhan is, with a lot of people who have never even visited the city judging it negatively. Hopefully this video can show people what a wonderful city Wuhan is.
