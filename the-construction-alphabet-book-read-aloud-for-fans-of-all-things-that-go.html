For fans of all things that go this noisy alphabet book explores construction equipment from A to Z. Find out about these construction machines and more, from a huge saw that cuts through roads to a massive vacuum that sucks up boulders. You'll even learn a quick and easy recipe for concrete. Rock crushers, jackhammers, and wrecking balls will delight the youngest of readers learning their ABCs. The book covers the aerial lift, backhoe, cement mixer, dump truck, excavator, front-end loader, grader, horizontal borer, impact hammer, jackhammer, knuckleboom loader, laser, mobile rock crusher, night lights, off-road dump truck, paver, quad-axel lowbed, road cutter, scraper, tower crane, utility truck, vactor, wrecking ball, x-bracing, yard crane, zipper, asphalt reclaimer, bulldozer, and compactor.

Jerry Pallotta's trademark humor punctuates the informative text. Vibrant oil paintings bring to life a busy construction site.
