This outstandingly located one bedroom top floor apartment can be found in the much sought after area of Latchi, and is 200m walk of the beach and marina, and offers the most amazing sea & mountain views.

This modernised apartment is being sold with title deeds and is fully furnished.

Latchi is a village just 5 minutes from the town of Polis and is well known for its exciting water sports and crystal clear water as well as its large number of fish restaurants. This apartment is ideal not only for investment purposes but also for permanent residency. Shops and cashpoint machines are within easy walking distance.

As soon as one walks through the front door one cannot help but be impressed the apartment is light & bright with brand new fitted windows & doors complete with fly screens. A delightful archway leads from the hallway to the lounge/diner.

The Lounge/Diner is spacious and comes complete with two 2-seater settees, glass coffee table and glass shelfing cabinet. The modern dining table and chairs set is large enough to accommodate 4 diners. From here one can enter straight out on the large balcony to the left where you have amazing sea and marina views or to the right balcony with beautiful mountain views.

Just imagine luxuriating on this balcony with a cup of coffee watching the sunrise in the east and then relaxing with a glass of chilled Cyprus wine in the evening as the sun sets in a glowing ball in the west, prior to strolling to one of the many family owned restaurants.

The well equipped kitchen which includes Hotpoint washing machine, ROWA oven, Hob, Fridge Freezer and SAMSUNG microwave and the kitchen has plenty of cupboard and storage space with a breakfast bar area.

The master bedroom includes doors leading directly to the balcony, fitted wardrobes, stunning views and plenty of space.

The modern Bathroom is on the right hand side of entering the apartment.

Owners of this property also have the use of a delightful communal pool for those days when it is just too hot to walk the extra two minutes to the beach.

This apartment is located in one of the best locations in Cyprus.
