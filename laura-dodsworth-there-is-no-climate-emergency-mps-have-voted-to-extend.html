MPs have voted to extend the Coronavirus Act for another six months to March 2022.

Although the Act is not behind lockdowns, it allows authorities to impose a restrictions on the public, including the power to detain people suspected of having Covid for up to 28 days.

The Act also included powers for the Health Secretary to shut down individual events, gatherings, shops or restaurants if they pose a specific risk.
