Rheumatoid arthritis (RA) is a lifelong condition that causes inflammation that can
damage the joints. No treatment can cure RA, but the drugs in this video can slow down the disease and reduce damage to joints. They can relieve pain and make it easier to do everyday tasks. Most people can find an RA drug that works for them. This video can help you work with your doctor or nurse to find a medicine for RA.
