The most sacred mountain in Tibet which has never been summited, Mount Kailash. This documentary is based on Tom Riddles September 2015 trip, his third trip around Mt Kailash in Tibet. On this trip he took a GoPro mounted on a gimbal. In 25 minutes he offers highlights of the trip and a few reflections of how things have changed in the last 10 years in Tibet.
