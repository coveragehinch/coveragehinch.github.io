FC Barcelona returned to winning ways in the Turkish Airlines EuroLeague Regular Season by overwhelming Bitci Baskonia Vitoria-Gasteiz 93-67 at Palau Blaugrana on Thursday. Barcelona improved to 7-2 in the standings, snapping a two-game losing streak, while Baskonia dropped to 3-6. Nikola Mirotic led the winners with 25 points. Nico Laprovittola and Brandon Davies each added 11 for Barcelona. Steven Enoch led Baskonia with 22 points while Rokas Giedraitis added 12.
