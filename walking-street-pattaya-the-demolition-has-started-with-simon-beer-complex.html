Walking Street Pattaya has stood as one of the best nightspots in the entertainment world for decades. Sadly now it appears things are very much changing as the signs are crystal clear as Simons Beer Complex in the heart of Walking Street is demolished.

So what is on the agenda for the Pattaya iconic location? Will they really clean up the bars and agogo's and turn this very famous Street into a more family friendly destination?

I would have said a few changes yes, perhaps, but this demolition of a Walking Street bar complex shows that this time the Pattaya officials really do mean business!
