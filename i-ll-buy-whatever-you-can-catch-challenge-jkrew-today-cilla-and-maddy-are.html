Today Cilla and Maddy are going shopping again and this time whatever they can catch I’ll buy it. This challenge was very fun. This challenge had no budget, but they can only buy anything they can catch, if they don’t catch it and it falls to the ground, they must put it back. The first store they went to was Hallmark, there are a lot of cool things to buy. Cilla and Maddy really love Squishmellow’s so I decided to throw a few at them. How many Squishmellow’s did they catch? You must watch and see how many cute Squishmellow’s they’ll catch, or did they catch any? The next store Cilla and Maddy went to was Target. So threw a whole stack of clothes at them, which made it harder to catch. Do you think this shopping challenge was successful? You must watch and see for yourself!
