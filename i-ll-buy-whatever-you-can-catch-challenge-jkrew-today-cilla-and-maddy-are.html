Today Cilla and Maddy are going shopping again and this time whatever they can catch I’ll buy it. This challenge was very fun. I (mom) how much the girls love to go shopping especially Maddy, so I decided to make a challenge out of it. This challenge had no budget, but they can only buy anything they can catch, if they don’t catch it and it falls to the ground, they must put it back. The first store they went to was Hallmark, there are a lot of cool things to buy. Cilla and Maddy really love Squishmellow’s so I decided to throw a few at them. How many Squishmellow’s did they catch? You must watch and see how many cute Squishmellow’s they’ll catch, or did they catch any? The next store Cilla and Maddy went to was Target. Target is by far their favorite store. There I decided to throw cute clothes at them. Throwing clothes at them was way too easy. So threw a whole stack of clothes at them, which made it harder to catch. Do you think this shopping challenge was successful? You must watch and see for yourself!
