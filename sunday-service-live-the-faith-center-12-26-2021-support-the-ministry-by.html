An inspirational speaker, author, community leader, and businessman, Henry is a man on a mission—a man with purpose. Driven by a passion to make a difference in the world, his desire is to see people maximize their full potential and use their God-given gifts to influence others to make a difference. Charismatic in his delivery, he brings a strong message of hope—both in and away from the pulpit.
