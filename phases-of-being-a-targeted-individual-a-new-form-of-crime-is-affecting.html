On the flip side you could going on with your life for years without being aware.

Typically phases that targeted individuals go through are more or less
1. Non Victim, everything appears to be normal no drama

2. List of Strange Stuff - from work, to mails being tamperred with to getting strange phone calls

3. Intense harassment- can take anyform such as workplace mobbing, community harassment

4. You have the terminology, you know the terms such as gang stalking, electronic harassment

5. Living the rest of your life - a hard challenge but part of a new reality

At the moment there is no way for targeted individuals to get justice for the crimes.
