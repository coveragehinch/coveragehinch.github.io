This step-by-step tutorial shows you how to knit an easy headband using a loom. So, if you have never loom knitted before, this project is perfect for you! And if you are already a loom knitter, this video tutorial may help you guide your family and friends on how to start with your beloved hobby.

In this tutorial you will learn:
- How to start your headband (how to cast on stitches on the loom)
- How to knit your headband (how to work e-wrap knit stitches)
- How to take your work out of the loom and close the headband (how to bind off)
