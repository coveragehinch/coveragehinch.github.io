No doubt about it, traveling is stressful. From booking flights, packing, checking in, security lines and taking off your shoes, juggling gates and making sure you don’t miss your flight… oof. Have you ever had a broken wheel or zipper on your suitcase? Talking about annoying! As frequent fliers, we’re big fans of anything that can make traveling easier.
In this episode of Products Worth Talking About, we’ll unpack the luggage industry and look at the Goliath of suitcases, Samsonite, versus the hottest luggage in the market, Away Travel (known as Away).
