"sanagi" is the eighth single (fourth major label single) released by POSSIBILITY. The main was chosen to be the fourth ending theme of the anime Gintama. The single reached #17 on the Oricon charts and charted for six weeks, selling 11,836 copies. It's the group's most successfull single so far.
