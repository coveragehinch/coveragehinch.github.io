Please join us for some devotions and a fireside chat. This week we will be talking about the document "The Prosperity of Humankind" with Dr. Chris Johnson.

Dr. Christopher J. Johnson received his Ph.D. in Sociology from Iowa State University with a major in Aging and Family. His minor is Social Psychology. His UG degree is in Philosophy from Denver. Dr. Johnson had a small practice as a licensed Marriage and Family Therapist and LPC in Louisiana. At the University of Louisiana, he was Director of Gerontology and Professor of Sociology at the Institute of Gerontology where he retired after 25 years. He taught in the Dementia Studies graduate program at the University of Stirling, Scotland and worked for the Dementia Services Centre for two years and then came to Texas State to initiate and be a major contributor to America's first program in Dementia and Aging Studies (link to a program description)--there are 17 such programs in Europe and Austria. He and his wife Dr. Roxann Johnson pioneered Scotland for two years while she completed her dissertation in Dementia Studies. He has been a featured speaker in Scotland and Northern Ireland Baha'i Summer Schools. He has served on numerous LSAs and spoken at Texas, Mississippi and Louisiana Baha'i Winter and Summer School.
