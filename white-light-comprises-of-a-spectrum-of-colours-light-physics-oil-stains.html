Oil stains show rainbow colours for the same reason that a prism disperses white light into seven colours. The reason is that "white" light or sunlight is really the presence of a wide range of colours or frequencies at once. The human brain perceives these signals together as "white". Different frequencies refract at different angles and by using a prism, we can separate these components to see them as separate colours.
