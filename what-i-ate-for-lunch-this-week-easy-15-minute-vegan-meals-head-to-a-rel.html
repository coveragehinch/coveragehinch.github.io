vol-au-vent filling:
- 1/4 head finely diced cauliflower
- 1/4 cup finely diced leek
- 300ml coconut cream
- 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
- 1/2 tsp salt
- pinch of pepper

easy + delicious tempeh:
(for rice paper rolls & easy tempeh wrap)
- 100g soy tempeh
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp soy sauce

one-pot pasta:
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 spring onion
- 1 clove of garlic, diced
- 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
- 1 tsp dried basil
- 400ml crushed tomatoes
- 400ml water
- 1/4 tsp salt
- pinch of pepper
- 150g pasta

peanut butter sauce:
(for rice paper rolls)
- 1 heaped tbsp peanut butter
- 2 tsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- water (until desired consistency)

crispy chickpeas:
(for salad)
- 400g can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp dried basil
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
baked 8-10 minutes at 180°C fan-forced

savoury chickpea pancakes:
- 1 cup chickpea flour
- 1/4 cup nutritional yeast
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1/4 tsp salt
- pinch of pepper
- juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1/2 cup water (add more water to get to desired consistency)
+ tomatoes, feta, other veggies if desired :)

warm black bean salad:
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- fresh corn (from 1 cob)
- 300g black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1/2 cup rice (pre-cooked or from microwave packet)
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp smoked paprika
- 1 head cos lettuce
- 6-8 cherry tomatoes
- 1 heaped tbsp coconut yoghurt
- fresh coriander and spring onion to serve
