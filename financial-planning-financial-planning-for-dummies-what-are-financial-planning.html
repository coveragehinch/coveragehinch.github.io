Financial Planning Definition

A financial planner looks at the client's financial picture, and advises them about how they can achieve their long-term and short-term goals. Financial planners build trusting relationships with clients in order to give them confidence and help to ensure their future.

Financial Planning Checklist
Financial planners who are ethical and competent combine the various parts of clients' financial lives to create a plan for the future. This is why it's so important to have deep expertise in all these subjects.

What's the point of a financial planning plan?
Each client is treated as an individual by a personal financial advisor. A financial planner provides holistic advice that guides clients in all areas of finances to help them achieve their financial goals. You may enjoy working as a financial advisor to clients if you are a person who is open to listening, asking the right questions and creating a plan to achieve those goals.
