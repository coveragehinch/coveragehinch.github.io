Sinccera is a solution developed by Objective which helps companies with managing multiple teams, projects and products, enabling better decision making with a unified portfolio view. Through dashboards it’s possible to have predictability and create hypotheses based on reliable data aiming at the most efficient delivery plan.

Dashboards with minimal data structure representing time, scope, cost and defects, automatically filled in throughout the project/product development, allows you to see the system as a whole and be able to read its signals, elements and interactions to make decisions that influence directly and leverage the results, investing in a “dynamic balance” and exerting efficiency and effectiveness.

About the Objective

Objective is a Brazilian multinational organization founded in 1995 by developers and friends as high-performance multi-disciplinary teams. We help our customers break technological barriers through our digital products, consulting services, training, and software development. We focus on quality beyond software, creating value for our clients.

We developed our own way of managing and operating a software company, adopting and evolving our tools and agile methodology throughout our history. Sinccera was developed to solve our own challenges in scaling agile software development.

With more than 300 restless specialists and creators based in São Paulo with offices in Chicago-IL, Curitiba-PR, and Maringá-PR we continue to help our customers deliver agile results at scale.
