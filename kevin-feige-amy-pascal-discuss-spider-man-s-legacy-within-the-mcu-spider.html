Spider-Man: No Way Home Executive Producers Kevin Feige and Amy Pascal talk about the rewards and responsibilities of producing Spider-Man movies between two studios, as well as seeing their careers coming full circle with a little help from the MCU multiverse.



Marvel President Kevin Feige and movie producer Amy Pascal both discuss the legacy of Spider-Man in the comics, honoring Stan Lee and Steve Ditko's vision for Peter Parker in film, and getting to use villains from the Sam Raimi and Marc Webb Spider-Man movies, such as Green Goblin, Doc Ock, Electro, Sandman, and The Lizard.



And speaking of legacy, Feige and Pascal also address whether or not a certain icon line would be uttered in its entirety in the Tom Holland/Jon Watts trilogy.
