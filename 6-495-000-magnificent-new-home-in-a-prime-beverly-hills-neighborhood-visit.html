✔️ ABOUT THE PROPERTY: 128 S WETHERLY DR, BEVERLY HILLS, CA
Opportunity of a lifetime to own a magnificent new home in a prime Beverly Hills Neighborhood. Perfectly executed and balanced between design and function, this three story masterpiece will take your breath away. As you enter through the five foot wide pivoting door, 'The Wetherly' reveals a rare balance between modern and warm. The high end finishes, perfect workmanship and abundant natural light are felt throughout the home. The heart of the home is its dynamic kitchen and den. Top of the line Miele appliances are neatly tucked away in exquisite cabinetry. The caesarstone center island offers seating for five. A wall to wall Fleetwood 4000 pocket door system allows for a seamless indoor outdoor flow and the incredible seven foot tall Carrara marble light-up feature wall by the sparkling pool anchors this private backyard oasis. The semi-formal dining room floats between two large exterior glass areas. As you walk up the artistic staircase to your left are three generous en-suite bedrooms and a laundry room.
