Dev, along with many other content creators, is a little disenchanted with the standard slog. It's not like Eldraine, a format that was filled to the brim with 10/10 cards and overpowered decks; no, instead it's something much worse: it's boring. Bas aren't always the best thing for a format, but sometimes they can make things feel fresh again, if only for just a little while. So today, we'll cover five cards that don't necessarily need to be banned, but if they WERE banned, Dev wouldn't care at all.
