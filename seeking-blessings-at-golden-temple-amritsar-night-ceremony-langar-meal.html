Our first evening in Amritsar, we spent a few hours at the Golden Temple, where we were fortunate enough to watch the night ceremony after seeking darshan, and also nourish our body with a comforting meal at the langar. We returned the next morning to bask more in the spiritual glow of Sri Harmandir Sahib that is not only a central religious place of the Sikhs, but also a symbol of human brotherhood and equality. Notwithstanding the teeming masses that converge here all times of the day, there is a certain peace that envelopes the mind when one spends time here. In this vlog, we have made a humble attempt to share certain aspects of our spiritual experience at this unique temple that embraces people of all caste, creed and faith. 🙏🙏
