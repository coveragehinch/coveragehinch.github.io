We returned the next morning to bask more in the spiritual glow of Sri Harmandir Sahib that is not only a central religious place of the Sikhs, but also a symbol of human brotherhood and equality. Notwithstanding the teeming masses that converge here all times of the day, there is a certain peace that envelopes the mind when one spends time here.
