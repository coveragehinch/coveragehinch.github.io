A land full of greenery, close to mother earth, enriched with fertile soil: is our small village named as Ambaulim situated in Quepem taluka.
We the people of this beautiful village live in complete harmony and peace irrespective of religion, caste or creed.
Our song 'Amcho Ganv Ambaulim' presents our culture, our traditions, our lifestyles and our journey from our ancestors to the present generation, filled with professionals in every field of time.
We thank everyone for their constant support, love n concern in the making of this song.
