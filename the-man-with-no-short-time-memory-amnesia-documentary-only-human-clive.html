Clive Wearing has one of the worst cases of amnesia in the world. This documentary focuses on his bizarre and remarkable story, once a renowned conductor and musician, Clive was struck down in 1985 by a virus that caused massive damage to his brain.
Against the odds, doctors managed to save his life but he was left with a memory that spans just seven seconds.
