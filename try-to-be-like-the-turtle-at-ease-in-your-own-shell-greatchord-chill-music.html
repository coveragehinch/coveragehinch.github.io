Greatchord is a creative music school located in the German town of Baden-Baden. The school provides comprehensive piano education, from beginner to advanced levels, for all ages and abilities. Greatchord has a library that accommodates every musical taste, with more than 7500 titles on CD and DVD available for rental or purchase.
