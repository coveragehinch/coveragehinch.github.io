Greatchord is a creative music school located in the German town of Baden-Baden. The school provides comprehensive piano education, from beginner to advanced levels, for all ages and abilities. Students can study privately or take group classes with other musicians at their own pace and level of ability. Greatchord has a library that accommodates every musical taste, with more than 7500 titles on CD and DVD available for rental or purchase. In addition to providing instruction in classical music, Greatchord also offers special courses for young children between 3-7 years old as well as an adult choir directed by Michael Kühnle—the former director of the American Jazz Orchestra Berlin.
