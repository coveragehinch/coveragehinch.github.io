After that we'd report back with how much money the repairs cost and how much I saved with the warranty. That's not what ended up happening, the truck went in to CarMax for service 3 times, and only one was actually an expensive repair. I'd say we're doing a bit better than break-even right now, but there is still quite a bit of warranty left, we'll have to see if it breaks again.
