Altaley Mining Corporation CEO & President Ralph Shearing tells Proactive that the miner's Campo Morado operations are reporting another excellent quarter with estimated revenue of $17.4 million for the third quarter of 2021.

Shearing says despite a planned shut down for the SAG mill liner change out and planned mill maintenance which interrupted production over several days, 'everything is moving really well' including into its fourth quarter, where he says production is ramping up.
