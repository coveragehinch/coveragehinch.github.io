Polystrate trees!)...I mean ok, I knew Matt had some type of learning disability or mental block when he couldn't even understand Dr. Alex Malpass trying to explain to him that his TAG argument was dead on arrival...but damn, this takes a level of scientific ignorance to a whole new level.
