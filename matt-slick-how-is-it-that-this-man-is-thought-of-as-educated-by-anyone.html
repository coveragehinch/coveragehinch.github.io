He actually said he thinks that the Earth is young, "less than millions of years" and used some of the most debunked YEC claims imaginable (Moon dust accumulation! Pleochroic polonium halos! Polystrate trees!)...I mean ok, I knew Matt had some type of learning disability or mental block when he couldn't even understand Dr. Alex Malpass trying to explain to him that his TAG argument was dead on arrival...but damn, this takes a level of scientific ignorance to a whole new level.
