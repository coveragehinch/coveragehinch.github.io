In this episode Lanterne Rouge interviews Alex Dowsett about his upcoming Hour Record attempt.

Alex, a professional cyclist at Israel Start-Up Nation, previously broke the Hour Record on 2 May 2015 in Manchester, England.

On 3 November 2021 he’ll return to the Velodrome in Aguascalientes, Mexico to attempt to reclaim the title and raise awareness for haemophilia.

You can watch the Hour Record attempt on Alex's YouTube Channel on 3 November 2021 at 22:00 CET: https://www.youtube.com/c/AlexDowsettOfficial
