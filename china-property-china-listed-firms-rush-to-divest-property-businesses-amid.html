China Property | China-Listed Firms Rush to Divest Property Businesses Amid Sector Crackdown.

Tightened lending curbs in the real estate industry early this year have exacerbated financial troubles at some property developers, triggering sector-wide liquidity stress.

Dozens of China-listed companies, including Xiamen ITG Group Corporation Limited, Aoyuan Beauty Valley Technology Company, and Zhangtian Financial Group Company Limited have announced plans to dispose of property businesses so far this year, the state-run Securities Times reported.

Reflecting the real estate sector’s liquidity crunch, data from the China Trust Association showed outstanding investment from trust firms fell below 2 trillion yuan (OR $313.66 billion) at the end of the third quarter, down 26 percent from a year earlier.
