Welcome back to the channel. I am so excited to be rolling out a brand new series all about small spaces! We’re talking furniture pieces, best studio apartment layouts, outdoor terrace and balcony ideas, tiny bathroom upgrades, even Feng Shui tips for your studio space where it’s one giant room. Let’s get rolling with this first video in my SMALL SPACE SERIES- it’s all about furniture pieces that work overtime in small spaces.
