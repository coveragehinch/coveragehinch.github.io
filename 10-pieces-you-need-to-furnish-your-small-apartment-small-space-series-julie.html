Welcome back to the channel. I am so excited to be rolling out a brand new series all about small spaces! We’re talking furniture pieces, best studio apartment layouts, outdoor terrace and balcony ideas, tiny bathroom upgrades, even Feng Shui tips for your studio space where it’s one giant room. Kitchen storage hacks, bedroom styling hacks… I hope to cover all of your major issues when it comes to designing a small space.

Let’s get rolling with this first video in my SMALL SPACE SERIES- it’s all about furniture pieces that work overtime in small spaces. Consider your existing furnishings and ask yourself “How else can this piece be used?” If it serves double or even triple duty- it’s a keeper! Remember that the smaller the home, the more important it is to design with both form and function in mind.
