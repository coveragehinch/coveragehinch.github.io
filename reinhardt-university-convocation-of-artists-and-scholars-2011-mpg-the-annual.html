The Annual Reinhardt University Convocation of Artists & Scholars Showcases Scholastic Achievement in Academics and the Arts

Reinhardt's best student minds will convene from April 9 - 13, 2012, for the University's Fourth Annual Convocation of Artists & Scholars (COAS). Enjoy scenes for COAS '11: music recitals, creative writing readings, a student research conference, a student-teacher portfolio exhibit, student art and science exhibits and a leadership conference. The public is welcome and encouraged to attend this unique scholarly event.
