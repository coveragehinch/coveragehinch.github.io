How many different ways can you think of to use ‘as’? Let Sam teach you four!

How is ‘as’ used in this example?
"He arrived just as we were leaving."

a) To describe somebody’s job or something’s function.
b) To say that something happened at the same time as something else.
c) To give a reason, like ‘because’.
