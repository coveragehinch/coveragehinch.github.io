I met Tim, founder of AirDrop Gaming/HipShotDot, through my friendship with Andrew Foley (Atlas Headrest), and got to demo their upcoming gaming product called Audio Radar. Audio Radar takes surround sound and turns it into surround sight. If you're someone who's deaf or hard of hearing or you're an amateur who struggles with audio cues like me, this could change the way you play FPS games. Let's get Ahnest.
