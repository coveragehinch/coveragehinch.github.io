RECIPE
1.2 kilos Bicarb (42.37oz)
575 grams Citric Acid (20.28oz) (grind your citric acid for a smoother finish)
18 grams SLSA (Optional) (0.6oz)
20 grams Fragrance Oil (0.7oz)
15 grams Witch Hazel (0.7oz) (round molds like less witch hazel for unmoulding, so this is less than I use for flat bottom moulds)
13 grams Carrier Oil (I used Apricot Kernel) (0.45oz)
5ml Polysorbate 80 (optional) (0.17oz)
1 teaspoon Water Based Liquid Dye (0.17oz)
