Greek-Turkish relations.
GREECE. TURKEY. FRANCE.
Turkish President Tayyip Erdogan met today with French President Emanuel Macron on the sidelines of the G20 summit in Rome.

"We also expressed our concern about the sale of weapons in Greece.
