BREAKING NEWS.
Greek-Turkish relations.
GREECE. TURKEY. FRANCE.
Turkish President Tayyip Erdogan met today with French President Emanuel Macron on the sidelines of the G20 summit in Rome.
Although this meeting seems unbelievable, for normal people, since a few months ago the Turkish president stated that Emanuel Macron needed a psychiatrist, however, it took place and even made incredible news concerning Greece, as, after the end of the summit, and during a press conference given by Tayyip Erdogan, he referred to this meeting and what was discussed between the two presidents, saying that:
"We also expressed our concern about the sale of weapons in Greece.
We spoke openly, with a broad agenda."
thus showing for the first time, the fear that Turkey feels, for Greece, for its armaments movements.
