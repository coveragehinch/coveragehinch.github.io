Kate Middleton knows how to dress for any occasion. On Wednesday the 39-year-old royal spearheaded and hosted the "Royal Carols: Together at Christmas" concert at Westminster Abbey. The Christmas carol concert celebrates the people and organizations across the United Kingdom who have helped their community during the Coronavirus pandemic.
