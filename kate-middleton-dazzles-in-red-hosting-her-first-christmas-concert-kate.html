Kate Middleton knows how to dress for any occasion. On Wednesday the 39-year-old royal spearheaded and hosted the "Royal Carols: Together at Christmas" concert at Westminster Abbey. At the event, the Duchess of Cambridge donned a festive red coat with a bow on the front and accessorized the look with a pair of the Queen's earrings. The Christmas carol concert celebrates the people and organizations across the United Kingdom who have helped their community during the Coronavirus pandemic.
