For two months Rainer Zietlow and his co-driver Dominic Brüner tested the capability of the German EV charging infrastructure. After 65 days and 28,198 kilometers we can proudly say, that eMobility has truly arrived as a serious force in Germany. Also, they set a new Guinness World Record for the longest continuous EV journey through a single country.
