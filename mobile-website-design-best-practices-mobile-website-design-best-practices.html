Is there really a need for designing a mobile website?

In previous years, one could get away without a well-designed mobile website. But now, with 57% of the search traffic coming from mobiles and tablets, its no longer optional. Google announced Mobilegeddon in 2015, saying that the rankings of websites are being influenced by mobile-friendly web designs. The mobile-first indexing is thereby fully integrated into the picture. It is utterly important to make the mobile surfing experience better for the users by providing them with better mobile-enabled websites.
