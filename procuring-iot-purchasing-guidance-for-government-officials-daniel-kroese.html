Daniel Kroese, Associate Director, National Risk Management Center Cybersecurity and Infrastructure Security Agency, Department of Homeland Security

During this session, the Department of Homeland Security will provide a briefing on Internet of Things (IoT) purchasing guidance for government officials. This guidance, co-created by government and the tech industry, will assist those officials involved in the purchasing lifecycle in making risk-informed acquisition decisions and drive demand for secure IoT products and services.Learning Objectives:1: Learn about purchasing guidance for the IoT for government officials.2: Learn how the tech industry helps the government make risk-informed decisions.3: Learn how to make risk-informed acquisitions decisions.
