Winter is here and more piglets are coming! Brownie the sow is nearing her due date and I have to get her farrowing pen ready. Farrowing piglets in cold weather can be tricky, and I want to make sure everything is ready. Also, I visit with Red's three week-old piglets and the pigs in the field, and talk about an alternative feeding approach I've started with our cull heifers.
