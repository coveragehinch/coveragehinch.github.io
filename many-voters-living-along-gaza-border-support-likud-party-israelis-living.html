Israelis living along the Gaza border have witnessed tens of thousands of rockets and mortars launched from both sides of the strip for almost two decades, but many residents here have repeatedly voted for Benjamin Netanyahu's Likud Party. As Israel gets ready for general elections on Tuesday, CGTN's Stephanie Freid speaks with some voters about the current government and why they support it.
