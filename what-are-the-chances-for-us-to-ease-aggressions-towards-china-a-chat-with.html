Reaffirming the One-China policy, promising cooperation and preventing the competition between the two countries from escalating into conflicts, these were what have been discussed during the virtual meeting between Chinese President Xi Jinping and US President Joe Biden.
But does it mean the tensions will really be eased? Will US cut down its aggressions towards China?
In this episode, I talk to Carlos Martinez, the co-founder of No Cold War and Friends of Socialist China to discuss whether the US government will changes its behaviors after the meeting.
