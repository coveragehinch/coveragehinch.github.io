Vince Carter is known worldwide as quite possibly the greatest dunker who ever played in the NBA. That honor is a double-edged sword however, as it has overshadowed his complete basketball game and supreme skillset as a player. It's unfortunate that many NBA fans don't know this about him. You often see the quote "Vince was just a dunker" on social media.
