Vince Carter is known worldwide as quite possibly the greatest dunker who ever played in the NBA. That honor is a double-edged sword however, as it has overshadowed his complete basketball game and supreme skillset as a player. Vince worked diligently to improve his ball handling, his post moves, his passing, and his perimeter game. And when he was in his prime, he was truly one of the best all around players. It's unfortunate that many NBA fans don't know this about him. You often see the quote "Vince was just a dunker" on social media. I made this video in the hopes it will open eyes and teach people how good he was in virtually all aspects of the game.
