Let's face it, who doesn't want a luxurious bedroom sanctuary? This short film from Fine Living channel shows you how to create a sumptuous bedroom that you'll look forward to at the end of each day. Full of gorgeous inspirational colours and lush textures, it's a step-by-step guide to achieving quick and simple bedroom opulence.

We had a lot of fun working with leading luxury lifestyle channel Fine Living. Famous Rebel (Formerly OCP) were given the task of providing a series of bespoke short-form content to refresh the filler material on its channel.

We used this opportunity to explore a variety of different video formats and styles: producing mini-documentaries to celebrate skills and craftsmanship, stop-motion for decorating ideas for your home, and tutorial videos for tips on healthy-living.
