Let's face it, who doesn't want a luxurious bedroom sanctuary? This short film from Fine Living channel shows you how to create a sumptuous bedroom that you'll look forward to at the end of each day. We had a lot of fun working with leading luxury lifestyle channel Fine Living. Famous Rebel (Formerly OCP) were given the task of providing a series of bespoke short-form content to refresh the filler material on its channel.
