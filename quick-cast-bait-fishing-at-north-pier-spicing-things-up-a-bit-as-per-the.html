Arriving on a low tide was a concern but none the less he fished on.
The plan was to get a bait out whilst drop-shotting alongside.

Rigging up his NEW Okuma Precision 14.6ft rod for a cast intended for an edible,

his fourth cast was a Gernie lol....
Undergunned and with only 3 baits he called it in speeding off to work ....
