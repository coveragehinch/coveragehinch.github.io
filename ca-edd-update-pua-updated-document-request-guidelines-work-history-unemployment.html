However, some PUA claimants who are submitting there 2020 tax returns are seeing rejections because they cannot establish work and income prior to the start of the PUA Claim. For those who fall under that scenario the EDD is asking for additional items to substantiate the claim such as receipts and invoices along with bank statements and so forth.
