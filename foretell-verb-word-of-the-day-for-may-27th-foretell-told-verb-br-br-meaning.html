Foretell (told)- verb

Meaning- to say what is going to happen in the future:

Examples:- 1. He was a 16th-century prophet who foretold how the world would end.
2. Who can venture to foretell how their strange career will end?
3. Willow trees breaking out into buds foretell the coming of spring.
4. You can't foretell how the war will end.
