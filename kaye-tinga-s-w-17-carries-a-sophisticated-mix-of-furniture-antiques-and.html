A decade ago, Kaye Tinga and her partner Andres Vázquez-Prada transformed a simple warehouse into W/17. Since then, they have kept to their principle of bringing tasteful decor and unique artisanal offerings to their clients. In here you will find a veritable treasure trove of bespoke furniture, gorgeous antiques, and unique objects of art, all handpicked for their discriminating clientele. Further testament that when it’s in good style, it will never die.
