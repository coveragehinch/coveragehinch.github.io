Keira lost her senses after suffering a battle wound
while fending off the State Army of Representatives
that sought to citizen arrest the immigrants.
Her left arm was bleeding.

She fastened a tourniquet after her own shirt,
tightened it and ignored the blood by shutting her eyes.

She has no recollection of the events
that led to her waking up in another galaxy.

She knows that she bled for a while, a woman came to her,
screamed help, picked her up.
Her name was Bertha. That, she remembers.

From then on, it's only music.

Keira is fine.
The immigrants are fine.

Someone saved them.

They are now safe but far away from home.

It was during the pandemic that this project took shape.
After the release of Echoes, the improvisational nature
of the tracks gave way to a more concrete songwriting but never abandoning its loose character.

This album is "name your price" as a humble thank you to all your support.

I hope you like it.
