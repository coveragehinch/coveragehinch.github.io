Cyber Monday, the sales event that grew out of the nation’s obsession with Black Friday, has moved past its predecessor in sales. The deals are leading to what experts anticipate will be the biggest online shopping deal of all time. Inside Edition’s Les Trent went to an Amazon fulfillment center in New Jersey to see packages moving out at breakneck speed. One big reason people are shopping from home this year is the COVID-19 pandemic, especially with last week’s emergence of the Omicron variant.
