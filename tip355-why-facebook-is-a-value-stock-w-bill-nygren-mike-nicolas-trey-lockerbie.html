Trey Lockerbie welcomes back Bill Nygren and Mike Nicolas from Oakmark Funds, which currently manages over $60B in assets. Since then, BOA’s stock price has risen over 100%, so Trey takes the opportunity to discuss how they value the stock today. But, the main point of today's discussion is to take a deep dive into a potentially surprising value pick: Facebook.
