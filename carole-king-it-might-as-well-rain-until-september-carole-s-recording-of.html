Carole's recording of "It Might As Well Rain Until September" has been included on many compilation albums since the 60's and most recently on Britain's Greatest Hits 1962, which was released by Acrobat label in June 2013. This single was Carole's highest peaking song in the United Kingdom reaching #3 on the UK Singles Chart in October 1962. It charted at #22 on Billboard chart in September of that year, appearing on the Hot 100 chart dated October 6, 1962.
