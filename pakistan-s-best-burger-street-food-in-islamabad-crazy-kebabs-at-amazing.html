For our last day in Islamabad, we are showing you some of the BEST non-traditional cuisine Pakistan has to offer. Islamabad is an international city, where people from around the world live and work. That being said, your options for dining reflect modern, global cuisine.

We had breakfast at Burning Brownie, one of the few places in Pakistan to find amazing brewed coffee and western sweets. For drinks we ordered latte, flat white and a macchiato. They were perfect! We ordered Afghani Kebabs (minced meat), Afghani Tikka (meat chunks), Kabuli Pulao (steamed rice with carrots, raisins and meat) and Mutton Karahi. This restaurant was MEAT HEAVEN!!!
