QUESTIONS TO FIND YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE:
1. Who do you intend to buy your products?
2. What lifestyle does your target audience lead? (Interests/hobbies)
3. How does your target audience engage socially online?
4. What drives your target audience to make buying decisions?
5. What problems are you attempting to solve with your business?
6. What reason would your target audience buy your products?
7. How can you best reach your target audience?
8. How does your target audience engage with other brands?
