Follow these ideas to fill your day and make your retirement happy, we share 7 things that happy retirees do well. Join us for a cup of tea and some Scottish shortbread biscuit cookies and we will go through some ideas that make us happy as a retired couple.

If you can plan and be prepared for retirement and be positive, see what you have gained by retiring and not what you have lost leaving your job. The extra time you have now can be so rewarding, it's your time to spend time on yourself and your partner, keep well and fit. We have ideas for you, maybe you could consider fitting into your retirement life.

Whatever else we want you to be happy and these 7 things that retirees do well is a good starting point for you to explore. We want your retirement to be fulfilling, do not fear retirement.
