Welcome back to the Market Chat. Puru Saxena is a growth stock investor and former Money Manager based in Hong Kong who managed over 400 million in assets. In this interview we talk about his background and go in depth into his growth stock investing process from fundamental metrics he focuses on all the way to determining capital allocation and finally when to sell a position.

We also cover his favorite growth stock investing books including the following:
