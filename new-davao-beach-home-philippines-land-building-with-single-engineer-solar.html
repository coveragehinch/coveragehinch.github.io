WE ARE BUILDING A NEW BEACH HOME HERE IN CATEEL DAVAO ORIENTAL...

Single engineer and married engineer have been really amazing in helping our projects come together here along the coast. On this Philippines land building day, we get up to date with the new beach home. It may look a little cluttered right now, but everything will clear up... especially when we move the solar onto the roof! Yup, the solar panels will be heading up on top of the new beach house. And then we have lots of really nice plans for the land.

Building a life in Davao has been a wonderful experience. And so it continues... One day at a time. Oh, and one moment of advice from the amazing Mama Rose!! So wonderful chatting with her. The best neighbor!
