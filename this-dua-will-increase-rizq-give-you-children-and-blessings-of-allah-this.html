“Allahumma ak-tsir maalii wa waladii, wa baarik lii fiimaa a’thoitanii wa athil hayaatii ‘ala tho’atik wa ahsin ‘amalii wagh-fir lii

O Allah increase my wealth and my children, and bless the gift that you give me . Extend my life in obedience to You, and purge my deeds and forgive my sins. " Ameen
