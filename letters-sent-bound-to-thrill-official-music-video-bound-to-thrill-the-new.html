LYRICS

start to dance - all of a sudden
what’s the chance? - low like forgotten.
nothing at all was on my mind, then a short lapse in time
and you caught my sight

dense clouds wrap my head close: blurring reality
ignore, forfeit, losing its shape
glaring light impales my sight,
all contours they vanish in bright flames burning cold

the moon light still blazing
between scattered clouds as ink
draws our world: it merely consists of shadows.
a new hope is rising
and in the moment of a blink
you appeared, the haze rushed down to the ground

(in this weird game called life)
you've sent wavering shivers down my spine
all the fears decline – leave all shadows behind
top of the mountain clear's the air as is the sight
your eyes, my light – on this dark trail of life

I've been so blind all of my whole life wondering:
why is my sight so blurred? - And why my heart so hurt?
chasing the neon lights, constantly lost control
why am I bound to thrill? - and why's my heart so still?

it was just a wink
but the flames are burning stronger
you're eyes are erasing:
my demons, will never bring
my sight down again

shadows in my sight, blocking the final light
hours are passing by, bright neon light remains

constantly flickering
it’s so constantly triggering
I feel those cramps inside - am I afraid of light?
on and off pitching the switches
outlines now come off as glitches
flaring light cramps me inside I need a place to hide my eyes
in this weird game called life

praise natural sunlight
give me clear sight
on this dark trail of life
