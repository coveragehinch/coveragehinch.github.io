In this video we explain how to avoid the 1.8% debit/credit card fee that Binance (currently) charges for non-SEPA deposits. We show you how to avoid these fees by sending USD to Binance directly using Revolut. At time of recording, Euro deposits to Binance using SEPA have been suspended but will hopefully return soon 🤞 Subscribe to stay updated!

⚠️ Please note: your first transfer using this method MAY take up to 3-5 business days (excluding weekends). Some receive it quickly, others need to wait - it's random! We recommend sending a $50 test order first to make sure everything works ok.
