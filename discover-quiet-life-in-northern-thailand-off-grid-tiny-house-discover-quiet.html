Discover Quiet Life in Northern Thailand and tour this amazing off grid, tiny house lifestyle. Self sustainable living for $10/year!

This small house was built by a couple who is enjoying a simple, quiet life. And you won't believe the cost. Watch the video and learn all about how much it costs (or how little it costs) to live this sort of life. Prepare to be amazed!
