In Italy, on the island of Sicily, the eruption of Mount Etna began. The plume of smoke reached a height of 10 kilometers, according to experts from the National Institute of Geophysics and Volcanology of Catania.
Local residents talked about the fallout of ash in nearby settlements. In some villages, ashes covered roads and rooftops in a thick layer.
The volcanic eruption did not affect the operation of the Catania Fontanarossa airport. At the moment, there is no data on the victims and material losses due to the eruption that happened.
