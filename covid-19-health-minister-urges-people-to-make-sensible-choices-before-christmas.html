People should 'make a sensible choice' if their priority is to spend Christmas with their families, a Health Minister has said.

Gillian Keegan told Sky's Kay Burley that individuals should 'take a different approach' if they want to 'make it through to Christmas Day'.

Her comments come after England's Chief Medical Officer Chris Whitty expressed caution about festive plans.
