Ziiana - the Crown Princess of #BGCMelody - officially makes her introduction to the world with this amazing blend of #Amapiano and the champion sounds of #Cameroon. Giving us a sneak preview of this new genre known as #Kamerpiano developed by the PanAfrican record label's ace production team.

Ziiana has dedicated this song to contemporary Cameroonian champs Joel Embiid (NBA, Philadelphia 76ers) and Francis Ngannou (UFC), who have both risen to the top by patiently respecting "The Process"; and in honor of her favorite African and African American historical icons: Queen Nefertiti, Nelson Mandela, Myriam Makeba, Kwame Nkrumah, Rosa Parks and Martin Luther King, Jr.
