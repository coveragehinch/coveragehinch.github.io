Xpediator PLC's (LON:XPD) Robert Ross speaks to Proactive London's Katie Pilbeam about their increasing its financial guidance for the current year as strong trading continues for the freight management specialist.

Demand for the Group's services across all three divisions has been strong, with those parts of the Transport and Logistics & Warehousing divisions that were impacted by the Covid-19 pandemic, now all trading positively.

Ross says they have had a good first half which has positioned them well for a strong outcome for the full year.
