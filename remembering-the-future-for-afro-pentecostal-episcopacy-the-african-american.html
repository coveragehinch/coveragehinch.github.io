The African American Pentecostal Church has been predominantly led by monarchial episcopal structures. Some assert that these structures are passe and non-functional for the future of the church. Tonight, on Global Pentecostal Perspectives, Dr. Johnathan Alvarado will discuss the future of Afro-Pentecostal Episcopacy with a Millenial, fourth-generation, Afro-Pentecostal in hopes to ascertain what a future for Afro-Pentecostal Episcopacy might look like.
