Russian troops forced the Turkish army in northern Syria to immediately retreat

The full-scale offensive of Turkish troops in northern Syria was stopped by the Russian military.

The Turkish army was forced to temporarily suspend its offensive operation in northern Syria after the Russian military was stationed at the positions of Kurdish formations in the region. We are talking about raising Russian flags, as previously reported by the Avia information and news agency.missile defense has already been reported, as well as the deployment of the Russian military contingent in the region. Information on this subject is provided by the publication "Halab Today".

"Russian forces raised their flags instead of the banners of the Syrian Democratic Forces, on Sunday, in several places on the outskirts of Ain Issa north of Raqqa governorate, which is a step to stop the expected Turkish military movements to the east. It is reported that Russian troops raised their flags in the villages of Al-Kantari and Al-Tarwaziya in the northeast of Raqqa governorate, as well as in the areas of Camp and Al-Hoshan to the west of the city of Ain Issa. Agreements were concluded with local factions with Russia regarding raising the flags of Russia and Syria instead of the flags of the SDF group in order to stop the Turkish movement east of the Euphrates. Russian troops raised six Russian flags in the aforementioned areas over the headquarters of the Syrian Democratic Forces," the published material of the Halab Today edition says.
