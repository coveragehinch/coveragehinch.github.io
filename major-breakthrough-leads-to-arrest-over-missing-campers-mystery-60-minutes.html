For 20 months, what happened to the couple was a mystery - but three weeks ago there was a breakthrough. On 60 MINUTES, detectives released details of a four wheel drive they were looking for. Since then more significant developments: Detectives have now seized the vehicle and on Thursday charged a 55-year-old airline pilot with murder.
