• Don't throw ice cold water on an overheated dog (obviously)! When Mabel gets hot from running around, we just put cool water on her belly, chest, and paws.

• The eyedrops and eardrops were both recommended by our veterinarian. They're a preventative measure. Mabel has had an ear infection and an eye infection, but since we started using these products, she hasn't had infections. It's up to you and your vet whether you want to use these products. Mabel now gets eye drops WEEKLY, not daily.
