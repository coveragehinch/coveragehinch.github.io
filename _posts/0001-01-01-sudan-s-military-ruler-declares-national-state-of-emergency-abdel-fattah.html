--- title: "Sudan’s military ruler declares national state of emergency" image: "https:\/\/i.ytimg.com\/vi\/NKM2N4fJEgw\/hqdefault.jpg" vid_id: "NKM2N4fJEgw" categories: "News-Politics" tags: ["Abdel Fattah al-Burhan","Al Jazeera","Breaking news"] date: "2021-10-26T06:33:09+03:00" vid_date: "2021-10-25T14:44:05Z" duration: "PT8M58S" viewcount: "34920" likeCount: "453" dislikeCount: "29" channel: "Al Jazeera English" --- {% raw %}Abdel Fattah al-Burhan, the military officer who heads a power-sharing ruling council, announced a state of emergency across the country and the dissolution of the transitional sovereign council and the government.

He said the 2019 agreement on the transitional government which the civilian and military leadership had shared had turned into a struggle that was threatening peace and security.

The military needed to protect the country’s safety and security as stated in the constitutional declaration, he said, announcing the dissolution of the power-sharing ruling council and the government.

He also announced the removal of state governors, saying the elections will be held in July 2023.
