Today’s video will be the top 10 completed fantasy trilogies that I’ve read so far. This video is ranked based on the 45 completed fantasy trilogies I finished reading so far. After receiving a lot of requests for this video, and now that we’re near the end of 2021, I think it’s the right time to post this recommendation video. There are still so many fantasy trilogies I want to read, and I will make sure to update this list again once I’ve read about 10 or 20 more finished fantasy trilogies.
