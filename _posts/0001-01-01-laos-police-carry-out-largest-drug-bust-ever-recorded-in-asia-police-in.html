Authorities intercepted a truck carrying beer crates in Bokeo, leading them to a nearby house where two men were detained.

Laos has been a gateway for drug traffickers who are known to transport meth shipments into Thailand and Myanmar as part of "the golden triangle region".{% endraw %}
