H." image: "https:\/\/i.ytimg.com\/vi\/LyVyFGLX1TY\/hqdefault.jpg" vid_id: "LyVyFGLX1TY" categories: "Education" tags: ["Hetzer's","Useless","Uncle?"] date: "2021-11-03T02:08:10+03:00" vid_date: "2021-11-02T20:00:03Z" duration: "PT16M7S" viewcount: "9427" likeCount: "1377" dislikeCount: "7" channel: "Military History Visualized" --- {% raw %}A look at the Marder III Ausführung H or Marder 38T, this was a German tank destroyer (Panzerjäger - literally "Tank Hunter") in World War 2. It was a stop-gap solution, yet it shares a lot of components and other aspects with the popular Jagdpanzer 38 "Hetzer". In this video we look at experience reports, development, the Marder series, firepower, mobility and armor protection.
