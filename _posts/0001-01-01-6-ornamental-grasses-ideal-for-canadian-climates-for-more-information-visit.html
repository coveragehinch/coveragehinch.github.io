Mike Byland discusses 6 great grasses perfect for our Canadian climate. Karl Foerster, Avalanche and Overdam Feather Reed grasses and Elijah Blue Fescue and Blue Oat grasses all drought tolerant and hardy. Then the Japanese Blood grass which is ideal for zone 5 and features beautiful red tipped blades.
