In this video, children will discover the disadvantages of non-renewable energy sources as contrasted with renewable energy sources: non-renewable energies emit contaminating gases and sometimes create radioactive residues or even natural disasters. To contribute in looking after the environment, it is important to reduce the use of non-renewable energy resources.
