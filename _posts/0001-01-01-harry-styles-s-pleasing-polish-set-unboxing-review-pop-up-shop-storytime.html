With 20 minutes notice, I ran to get ready to get over to Studio City. At the truck they were only selling the Perfect Polish Set, so that's what I wound up getting. I'm a big nail polish fan, so I was curious to try these out. It's definitely the most high end nail polish I've ever used in my life.
But is it worth that price tag? Since it's still before the general sale, I figured I would show you what these colors look like IRL best I can and give a review of the polish itself with this unboxing and review.
