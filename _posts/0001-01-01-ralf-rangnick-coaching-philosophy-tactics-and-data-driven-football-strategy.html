Ralf Rangnick was the keynote speaker at the inaugural Coaching Conference, staged by The Coaches’ Voice in London in September 2021.

In this excerpt from a wide-ranging discussion with host David Jones, the highly respected coach and sporting director explains his playing style and how data helped shape his philosophy.
