'Love Inside' is the record that catapulted Nottingham-born Philip George from enthusiastic bedroom DJ to international touring artist within a matter of weeks. Released on 3 Beat when he was just twenty-two, this track exploded onto the scene in 2015, landing the youngster UK festival slots at the likes of EDC, Creamfields, Bestival, We R Fstvl and more.
