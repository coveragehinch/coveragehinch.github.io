HARI OM TAT SAT

We all have desires that we live for, struggle for and work for. But there are certain desires which seem very far and difficult to achieve, especially the ones that give true joy.

Hari is Joy. Hari is also the biggest healer of all. Also known as Vishnu, Krishna and Ram, Hari brings Intention, Attraction and Abundance to turn desires into achievable goals.
.
'Tat Sat' is the ultimate truth one has to live by to reach the goals. The ultimate truth is Karma. Whatever we do, stays in form of Karma.

When chanted to or listened to, this mantra attracts positivity around you and strength inside you. it generates hope and faith in yourself. Your confidence levels go up sky-rocketing. OM
