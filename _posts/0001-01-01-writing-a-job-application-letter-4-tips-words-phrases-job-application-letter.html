The 4 things you should include in a JOB APPLICATION LETTER!
#1 – A powerful OPENING STATEMENT that grabs the attention of the hiring manager.
#2 – The skills and qualities you have that MATCH the job description.
#3 – Why you want to work for their company.
#4 – How you will ADD VALUE to their organization.

JOB APPLICATION LETTER TIPS
- Keep it to just one side of A4.
- Include your email and telephone number within the letter.
- Carry out a spell check for errors!
- Send the letter via email and post.
