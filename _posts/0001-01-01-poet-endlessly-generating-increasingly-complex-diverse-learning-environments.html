--- title: "POET: Endlessly Generating Increasingly Complex & Diverse Learning Environments and their Solutions" image: "https:\/\/i.ytimg.com\/vi\/D1WWhQY9N4g\/hqdefault.jpg" vid_id: "D1WWhQY9N4g" categories: "Science-Technology" tags: ["POET:","Endlessly","Generating"] date: "2021-12-19T08:40:59+03:00" vid_date: "2019-01-08T17:31:21Z" duration: "PT2M13S" viewcount: "13705" likeCount: "" dislikeCount: "" channel: "Uber AI Labs" --- {% raw %}This video introduces an algorithm called POET (Paired Open-Ended Trailblazer) that is designed to continually invent increasingly complex and diverse problems, along with their corresponding solutions. Here, we demonstrate POET's potential by unleashing it on a problem space of two-dimensional landscapes and a solution space of robotic behaviors that aim to traverse them. In this domain, POET builds out a diverse collection of solved landscapes through experiments that modify previously-solved landscapes and apply and refine previously-discovered successful robotic gaits. The process culminates in robots able to skillfully traverse rugged obstacle-filled landscapes that are not solvable when approached in isolation with standard optimization techniques. The conclusion is that POET represents a step towards truly open-ended machine learning algorithms that continually invent and solve new challenges.

For more information, see our corresponding blog post (https://eng.uber.com/poet-open-ended-deep-learning) and research paper (https://arxiv.org/abs/1901.01753).
