Expert chef instructor King Phojanakong from the Institute of Culinary Education and home cook Onika are trading pad thai recipes and hitting the kitchen. We set Onika up with $209 worth of ingredients and King’s recipe while sending a modest $18 worth of goods back the other way. To assist Onika with King’s massive ingredient list, food scientist Rose dialed in to lend some expert advice. Which pad thai are you itching to try?
