Expert chef instructor King Phojanakong from the Institute of Culinary Education and home cook Onika are trading pad thai recipes and hitting the kitchen. To assist Onika with King’s massive ingredient list, food scientist Rose dialed in to lend some expert advice. Which pad thai are you itching to try?
