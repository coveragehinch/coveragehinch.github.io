Today's video was researched using a different resource than normal: a 1997 edition of the Merck Manual of Medical Information. I followed the procedure outlined for a neurological assessment, and as such, the way this is conducted is not realistic for today's exams - I'm going off of 24 year old info, haha!

This video includes a mental status exam, cranial nerve exam, motor & sensory nerve exam, an autonomic nervous system test & checks on blood flow to your brain at the end :) it's jam-packed with different tests to help you chill out & sleep :)
