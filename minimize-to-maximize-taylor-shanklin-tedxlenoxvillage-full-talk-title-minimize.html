Full talk title: Minimize to Maximize: The Art of Focusing on Less to Maximize Results

This discussion will introduce a framework that will allow you to show up in your life and your work happier and more authentically. Research shows that the American workforce is stressed, and many of us find ourselves chasing an ideal of success that often does not line up with our own personal gifts or passions. By minimizing distractions in your life and letting go of things that do not truly give you fulfillment, you can maximize opportunities for personal and professional growth. Focus on what matters, lift off. Taylor is a speaker, podcast host, and marketing innovator in the nonprofit sector. In her nonprofit career, she has helped hundreds of organizations build and grow online giving programs. She consults non-profit organizations in her work with Firefly Partners, where she leads the Growth team. Taylor is also the founder and CEO of Barlele, a brand and growth strategy firm that helps businesses grow through great storytelling and strategic branding.

Taylor is the founder, producer, and host of the Soar podcast - a podcast dedicated to helping people live and work happier by tapping into focus and passion to achieve growth. Taylor lives in the mountains of western North Carolina with her husband, two kids, and a lot of nature. She enjoys skiing, hiking, and taking in as much fresh air as possible.
