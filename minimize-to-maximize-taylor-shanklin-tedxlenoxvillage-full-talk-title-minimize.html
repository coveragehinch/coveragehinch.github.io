Full talk title: Minimize to Maximize: The Art of Focusing on Less to Maximize Results

This discussion will introduce a framework that will allow you to show up in your life and your work happier and more authentically. Research shows that the American workforce is stressed, and many of us find ourselves chasing an ideal of success that often does not line up with our own personal gifts or passions. By minimizing distractions in your life and letting go of things that do not truly give you fulfillment, you can maximize opportunities for personal and professional growth.
