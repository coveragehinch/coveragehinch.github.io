++If you have a headache that persists for more than four days despite simple pain relief or is severe then you should contact the department that gave you the vaccine or your local medical practice or emergency department if very severe++

All information shared in this video is taken from the UK AstraZeneca COVID19 vaccination leaflet https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/regulatory-approval-of-covid-19-vaccine-astrazeneca/information-for-healthcare-professionals-on-covid-19-vaccine-astrazeneca

Please discuss with your local vaccination team or healthcare team if the vaccine is suitable for you.

00:00 AstraZeneca vaccine side effects

00:20 Very common side effects:
Occur in more than 1 in 10 people who have the vaccine.
✳️ Nausea
✳️ Headache
✳️ Chills
✳️ Joint pains
✳️ Tenderness, pain, warmth, bruising, swelling, erythema, and/or itching at the site of injection
✳️ Fever
✳️ Malaise
✳️ Chills
✳️ Fatigue

00:49 Common side effects:
Occur in 1 in 10 people who have the vaccine.
✳️ Vomiting
✳️ Induration at the site of injection
✳️ Flu-like illness

01:07 Uncommon side effects
Occur in 1 in 100 people who have the vaccine
✳️ Enlarged Lymph nodes
✳️ Less appetite
✳️ Dizziness
✳️ Tummy pain
✳️ Sweating
✳️ Itching
✳️ Rash

00:54 What do I do if I get other side effects?

▶️ disodium hydrogen phosphate dihydrate

▶️ sucrose

▶️ water for injections
