From their desire to make others feel comfortable to get too immersed in their own experiences, INFJs can’t help but radiate their intricacies into their external environment. While it annoys most people, it amazes some. As an INFJ, how can oversharing be a major disadvantage to you? Is it more beneficial or detrimental to your inner peace?
