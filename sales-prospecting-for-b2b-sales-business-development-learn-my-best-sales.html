Sales prospecting can be executing using cold email, LinkedIn cold messages, or even cold calling.

But as simple as sales prospecting may sound on the surface, it can be quite challenging to get an appointment with your dream clients.

How do you know who these people are?

What's the best way to get in contact with them?

And more importantly, why would they ever agree to take a meeting with you?

As you can see, lead generation, no matter if you're using cold email, LinkedIn, or cold calling, can get a bit confusing.
