Be careful with this one, and GO SLOW!

This is not intended to be a strengthening exercise, rather, it is intended to help a "stuck" condyle begin to move fully through the complex translational movement out of the Glenoid Fossa.
This exercise isolates the Lateral Pterygoid Muscles.
