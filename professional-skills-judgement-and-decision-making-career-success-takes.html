Career success takes more than technical and specialized know-how. It takes professional skills like active learning, time management, collaboration and more.

When you demonstrate a clear thought process to evaluate risks, analyze solutions and use information to find an outcome, you are engaging in judgement and decision making. Judgement and decision making can help you reach solutions and conclusions to complex problems with efficiency.

Find more about Professional Skills and how they can help you boost your career in electricity at electricityhr.ca/resources/introduction-to-professional-skills
