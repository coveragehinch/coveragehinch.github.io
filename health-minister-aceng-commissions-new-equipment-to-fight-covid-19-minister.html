Minister for Health Dr. Jane Ruth Aceng has commissioned an assortment of equipment procured by the global fund to support the COVID-19 resurgence plan. This is part of the 178.8 million US dollars, approximately 637 Billion shillings, that will be received over three years. Dr. Aceng said the ministry will ensure the equipment is efficiently utilized. #NTVNews #NTVTonight #NTVWeekendEdition
