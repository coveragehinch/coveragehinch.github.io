Queen Elizabeth's only daughter, Princess Anne, is not in the public eye as much as other members of the royal family these days, but at one time, she was quite the prominent figure. She's a fierce advocate for charity, has more official engagements than just about anyone, and even was an Olympic athlete! She holds several military titles, too.

She was also quite the lightning rod for controversy in her day. She was the victim of a kidnapping attempt and went through a divorce around the same time as her brother, Prince Charles. She even has a criminal record! Here are some of the strange things you may not know about Princess Anne.
