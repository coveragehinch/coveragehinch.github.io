Most audiences around the world are enjoying Spider-man No Way Home this weekend, but Sony pulled the rug under a number of countries, such as Norway – Andre’s homeland.
Other affected territories include Japan, The Philippines and Slovakia, and reportedly, Poland and Romania. If you’re not in those countries, this video will only be of limited interest to you – but if you are, or you just want to hear a tale about how badly distribution can botch an event like this, keep watching this video till the end.
