Will AI culminate in degraded society or bring a lot of advantages for people? Farid Pardashunas is a traveller, Tech Blogger, Entrepreneur, and ChatBot guru. He graduated from Baku State University with a major in International Relations and World Economy. Farid is being a co-founder of HotBots and Technote, and, at the same time, CEO of the latter. He has a five-year experience in Tech and Entrepreneurship Blogging.
