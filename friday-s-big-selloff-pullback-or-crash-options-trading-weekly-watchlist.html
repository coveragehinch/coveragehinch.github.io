During the Weekly Watchlist we review the best stocks for options trading. We go over key levels of support and resistance and create an actionable game plan for the week of November 29th 2021.

In the SPY we saw a pretty aggressive sell side day on Friday. That being said, we need more information on wether or not the selling will continue. With the half day and significant amount of volume being transacted, it would appear as if there is more room for lower into the prior highs and then a re-evaluation. Not only would it be the 50 SMA, it would be the 50% fib re-tracement. Watch the financial sector for a heads up on higher or lower early on in the week.

In the QQQ and Nasdaq we see less impact from the news on the variant. Obviously tech is more resilient to economic shut down and doesn't have the financials sector weighing down on the upward progress. Its all about the head and shoulders and the neck line at 390 which is also the 38.2 fib.

IWM and the small cap stocks have now officially seen the look above and fail back down firmly into the range. The small caps have the most to lose in terms of shut downs and travel restriction. Being back in the middle of the range they are signaling neutral risk....again...
