With the half day and significant amount of volume being transacted, it would appear as if there is more room for lower into the prior highs and then a re-evaluation. Not only would it be the 50 SMA, it would be the 50% fib re-tracement. Watch the financial sector for a heads up on higher or lower early on in the week.
