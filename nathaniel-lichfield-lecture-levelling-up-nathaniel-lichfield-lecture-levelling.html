Professor Philip Mc Cann examines different aspects of the Levelling Up agenda, both conceptually and also operationally. It discusses the role that land markets, land use planning and also the links with financial markets play in shaping economic development in general, and also in the specific context of addressing the UK‚Äôs interregional inequalities and climate change mitigation strategies.
