In this video, you are going to learn "What are the types of organizational structure in management?"
Business of all categories, shapes and sizes use organizational structures for their growth. There is a specific hierarchy within an organization. An eminent organizational structure defines each employee's job and how it fits within the entire system.
What is organizational structure video -
The different types of organizational structure are- 1. Bureaucratic Structures; its includes- Pre-bureaucratic structures, Bureaucratic Structures, and Post-bureaucratic structures.
2. Hierarchical Organizational Structure.
3. Functional Organizational Structure.
4. Divisional Organizational Structure, this includes: Geographic Divisional Organizational Structure, Market-Based Divisional Organizational Structure, Product-Based Divisional Organizational Structure.
5. Matrix Structure.
6. Flat, or Horizontal Organizational Structure.
7. Circular Organizational Structure.
8. Team-based Organizational Structure.
9. Network-Based Organizational Structure.
