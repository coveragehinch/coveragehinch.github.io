Now, it's perfectly reasonable that they can't be super friendly all the time. After all, stars are humans, just like us. Everyone in their shoes would likely come off as a bit flustered at times – especially considering all of the stress-inducing pressure and attention that they have to contend with on a daily basis. But there are quite a few stars that are known for treating their fans with the care and respect that they deserve.

Dolly Parton, Taylor Swift, and Keanu Reeves have all been said to go out of their way to be cordial with their fans – but this video isn't about any of those stars.
