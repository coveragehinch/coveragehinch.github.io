I wasn´t expecting to see this, when I went for a walk through Goch, a town we know existed in 1259. It´s right on the border to Holland. The artist, Vincent van Goch, probably had relatives form this town. There are so many more beautiful historical buildings in Goch, but I only had time to film these. I´ll just have to come back. The people were so very friendly here, and I´ve only scratched the surface of what´s to be discovered in Goch.
