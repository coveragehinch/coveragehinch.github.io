There's a new supercar dealer in Dubai, F1rst Motors, and the inventory is out of this world! Let's check out the showroom filled with the latest and greatest, from the hypercars to brand new releases.

It's always fun to go window shopping in Dubai and see some of the world's greatest cars on sale. I met the team behind F1rst Motors back on my last visit in January and they told me the plans, and the result certainly does not disappoint! With the display featuring a McLaren Speedtail and P1 GTR, an SLR Stirling Moss and a Bugatti Chiron, it's just the start.

As the name suggest, First Motors like to be the first, and have the most amazing selection of Ferrari SF90 Stradale, AMG GT Black Series and Porsche 992 GT3 models to choose from, as well as cars like the Maserati MC20 that I'd never even previously seen.

Congratulations to the guys on bringing their dream to reality and thanks for letting me check out the stock!
