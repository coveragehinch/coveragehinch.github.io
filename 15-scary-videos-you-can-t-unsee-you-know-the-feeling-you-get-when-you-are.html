While that is usually a good way to go about it, don't look at this scary comp as an opportunity to face your fears — because these creepy videos may scare you so badly, your plan may backfire, especially if you get frightened easily.

Know what’s coming your way before watching these creepy videos. No amount of scary stories you may have read will make for enough preparation to go through these scary videos unscathed. My advice?
