Here are our top 10 picks.

CHAPTERS:
0:00 Intro
0:28 1. CIUTADELLA PARK
This historical garden is considered the greenest oasis in Barcelona. See also Arc de Triomf which is very close to the park.
1:02 2. LA BARCELONETA
Nowadays famous for its sandy beach and numerous restaurants and nightclubs along the boardwalk.
1:31 3. PARK GÜELL
UNESCO declared the park a World Heritage Site under "Works of Antoni Gaudí".
2:21 4. SAGRADA FAMÍLIA
A large unfinished Roman Catholic church by Antoni Gaudí.
3:10 5. GOTHIC QUARTER
The center of the old city of Barcelona.
3:46 6. LA RAMBLA
A 1,2 km long crowded street in central Barcelona.
4:23 7. LA BOQUERIA MARKET
A large public market in the Old City district of Barcelona, firstly mentioned in 1217.
4:58 8. CATALONIA SQUARE
A large square in central Barcelona where some of the city's most important streets and avenues meet.
5:37 9. THE MAGIC FOUNTAIN
Constructed for the 1929 Barcelona International Exposition.
6:18 10. MUSEUMS AND EXHIBITIONS
There are numerous remarkable museums and exhibitions, such as Palau Nacional, macba, CosmoCaixa - Science Museum, Design Museum of Barcelona, Museu de les Ciències Naturals de Barcelona, Monastery of Pedralbes, CCCB, etc.

Of course, there are plenty other sights to see and things do in Barcelona, for example, Casa Mila, Montjuïc Castle, Camp Nou, Tibidabo, Columbus monument, etc.
