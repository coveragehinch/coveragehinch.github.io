Wealth and poverty, immorality, and of course, wisdom.
These proverbs are short poem (usually in couplet form), containing a holy mixture of common sense and timely warnings.
Although they are not meant to teach doctrine, a person who follows their advice will walk closely with God.
The word “Proverb” comes a Hebrew word that means “to rule or to govern”, and these sayings, reminders, and admonitions provide profound advice for governing our lives.
