Following the success of his ‘Rap & Drill’ series, amassing over 17 Million views on Tik Tok alone, in recent months, South London based lyricist Big Jest returns to the channel with the music video to the latest instalment of the popular release, titled ‘Fresh Home’.

Best know for his wordplay and story telling, Big Jest recruits some familiar faces to help tell a story of revenge, betrayal and more relatable concepts, inspired by “real life”, what the rapper has seen, heard and, or been through.

The cinematic set of Kamcordings directed visuals features cameos from a star studied lineup of online personalities including Tyrone, MularJuice, DTG, ProdByWalkz, Bailey LDN and more.
