What does Neville Goddard teach?

When Neville says imagine, and imagine intense right before you sleep until your imagination comes into past, he is teaching you how to re-program your subconscious mind so it starts to change your beliefs. And you start with little manifestations and build up your faith that way.

Construct an Imaginal Scene that implies you are or have your desire .
Write Your Happy Ending.
Enter a Meditative State.
Imagine Your Desire in First Person.
Feel it with All Your Senses.
Impress Your Subconscious Mind.
Drop It and Let the Seed Grow.
