Hello everyone and thanks for tuning in to today's video. We're going to look at the weather for the next ten to fourteen days for this video. Day ten will take us to the nineteenth of December and we'll be able to extend out beyond that with the extended GFS and ECM ensembles (they run to around a couple of weeks) - We'll have a look at CFS v2 for the next four weeks to early January at the end of the video.

Mid-December looks set to find high pressure building up from the south turning the weather very mild and drier but with the AO and NAO both trending negative in the second half of the month could the high pressure go north and bring colder weather for Christmas???

Christmas weather is very much up for grabs right now...
