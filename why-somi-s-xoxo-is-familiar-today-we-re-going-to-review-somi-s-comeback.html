Today we’re going to review Somi’s comeback with XOXO, we’ll tackle why it’s familiar, my thoughts on the music video, the song, and honestly everything in between.

And though she’s under the black label, I feel as though she’s starting more and more to get comfortable with her own sound, which I would describe as angsty teen crush.
