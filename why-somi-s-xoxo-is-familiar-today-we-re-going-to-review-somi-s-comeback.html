Today we’re going to review Somi’s comeback with XOXO, we’ll tackle why it’s familiar, my thoughts on the music video, the song, and honestly everything in between.

Somi recently came out with her first album, also titled XOXO and featured all the songs she’s released since debut from Birthday to What you Waiting for and of course Dumb Dumb. And though she’s under the black label, I feel as though she’s starting more and more to get comfortable with her own sound, which I would describe as angsty teen crush. And this era in particular of Somi seems more refined, more detailed and even grungier than before, so I’m really excited to get into it.
