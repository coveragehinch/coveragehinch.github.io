One of Nigeria's most prosperous under 40, Ehizogie Ogbebor, without a doubt, is very beautiful, hardworking and can be very generous. Sadly the former model and self-made Billionaire have one flaw that allows people to drag her on social media literally every year.
Ehi, as fondly called, is a renowned owner of a luxury interior decorating company where she proudly caters to one per cent of the one in Nigeria.
The mother of three has featured on the cover of top magazines, blogs and interviewed with top media houses for her beauty and brains. And each time, she never fails to mention how she had always known she would be successful from the age of 6 after an Oba of Benin told her once that her head is meant for a crown.
The famous businesswoman has also come a long way when it comes to love, dating and marriage. The controversy surrounding her love life and lifestyle helped Ehi develop thick skin trolls can break — she tells her fans.
Operating from the comfort of her main office, as word on the street has it, the Edo-born divorcee hooks just about any man that catches her fancy after they patronize her business. Of course, how true this is has remained a subject of controversy as Ehi regularly fanned the flames of her shenanigans with married men, upon married men.
Currently dating a new man after her love affair with MC Oluoma crashed, Ehi is on the news again. This time, she is not only shaming her haters who are calling her out for jumping from one bad relationship to another while red flags are mounted all over this man. Interestingly, Ehi is also shaming the ex-wife of the man she is currently dating.
So why is everyone talking about Ehi's love life? Who are the men she dated? Why didn't the relationship work out? And lastly, why do many see her relationship with certain men as a pattern with flaws?
