From McDonalds, to Coke, Beats to Apple. Each company has a logo with a tonne of history. Their secrets hidden behind simple clean imagery. Either located in their origin such as Audi's Four Rings coming from it's 4 founding companies, or Toblerone with the bear from it's origin city Bern. Or in the tricks of illustration, such as the FedEx arrow hidden in negative space. There are so many interesting logos, with wonderful designs that we can all learn a lot from. Enjoy this collection of many of them!
