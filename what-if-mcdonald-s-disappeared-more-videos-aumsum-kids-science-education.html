What if McDonald's Disappeared?
Firstly, McDonald's serves over 69 million customers daily, if McDonald's disappeared, many people may start cooking again.
Secondly, if McDonald's disappeared, people would still dream of eating those world famous french fries.
Thirdly, if McDonald's disappeared, people will miss taking selfies with the primary mascot of McDonald's.
Fourthly, McDonald's has more than 37,000 outlets in over 100 countries, if McDonald's disappeared, many prime commercial estates will be up for grabs.
Fifthly, McDonald's employs more than 1.7 million people, if McDonald's disappeared, unemployment figures will skyrocket.
Lastly, if McDonald's disappeared, competitors of McDonald's will be one happy lot.
On the flip side, eating fast food frequently is not good for health, if McDonald's disappeared, people might start eating healthier food.
