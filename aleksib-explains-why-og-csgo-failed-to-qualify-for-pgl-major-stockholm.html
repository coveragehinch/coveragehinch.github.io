Aleksib Explains Why OG MISSED OUT on PGL Stockholm CSGO Major! | Despite consistently ranking among CSGO's top 10 teams, Aleksib, valde, mantuu, niko and flameZ failed to qualify for the CSGO PGL Stockholm Major in Sweden after losses to ZywOo's Vitality & b0rup's MAD Lions at IEM Fall 2021 subjected OG CSGO to an 11th-place finish.

Aleksib believes a change in format would have given OG a helping hand in qualifying, but mental blocks also stopped the team from playing at their peak.
