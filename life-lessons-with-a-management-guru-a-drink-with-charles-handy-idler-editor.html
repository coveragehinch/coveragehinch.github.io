Idler editor Tom Hodgkinson chats to management expert Charles Handy.

Philosopher and Idler magazine contributor Charles Handy is regularly rated in the Thinkers 50 list of the most influential living management thinkers. In this chat, Handy describes his career transition from Shell oil to management guru and self-professed 'storyteller'.
