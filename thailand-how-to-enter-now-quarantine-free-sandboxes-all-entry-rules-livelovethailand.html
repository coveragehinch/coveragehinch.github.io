In this V-log I am going to explain in details, all the new entry rules, for the 3 categories that can enter Thailand now, and all the documents that they should prepare and know before jumping into the plane.

Now everyone can enter Thailand, but they should know which category they belong, and what are the rules and obligations they should follow, so the three groups are, first the vaccinated ones from the eligible countries that they were announced lately, second the vaccinated tourists that are not in the eligible list, 3rd the unvaccinated tourists that are willing to visit Thailand, so what are the entry details for each group, and what documents they should prepare.
