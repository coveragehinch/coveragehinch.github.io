This webinar is hosted by monday.com champion user, Kyle McEwen, project manager at M Booth global digital communications agency based in NYC.

Kyle shares how the M Booth team manage some of their major processes to work collaboratively and ensure timely and smooth delivery of projects, across the board including: managing design requests and in-process requests, resource management and dashboards for forecasting and reporting.

Plus, creative rate cards, fee estimate tracking board, client timeline board with dependencies, and a "check out" board to know who has finished working each day when working from home!
