Ralf Rangnick was the keynote speaker at the inaugural Coaching Conference, staged by The Coaches’ Voice in London in September 2021.

In this excerpt from a wide-ranging discussion with host David Jones, the highly respected coach and sporting director explains his playing style and how data helped shape his philosophy. He speaks about the five key areas he wants his teams to be in control of, the importance of transitions – both defensive and offensive – and explains what he means by ‘rest defence’.
