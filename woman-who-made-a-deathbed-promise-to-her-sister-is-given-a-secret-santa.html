Juanita has a very big heart and has stepped up to the plate to care for her great-nieces and nephews.

When their grandmother passed away about four years ago, Juanita promised to take care of the children. She now has three kids in her home doing her best to raise and care for them.

Juanita cares deeply for their well-being and makes sure they attend school and church meetings as the kids have said they need God in their lives.

Keeping the promise she made to her dying sister, “Auntie Juanita” truly is willing to give and sacrifice for these children.
