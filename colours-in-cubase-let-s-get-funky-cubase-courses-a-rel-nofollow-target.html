Who are we?
Born To Produce was founded by professional music producers Jay Hales & Jon Merritt with the aim of providing super usable and informative tutorial videos, that they never had access to when they were climbing the music production ropes. The entire aim is to boil complex topics down into easy to understand and easy to repeat, bite-size steps, so that everyone who wants to can discover the utter pleasure of music production. They have been teaching music production via their 'make a song from start to finish' style for 8 years now and consistently get fantastic feedback from students.
