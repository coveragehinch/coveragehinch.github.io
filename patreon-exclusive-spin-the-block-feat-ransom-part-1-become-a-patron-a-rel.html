A heavily anticipated Patreon episode with one of Joe’s oldest industry friends, Ransom makes a great appearance in this two-part episode. They talk about the greatest rappers and where someone like Nas should or should not be held, how people perceive the underground, Ransom’s new projects, what it means to be a top lyricist, and how money effects an Emcee.
