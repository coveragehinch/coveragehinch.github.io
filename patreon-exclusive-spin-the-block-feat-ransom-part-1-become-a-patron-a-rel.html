A heavily anticipated Patreon episode with one of Joe’s oldest industry friends, Ransom makes a great appearance in this two-part episode. The gang interviews the Jersey rapper and digs in to his past, what he feels the game has become and is growing towards along with the contrasts with the older way of running the game. They talk about the greatest rappers and where someone like Nas should or should not be held, how people perceive the underground, Ransom’s new projects, what it means to be a top lyricist, and how money effects an Emcee.
