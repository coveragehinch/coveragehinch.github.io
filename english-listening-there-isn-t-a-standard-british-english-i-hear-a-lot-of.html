I hear a lot of people referring to BBC English, RP (received pronunciation), or the Queen's English as "standard English" or "standard pronunciation" but in this episode I explain why I don't think this is quite right.

Listening to natural conversational English is the most effective way to understand and replicate native English speakers. For that reason I talk naturally but a bit easier about everyday topics in everyday English so you can learn to understand and speak real native-like English.
