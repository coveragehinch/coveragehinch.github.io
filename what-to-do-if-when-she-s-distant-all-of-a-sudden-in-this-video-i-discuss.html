In this video, I discuss what happens when you've been seeing a girl for some time, and what to do when she's distant all of a sudden. It can be very frustrating when she backs off out of nowhere, and you're just left confused. That's why it's important that if/when she pulls away, you properly handle it after watching this video. If she became distant, there's usually 3 main reasons why she distanced herself from you. At times, she's acting distant to test you, but at others, she's distant due to something that you've done to cause her to be distant. Whether she just seems distant or is actually distant, is something you need to be able to pick up on and learn, if you really want to fix this problem.
