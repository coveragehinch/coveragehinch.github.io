In this video, I discuss what happens when you've been seeing a girl for some time, and what to do when she's distant all of a sudden. If she became distant, there's usually 3 main reasons why she distanced herself from you. At times, she's acting distant to test you, but at others, she's distant due to something that you've done to cause her to be distant. Whether she just seems distant or is actually distant, is something you need to be able to pick up on and learn, if you really want to fix this problem.
