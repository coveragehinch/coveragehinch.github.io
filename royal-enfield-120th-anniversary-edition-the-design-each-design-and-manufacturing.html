Each design and manufacturing decision on the 120th Anniversary Edition Interceptor 650 and Continental GT 650 is a celebration of Royal Enfield’s 120 years of Pure Motorcycling.
The two motorcycles feature a distinctive black chrome finish and an all-black body, elevated with handcrafted brass badges, hand painted pinstripes, and exclusive anniversary decals — including a unique serial number on their tanks.
120 years in the making, limited to 120 in each region — soon to be yours.
