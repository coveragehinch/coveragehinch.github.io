What Happens If The Supply Chain Breaks For 24 Hours?
We know prepping is necessary as the fragility of the supply chain has show itself over and over. But what happens if it stops for only 24 hours?

Relying on our just in time delivery system is no longer an option. Preppers everywhere know that complete and total failure of the supply chain is becoming a real possibility. If it shuts down for even 24 hours, things start to spiral out of control very quickly. It is likely that we would never be able to catch back up from even a single day of full disruption. This is why we prepare!
