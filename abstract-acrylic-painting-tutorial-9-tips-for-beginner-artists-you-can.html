9 TIPS FOR BEGINNERS:
1. Start with the focal point. It will make creation of your composition easier
2. Use rule of thirds. Place the focal point off-center to make the composition more dynamic and interesting
3. Create the guiding lines to emphasize the focal point
4. Let modeling paste dry completely. I would recommend to start next day
5. Wet the canvas to get smooth color transitions
6. Mix paints with unbleached white. It makes colors look less childish and more sophisticated
7. Use very soft flat synthetic brush to smooth the gradient
8. Apply gilding wax to highlight the relief of modeling paste
9. Make your signature as a part of the composition in order not to ruin it
