“Have a look at this question and marvel at the answer … a question about Homer, the ancient Greek poet who gave us the Illiad and the Odyssey, and yet you get answers relating to his namesake, a cartoon character.

“Yeah, easy to confuse, remember all those epic poems in the Simpsons?”
