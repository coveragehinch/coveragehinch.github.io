CCTV footage shows Sean Bulle laughing and joking with Paulius Petrasiunas, a friend he would stab to death shortly afterwards.

Bulle was caught on camera fleeing the scene in Wolverhampton. He was arrested in Shrewsbury two days later and is now facing life in prison.
