Well, although the key differences between the turbulent and assertive INFJ aren't so easy to point out, there are definitely a few INFJ characteristics that set these two INFJ subtypes apart.

For example, when it comes to INFJ stress, the INFJ-T (turbulent INFJ) is known to be the more sensitive of the 2 INFJ types, easily expressing when they're stressed and overwhelmed.
