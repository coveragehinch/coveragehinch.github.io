INFJ-T vs INFJ-A - The World's Rarest Personality Type - So, you've been typed as an INFJ personality type, the rarest of the 16 personalities according to the MBTI test.. but now you're stuck wondering "What's the difference between an INFJ-T vs INFJ-A?"

Well, although the key differences between the turbulent and assertive INFJ aren't so easy to point out, there are definitely a few INFJ characteristics that set these two INFJ subtypes apart.

For example, when it comes to INFJ stress, the INFJ-T (turbulent INFJ) is known to be the more sensitive of the 2 INFJ types, easily expressing when they're stressed and overwhelmed. While the INFJ-A (Assertive INFJ) on the other hand seems less effected by external everyday stress and are more focused on the bigger picture.
