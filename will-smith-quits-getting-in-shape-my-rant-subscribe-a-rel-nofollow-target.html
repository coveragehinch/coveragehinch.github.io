Will Smith's brand new YouTube show 'Best Shape Of My Life' has recently gone live, and in episode 4... he gives up. Yup! That shouldn't deter you – far from it – but hopefully seeing someone like this go through the worst can inspire you to get back on track as and when you need a kick up the butt!
