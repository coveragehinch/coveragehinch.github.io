Stop Dressing Your Age & Do This Instead | Fashion Over 40 2021
The idea of dressing your age is outdated and in in today's video I’m going to tell you why and what you should be doing instead.I regularly get asked whether a particular fashion trend or clothing item can be worn at a certain age so I thought now was a great time to share how I answer this question. I always believe that the clothes that look and feel best the best on you have more to do with your body, lifestyle, style preference and confidence than the year you were born. That doesn’t mean your fashion choices don’t change as your body and lifestyle change – it just means your personal style may change. So in today’s episode I’m sharing some style secrets that have helped with my style evolution and might just help you too. I'd love to hear if you have any style secrets to dressing at any age in the comments below and as always thanks for watching and I'll see you in the next upload.
