The idea of dressing your age is outdated and in in today's video I’m going to tell you why and what you should be doing instead.I regularly get asked whether a particular fashion trend or clothing item can be worn at a certain age so I thought now was a great time to share how I answer this question. I always believe that the clothes that look and feel best the best on you have more to do with your body, lifestyle, style preference and confidence than the year you were born. So in today’s episode I’m sharing some style secrets that have helped with my style evolution and might just help you too.
