"If I Die Young" (previously referred to as "On & On & On" or "Going On & On") is an unreleased song by Lana Del Rey. The song is speculated to be recorded for her fifth studio album and fourth major-label studio album, Lust for Life, but ultimately did not make the final tracklist. A snippet of the song leaked on September 15, 2021, followed by a second one the next day. A third snippet leaked on September 29. The full track leaked on October 1.
