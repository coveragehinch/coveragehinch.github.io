An all-Canadian cast of backcountry chargers descended the feature-packed terrain of Baldface Valhalla earlier this month, with the goal of capturing the best single top-to-bottom line and complimentary video part. These two components — weighted 70/30 — were ranked according to risk, flow, creativity, and control, and winners secured a ticket to Tordrillo Mountain Lodge.

With the likes of Craig McMorris, Marie-France Roy, Chris Rasman, and Robyn Van Gyn in the mix, the competition was stiff and the stakes amounted to a chance at 2021 Natural Selection glory. Here’s what went down.
