An all-Canadian cast of backcountry chargers descended the feature-packed terrain of Baldface Valhalla earlier this month, with the goal of capturing the best single top-to-bottom line and complimentary video part. With the likes of Craig McMorris, Marie-France Roy, Chris Rasman, and Robyn Van Gyn in the mix, the competition was stiff and the stakes amounted to a chance at 2021 Natural Selection glory. Here’s what went down.
