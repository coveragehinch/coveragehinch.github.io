In this video, we are going to install Piwigo. With these apps you can manage your photos. An advantage of this application is the possibility to use it directly on your cell phone. This way there is no need to use this application on the website.
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
For more details about Piwigo, check out the link:
https://piwigo.org/

In this installation we will use the linux server/piwigo image. Check out the link:
https://hub.docker.com/r/linuxserver/piwigo
- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
For the database installation we will use the linuxserver/mariadb image. Check out the link:
https://hub.docker.com/r/linuxserver/mariadb
