Here's why NFTs are bad news for energy consumption and the environment.

Crypto art and non-fungible tokens (NFTs) are like expensive trading cards that you can sell, trade, and own in the blockchain — the same service used to trade cryptocurrency like Bitcoin or Ethereum.

These types of crypto transactions require massive amounts of energy because of the high-end computers required to sell, trade, and store cryptocurrency. They could consume as much energy as a small country.

Because of this, some artists have stopped selling with NFTs. More, if not all, cryptocurrency machines should run on renewable energy like wind, solar, and hydropower.
