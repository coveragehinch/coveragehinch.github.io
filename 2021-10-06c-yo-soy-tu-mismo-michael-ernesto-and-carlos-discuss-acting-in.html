In a Zoom meeting with Ernesto and Carlos of ‘Yo Soy Tu Mismo’ (a group of Spanish devotees of Bhagavan Sri Ramana) on 6th October 2021, Michael James answers the questions:

“We don’t know what we will experience in a year’s time, for example, could some of the reactions I stop having between now and then, detachment from likes and dislikes, understanding the meaninglessness of certain beliefs I still have as an ego, etc. be part of the Prarabdha Karma I will experience in a year’s time, etc.? That is, could acting as if everything depends on me as an ego imply that the will I put into trying to stop feeding my ego is part of what I have to do to make certain events happen or not happen in my experience one year from now?”

While answering these questions Michael refers to and/or discusses the following passages from Bhagavan’s writings:

The note he wrote for his mother in December 1898: https://happinessofbeing.blogspot.com/2020/08/praising-or-disparaging-others-is.html#dec98note

Uḷḷadu Nāṟpadu verse 38: https://happinessofbeing.blogspot.com/2017/10/ulladu-narpadu-tamil-text.html#un38

Upadēśa Undiyār verse 2: https://happinessofbeing.blogspot.com/2017/09/upadesa-undiyar-tamil-text.html#uu02
