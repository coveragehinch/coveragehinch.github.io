New World Patch 1.0.5 comes with a massive amount of fixes that the game needed. The Hatchet bug receives a fix and so do other perks that work similar, such as the great axe. Resilient is now finally working as intended and will no longer affect all damage. Gear Score is no longer capped at 589 (with 591 max drops), so we should actually be able to farm legendary loot. Outpost rush receives a variety of much needed fixes, too.
