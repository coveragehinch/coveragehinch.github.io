3 Tbs Fish Sauce
3 Tbs Tamarind Concentrate

1 Tbs Water

Fresh Ingredients:
3 Cloves Garlic
Some Garlic Chives (Spring Onion/Scallions work as a sub)
Half of a Shallot
2 Tbs Dried Shrimp
Peanuts
Lime
