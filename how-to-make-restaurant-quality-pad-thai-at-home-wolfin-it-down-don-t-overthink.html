Don't overthink it. Just go to the Asian Market, buy these ingredients and learn to make one of the most expensive stir fries around. For significantly less money at that.

3 Tbs Fish Sauce
3 Tbs Tamarind Concentrate
3.5 Tbs Sugar
1 Tbs Water

Fresh Ingredients:
3 Cloves Garlic
Some Garlic Chives (Spring Onion/Scallions work as a sub)
Half of a Shallot
2 Tbs Dried Shrimp
Peanuts
Lime

1 Tbs Sweet Preserved Radish
