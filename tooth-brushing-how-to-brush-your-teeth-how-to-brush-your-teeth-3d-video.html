How to brush your teeth? 3d video of Clínica Médico Dental Pardiñas (http://j.mp/cPardinas) about the correct procedure of brushing teeth.

Everyone has to find a way to do it in the best possible way, reaching well everywhere. And do it with a medium tootbrush, never a hard toothbrush. Brushing is important to avoid or reduce the incidence of caries, prevent peridontal disease, avoid halitosis, prevent / reduce plaque and tartar, and have clean teeth.

The toothbrush must be changed at least every three months, and beforehand if the bristles are open. Toothpaste can be fluoride to prevent cavities or another toothpaste if your dentist recommends it. A small quantity should be put in the toothbrush (the size of a pea). The duration of brushing should be three minutes. After that, rinsing with water should be done.
