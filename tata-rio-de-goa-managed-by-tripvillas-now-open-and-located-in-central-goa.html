Tata Rio de Goa, fully managed by Tripvillas is now open. Located in Central Goa, near Dabolim Airport, the property has the benefit of being directly accessible to the famous beaches and tourist areas of both North and South Goa, and at the same time, is in the midst of peace and tranquillity that used to be associated with Goa. Offering a wide variety of spacious, luxurious apartments for both sales and rentals, Rio de Goa is filled to the brim with high-end facilities such as a gym, infinity pool, sun deck, amphitheatre, restaurant, sauna, squash court, indoor games and much more.
