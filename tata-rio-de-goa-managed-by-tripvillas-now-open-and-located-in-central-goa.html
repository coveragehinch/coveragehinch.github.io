Tata Rio de Goa, fully managed by Tripvillas is now open. Located in Central Goa, near Dabolim Airport, the property has the benefit of being directly accessible to the famous beaches and tourist areas of both North and South Goa, and at the same time, is in the midst of peace and tranquillity that used to be associated with Goa.
