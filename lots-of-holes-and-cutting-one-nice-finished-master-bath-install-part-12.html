Lots of holes and cutting, one nice finished. Master Bath install. Large format tile continues to be installed the a side wall is started, still lots of cutting in this huge master bathroom. Installed most of the left wall but decided to finish one of the niches, which has a grand total of 17 pieces. Holes for the mixing valve diverter and the hand held had to be cut. I used a grinder with a dry cutting diamond blade for the larger hole and diamond hole saw for the smaller ones. When there is so much cutting that needs to be done very accurately it takes time to get the tile installed and set. I talk about many important details that will help use a wedge type leveling system efficiently and effectively. Also about the details that make for a great install rather than just a good one. All tile installed by Sal DiBlasi, Elite-tile Company, in the Boston North Shore area.
