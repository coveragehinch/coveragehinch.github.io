Gorenje have launched a brilliant product which is an essential part of our daily household. It is Freestanding gas cooker which has chic and modern look to it with stainless steel body and glass door and cover top.
It has superb ergonomic knob for easy accessing, big surface and powerful Chef burners.
This gas cooker has ultra-resistant and ultra-smooth silver matte to tolerate extreme heat while cooking and baking.
The characteristic rounded shape is the most beneficial features of Gorenje cooker inspired by traditional wood- burning ovens which enable the hot air to move around freely.
It has enlarged baking surface for extra big trays, powerful new wok burner with special shape of gas crown and great capacity of oven which is 70 litres.
Overall, this gas cooker is highly modified and equipped to handle heavy duty baking and cooking and it’s a great add on to any kitchen.
