Brazilian Air Force Will Receive Several SAAB J-39 Fighters Later This Month - the Brazilian Air Force will have received several #J39 #Gripen #combatfighters from Sweden's saab aerospace, avionics and defense consortium, part of the agreement signed in 2014 by Brazil for the purchase of 36 of these jets, following a complicated bidding process. The J 39 Gripens will be transported by sea at the end of November, following on the same logistic route as the first unit which was sent to Brazil just over a year ago, with the Portonave private terminal in Navegantes, state of Santa Catarina, as gateway to Brazil. @FORCE Technology
