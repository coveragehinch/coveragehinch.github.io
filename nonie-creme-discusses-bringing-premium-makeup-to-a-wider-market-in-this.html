In this installment of the Package Design Matters Series, PD interviews Nonie Creme, founder and creative director at Nonie Creme Colour Prevails and former co-founding creative director at Butter London.

Takeaways:
- Find out how Creme is using her knowledge of prestige cosmetics to bring premium makeup and innovative package design to a wider market.
- Learn the inside scoop on the company’s exclusive distribution agreement with Walgreens.
- Get insights on some of the brand’s unique packaging styles.
