🇺🇸 If there's one haircut that remains effortlessly chic, it's the tousled bob. We love the simplicity of this shape and the soft, airy steps. This cut is all about movement that occurs because a lot of weight is removed. At the same time, individual strands are also allowed to overlap the compact outline.
