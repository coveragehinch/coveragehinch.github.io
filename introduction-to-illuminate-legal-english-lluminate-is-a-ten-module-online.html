lluminate is a ten-module online legal English programme for international lawyers and law students: https://www.cambridgelawstudio.co.uk/online-courses/

A major aim of Illuminate is to increase your employability in an increasingly competitive job market. This course does what a legal English dictionary cannot help you with!
