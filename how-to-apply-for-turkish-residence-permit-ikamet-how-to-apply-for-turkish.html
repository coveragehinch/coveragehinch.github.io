How to Apply for Turkish Residence Permit | IKAMET. Once you have entered Turkey on a tourist visa and your plans are to stay for more than 3 months consecutively, then you must apply for a Turkish residence permit, in Turkish is known as ikamet.

In this video, we will guide you step by step on how to process it, what documents you will need, and the types of residences Turkey is providing.
