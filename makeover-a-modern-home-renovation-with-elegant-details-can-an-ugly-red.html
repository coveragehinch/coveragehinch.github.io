Can an ugly red brick building of rental units become a dazzling home filled with light? Yes it can! Designer Tara Finlay of Ancerl Studio used classic, high-end finishes and detailing to create a Parisian-inspired house in Toronto. You'll love the gourmet kitchen built around a Lacanche range, folding glass doors and classic Carrara marble. No detail was overlooked to totally transform the old, worn structure into this dreamy space!

Design, Tara Finlay, Ancerl Studio, https://www.ancerlstudio.com/
