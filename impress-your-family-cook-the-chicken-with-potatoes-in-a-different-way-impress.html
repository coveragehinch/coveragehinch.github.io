Impress your FAMILY - cook the CHICKEN with POTATOES in a different way!

Ingredients:
bacon - 350 g (12.35 oz)
chicken breast - 350 g (12.35 oz)
pressed cheese - 250 g (8.8 oz)
potatoes - 350 g (12.35 oz)
after each potato layer:
salt - 3 g (0.11 oz)
black pepper - 3 g (0.11 oz)
sweet paprika - 3 g (0.11 oz)
Tray size 10 x 24 cm (4 x 9.45 in)
IN THE OVEN 180 °C (356 °F)/35 minutes
