Hardy 42 Hybrid specification
LOA: 45ft 9in (14m)
Beam: 14ft 1in (4.3m)
Engines: Single 440hp diesel Yanmar & 20kW electric motor
Top speed: 16 knots
Starting price: £954,000 (inc. VAT)
