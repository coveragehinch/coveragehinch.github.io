WHAT A BEAUTIFUL MOMENT AT MAYON VOLCANO IN BICOL!

The road trip with my Girlfriend across the Philippines continues. We head towards Matnog to catch a boat, but along the way find Mt. Mayon clearing! Speaking of driving... we are also taking boats!
