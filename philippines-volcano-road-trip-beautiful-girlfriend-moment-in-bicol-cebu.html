WHAT A BEAUTIFUL MOMENT AT MAYON VOLCANO IN BICOL!

The road trip with my Girlfriend across the Philippines continues. We head towards Matnog to catch a boat, but along the way find Mt. Mayon clearing! What a stunning moment... so beautiful seeing this world famous volcano. The weather has been a bit difficult, but moments of clearing truly are magical while driving through Bicol!

Speaking of driving... we are also taking boats!

We made it to Matnog port, and thought for a moment we would catch a boat to Cebu! There is a new trip that goes three times a week from Matnog to Bogo!

But we missed it... and that turned into a long road trip night. Beautiful experiences, and tiring experiences.
