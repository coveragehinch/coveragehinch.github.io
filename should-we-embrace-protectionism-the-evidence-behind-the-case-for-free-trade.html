“Should We Embrace Protectionism? The Evidence Behind the Case for Free Trade”, was presented by Dave Donaldson, Ph.D., professor of economics at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), at the 33rd annual Fall Henry George Lecture on Nov. 2, 2018. The lecture series is presented by The University of Scranton’s Economics and Finance Department and the campus chapter of Omicron Delta Epsilon, an international honor society for economics, and is supported financially by a grant from the Robert Schalkenbach Foundation.
