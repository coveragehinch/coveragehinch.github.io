How far an electric vehicle can go on a single charge is one of the biggest barriers to entry for many punters, but it need not be a concern. Fortunately, there are now many electric cars offering claimed ranges in excess of 250 miles – allowing them to travel far and wide on a single charge. This, in conjunction with ever-decreasing charge times, means electric motoring is now an option for even more buyers.
