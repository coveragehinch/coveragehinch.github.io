The Degrees debut on Goldie's Fallen Tree 1Hundred label with a neo-soul crossover gem ‘All Said and Done’. The production touches on the slick, acid jazz sound of The Brand New Heavies, adds a dash of vintage Soul II Soul, talks loud and says something… The song itself a message of self-reflection, of looking back, being content with yourself and what you have in life.

Written and Produced by Charlie Bierman & Isha Campbell
