The term "accessible" has more than one meaning related to equity in education. And it's often confused with related terms about access for students with disabilities, such as accommodation, adaptation, or assistive technology. Join us as we take a deep dive into (1) distinguishing the meanings of "accessible" and (2) clarifying what "accessible" means in the context of access for students with disabilities.
