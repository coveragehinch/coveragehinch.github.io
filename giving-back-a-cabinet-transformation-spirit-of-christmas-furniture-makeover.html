Second Chance Ohana- I’m Dani, a wife and mother of three adopted teens and am working full time as a Furniture Flipper and YT creator in Phoenix, Arizona. My mom, Sherry, is a wife, mother, grandmother and retired RN. We share a passion for all things DIY. We are now using our design creativity and problem solving skills to turn our passion and hobby for painting and restoring furniture into a full fledged, furniture flipping business.
