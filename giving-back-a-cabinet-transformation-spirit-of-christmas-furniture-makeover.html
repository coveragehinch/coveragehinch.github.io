For this furniture makeover, Dani and Sherry update a 1930’s cabinet to gift to a wonderful couple who work with people struggling with addictions at Teen Challenge of AZ.

Second Chance Ohana- I’m Dani, a wife and mother of three adopted teens and am working full time as a Furniture Flipper and YT creator in Phoenix, Arizona. My mom, Sherry, is a wife, mother, grandmother and retired RN. We share a passion for all things DIY. We are now using our design creativity and problem solving skills to turn our passion and hobby for painting and restoring furniture into a full fledged, furniture flipping business. We teach you how to makeover furniture and how to make a profit doing it. We hope you are inspired by our channel and we invite you to partner and learn with us as we give old, broken-down furniture a Second Chance!
