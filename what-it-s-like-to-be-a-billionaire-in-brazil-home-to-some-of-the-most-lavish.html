Home to some of the most lavish cities in the world like Sao Paulo and Rio De Janeiro, this South American staple country is home to some of the richest people in the world. The lavish activities in this beautiful country range from exotic cars, to exotic cuisine, to luxury fashion brands known the world over. Football, Carnivals, and a booming market for commerce and culture makes this gorgeous South American nation one of the most lavish countries in the world where people can earn up to 30 billion dollars. The richest in the country range from big tech creators to investors to medical physicians who own and operate their own hospital and laboratory chains. The nation is known for many lavish activities that give the country’s wealthiest plenty of things to do when they’re not busy raking in the dough.

If you lived in a country like this, and had more money than you knew what to do with, what would be your first activity? Would you purchase front row seats to the World Cup? Or perhaps drop $62,000 on a gold necklace from one of the country’s high end jewelry brands? Also, if you had to choose between the two, would you rather visit the metropolis of Sao Paulo, or the paradise of Rio De Janeiro?
