Do you know that it isn't enough for an actuaries to calculate the reserve needed just until the loss incurred?

In practical, actuaries also need to calculate the reserve until it is paid. The reserve gap between the loss incurred until it is paid that what we call as 'claim reserve'. Wonder how actuaries calculate and predict claim reserve? Especially for losses that have been incurred but not reported (IBNR)?

There are two methods of calculating IBNR Reserve. It is Chain Ladder Method and Bornhuetter-Ferguson Method.

Let's dig it out how to calculate IBNR Reserve in this video!
