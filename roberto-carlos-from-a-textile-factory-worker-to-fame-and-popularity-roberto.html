Robert Carlos which was know for his speed, shooting power, and ability to take free kick shots ended his football career leaving behind him amazing goals which remain till now in the memories of many of his fans. One of these goals was the stunning curved free kick against France in the late 90's. The free kick was from a very far distance and none of the players expected that Roberto Carlos will shoot directly at the goal.
