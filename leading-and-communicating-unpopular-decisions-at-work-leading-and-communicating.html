Leading and Communicating - Unpopular Decisions at Work

Have you ever had an interview question about making unpopular decisions at work? Leading and communicating during these times is more important than ever. The way you go about communicating decisions to employees can make or break your organization. This week I'll be sharing some tips on communicating leadership decisions. I'll also be talking about communication and management skills.
