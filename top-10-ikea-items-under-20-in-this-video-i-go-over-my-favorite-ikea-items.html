In this video, I go over my favorite IKEA Items for under $20! I've done top 10 IKEA items lists in the past, but I thought it would be fun to limit myself to those amazing items that are under $20. These items are clever, beautiful and priced under $20 and they are such a good deal compared to similar products at other retailers. I've also really prioritized natural materials here like jute and bamboo just because I think they feel higher end than plastic, while still being affordable!
