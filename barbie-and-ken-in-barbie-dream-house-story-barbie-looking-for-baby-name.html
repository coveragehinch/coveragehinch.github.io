In Barbie Dream House, Barbie and Ken are showing Chelsea a calendar and explaining when Barbie’s Baby finally arrives. Chelsea can’t wait to be a big sister! They don’t have a name for Barbie’s baby! Barbie is stressing out about not having the name, so Ken and Chelsea go out to find the name. While they are looking for the name outside, Barbie stays home and searches the internet. She finds the perfect name in no time! Barbie’s sister Chelsea goes to the playground on her cute little train. She gives rides for little kids and asks all of them for their favorite baby name. Soon she finds the perfect name too. Ken is puzzled. He has no idea how to find the baby name, so he gets on Barbie’s scooter and rides to Madame Sylvia, the voodoo gipsy women that can predict future. He is little scared, but she gives him a card with the perfect name. When they all meet at Barbie house, they realize that they get the same name for Barbie’s Baby. Barbie, Ken and Chelsea had a great time searching for name for Barbie’s baby!
