They don’t have a name for Barbie’s baby! Barbie is stressing out about not having the name, so Ken and Chelsea go out to find the name. While they are looking for the name outside, Barbie stays home and searches the internet. She finds the perfect name in no time! Ken is puzzled. He has no idea how to find the baby name, so he gets on Barbie’s scooter and rides to Madame Sylvia, the voodoo gipsy women that can predict future. Barbie, Ken and Chelsea had a great time searching for name for Barbie’s baby!
