While they are looking for the name outside, Barbie stays home and searches the internet. She finds the perfect name in no time! Ken is puzzled. He has no idea how to find the baby name, so he gets on Barbie’s scooter and rides to Madame Sylvia, the voodoo gipsy women that can predict future. He is little scared, but she gives him a card with the perfect name. When they all meet at Barbie house, they realize that they get the same name for Barbie’s Baby. Barbie, Ken and Chelsea had a great time searching for name for Barbie’s baby!
