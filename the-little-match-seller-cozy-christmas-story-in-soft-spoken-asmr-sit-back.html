Sit back and enjoy this sad, but beautiful Christmas story written by Hans Christian Andersen in 1846.

While the story tells about a little poor girl in the midst of a cold winter night, the last night of the year, it also highlights the importance of being kind to one another. Feel and spread compassion, love and warmth, as you listen to this quiet and peaceful reading.

Close your eyes as you listen to this old story, relax and feel the magical Christmas spirit.
