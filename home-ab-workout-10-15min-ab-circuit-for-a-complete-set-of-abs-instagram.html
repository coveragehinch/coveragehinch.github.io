A quick but really effective ab workout you can do at home.
It will take not more then 10 - 15 minutes.

There are 7 exercises:
1) Sit-ups (20 reps)
2) Leg raises (20)
3) Side crunches on your back, elbow to knee ( 15 reps each side)
4) Bicycle with only legs ( 20 reps )
5) Side crunches on your side with leg raises ( 30 reps each side )
6) Hands to feet ( 15 reps )
7) Planking ( 1 minute )

You can do a burn out after the cicuit training. If you have a place at home you can hang by your hands. You can do a AMRAP with knee raises to get a nice burn in your lower abs.

Try this out for a couple of weeks and you will see a difference.

PS: if you just started doing abs it will be hard to do some of these exercises. So i recommend you to do half of the reps and only 3 times. And the leg raises, you don't need to straighten your legs all the way.
