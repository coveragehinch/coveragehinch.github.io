Sjogren's syndrome is a chronic autoimmune disease in which people's white blood cells attack their moisture-producing glands. Today, as many as four million Americans are living with this disease.



About the video

This program will introduce you to three Sjogren's patients who will share their journey with you. In addition, you will hear from Sjogren's experts about the causes, treatments and manifestations of Sjogren's.
