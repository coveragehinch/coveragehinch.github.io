Lesson 556 - How to use A LOT OF LOTs OF MUCH and MANY English Grammar

This lesson we are going to learn about the meaning and the use of the word a lot of, lots of, much and many.

Much + uncountable noun
I don’t have much money.
We don’t know much about him.
He doesn’t have much time left.
Does this car consume much gas?
How much money should I bring?

Many + countable noun
I don’t have many friends.
There aren’t many jobs here.
You can’t do many things at once.
Do you think many people join?
How many books do you have?

A lot of + uncountable noun
I need a lot of coffee.
= I need lots of coffee.
There is a lot of information today.
=There is lots of information today.
I need much coffee.
I need a lot o f /lots of coffee.

A lot of + countable noun
She has a lot of friends.
= she has lots of friends.
There are a lot of tourists today.
=There are lots of tourists today.
I have many friends.
I have a lot of/lots of friends.
