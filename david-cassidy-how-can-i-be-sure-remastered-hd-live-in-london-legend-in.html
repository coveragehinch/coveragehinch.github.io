In Britain "How Can I Be Sure" was a #1 hit for two weeks in the singles charts being the second of Cassidy's overall 11 UK chart hits six of which would reach the Top Ten including his second #1 UK hit: the double A-side hit "Daydreamer"/"The Puppy Song" (#1 for three weeks).
