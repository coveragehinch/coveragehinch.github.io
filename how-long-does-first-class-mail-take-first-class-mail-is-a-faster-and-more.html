Virtual mailboxes are online platforms (think e-mail) via which you can access all your postal mail and packages. Once you receive something in the mail, it gets scanned and uploaded into your account. So you can read your letters and see your packages from your phone or computer.

All junk mail gets thrown out before entering your mailbox, allowing you to only receive mail items that are important to you.
