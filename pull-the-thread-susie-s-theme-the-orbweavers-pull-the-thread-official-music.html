Written in and out of Melbourne’s lockdowns, on the rhythm of daily walks in nylon tracksuit pants, with our greyhound companion Susie. Tracing a pattern of streets. Silent nights. She bounds down the hallway and leaps onto the bed, a towering joyous wave. Drops and curls into a circle. Makes us smile.
