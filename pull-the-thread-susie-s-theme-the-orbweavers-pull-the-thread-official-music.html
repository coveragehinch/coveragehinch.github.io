Written in and out of Melbourne’s lockdowns, on the rhythm of daily walks in nylon tracksuit pants, with our greyhound companion Susie. Tracing a pattern of streets. Silent nights. Watching sewing and embroidery videos. Susie chews on the Venetian blind cord and unravels the weave of a blanket. Stuart buys a small handloom. On the hard days, I call out to Susie: “Jump up!”. She bounds down the hallway and leaps onto the bed, a towering joyous wave. Drops and curls into a circle. Makes us smile.
