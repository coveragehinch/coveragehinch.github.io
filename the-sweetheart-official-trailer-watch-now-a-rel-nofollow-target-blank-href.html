With her parents Paul and Samantha now separated, Jane adjusts to life at home without her dad as she works through her physical rehabilitation. Jane is blindsided when Samantha introduces her mysterious new boyfriend Brian, a handsome younger man with excessive charm.

When a physical altercation between Paul and Brian takes place in front of Jane at their family home, Paul is given a restraining order by the police and Jane’s life is in turmoil. As Brian tries to inch his way closer to Samantha and her family, Jane becomes suspicious of Brian and starts to believe that he has dangerous intentions.
