After posting my "how all my hair fell out" storytime video a week ago I have gotten such a response I realized how much information I accidentally left out. Hopefully in this video I can further explain and answer all your questions.

How All My Hair Fell Out Because of One Product - Q & A

The main question (of course) was "What product did you use to make your hair fall out?" What I used was an at home hair relaxer and conditioner cream that was advertised to relax curly hair which I wanted so I could stop damaging my hair by blowdrying and straightening my bleached hair everyday. The warnings on the directions did say "Use caution when using on damaged hair due to possible hair loss", I was unaware that when you bleach your hair, dye your hair, tone your hair, color your hair, (etc) all of that damages your hair beyond repair. In turn when I used a product I believed would fix my dry damaged hair in minutes, it actually caused all my hair to burn up and get so fragile that is broke apart and fell out as I washed the product out of my hair. I am sure my hair will all grow back even though it was a pretty good chemical burn. It was a mistake and a huge hair fail on my part.

After reading many negative comments asking me what reason do I have to cry about this situation when there are worse things going on in the world. I know this, I dont think my hair falling out due to a mistake can compare. I simply shared a story that happened to me. If you went through a hard time and want to make a video about it then I fully support you.

Questions about me deleting my instagram pictures - I archived them so I was able to put them back up. After my hair fell out and I had to get my whole head shaved I woke up from a restless night and was so ashamed and depressed that looking at pictures of me with gorgeously long platinum blonde hair made everything worse. So I took everything down and was unsure if I would ever feel good enough to post or even live stream ever again.

Will you bleach your hair again? Not planning on it! I am using many oils and nourishing treatments on what was left of my hair currently and am only focusing on growth and keeping my hair as naturally healthy as I can. I would like to give a HUGE thank you to everyone that commented/messaged/emailed me giving me tips and advice on how to grow my hair fast. I appreciate it so much.

Was my social medias tied to my hair? In a round about way, yes. I am able to live the life I have by daily live broadcasting and the base of my broadcasts are to uplift people and I was feeling so down about myself that I felt there was no way I could put on an act and pretend everything was okay.

How do you feel about all the attention? It is so hard to believe so many people found my video. The only reason I posted it was to give an explanation to my friends/family and those that watch my broadcasts. I am overwhelmed with love and happiness with the support I received from my last video.

Thank you so much for watching, make sure to SUBSCRIBE to keep up to date with me!

Much love
