Today's theme is winter fragrances, and I made a selection of 5 perfumes I like and wear when I want something warm and comforting.

Guerlain Shalimar (my flanker is Ode à la Vanille, but I recommend any version of Shalimar; they are all beautiful)
Hermessence Ambre Narguilé
Serge Lutens Ambre Sultan
Frédéric Malle Iris Poudre
Attar

I wanted to mention attar in this video to highlight the traditional perfume style, and while I don't have a specific recommended blend, here are two reputable sources for attar:
Authentic -
M.l. Ramnarain Perfumers (they also have an Instagram page, which I recommend following to learn about distillation)
Ali Brothers Kannauj -- I visited their distillation in Kannauj, and I liked their attars very much. They also an Instagram page and can be contacted directly to learn about their products.
Affordable -
Enfleurage.com is a reliable supplier of natural essences, including attar blends. Their selection varies, but I have their rose and lotus attars (made with sustainably grown sandalwood) and they are nice. I would describe them more as perfume blends, rather than true attars, but they convey the richness and opulence well.
