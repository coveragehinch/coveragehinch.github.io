A US$9 million rare sapphire from Brazil which gleams in the dark and carefully matched, rare diamond sets were among the stand-out attractions at the HKTDC Hong Kong International Diamond, Gem and Pearl Show 2015, running 2-6 March 2015 at Asia-World Expo in Hong Kong. The world’s largest jewellery market also included the twinned Hong Kong International Jewellery Show 2015 which saw the launch of the Natural Diamonds Quality Assurance Mark Scheme.
