PNG swimming has seen the injection of fourteen new swimmers this year...all have proven worthy of representing the country.

They all participated in the Fina World Swimming Championships in Canada, early this month.

Pacific Games star and Commonwealth Games gold medallist Ryan Pini, along with Livingston Aika, Stanford Kawale, Ryan Maskelyne, Shanice Paraka, Ashley Seeto, and Sam Seghers are some of the swimmers are some of PNG's top swimmers competiting.
