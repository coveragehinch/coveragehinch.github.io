At the Moldconstruct Fair in Moldova we decided to put the quality that Frisomat produces to the test in public. Two bolts were placed into a bowl with water to see if they would show any sign of oxidation.

At the end of the fair, two days later, they didn't show any sign of oxidation whatsoever!

All Frisomat metal parts of a steel industrial building, from the smallest bolt or washer to the biggest column are 100% dip galavanized and ready to take on any weather condition possible.
Our steel buildings are built to last!
