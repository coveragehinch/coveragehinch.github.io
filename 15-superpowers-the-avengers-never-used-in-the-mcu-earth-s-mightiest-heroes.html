Yes, in the MCU we’ve seen plenty of superpowers that leave us in awe, like Cap’s super strength, Hulk’s ability to smash and Iron Man’s genius-level intellect, but what about all of the abilities that we didn’t see? Having decades worth of comic history means that our favorite heroes have gained and lost a plethora of other abilities that you might not know about and that’s what we’re going to talk about today!

Did you know that Tony Stark once developed technopath abilities and could actually speak to machines? If the hero was still alive, it definitely feels like that would be the next upgrade after the nanotech armor wouldn’t it? I guess we’ll never know!
