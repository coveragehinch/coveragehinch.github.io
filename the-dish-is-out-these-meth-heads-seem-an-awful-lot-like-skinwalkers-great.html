The Dish is Out is a horror game made for a gamejam about working late at night in a blizzard. You have to reset a a communication satellite and also avoid what is lurking in the woods.

A short horror game about repairing a satellite dish.

After being awoken in the night by your coworker, you must venture out into a blizzard to repair an important satellite dish. However strange things happen in the dark and your coworker may not be as they seem.
