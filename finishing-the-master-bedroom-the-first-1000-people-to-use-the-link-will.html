It's time to finish the master bedroom in our home! This is the final interior project. Since there are no permanent bedrooms in this portion of the A-frame, the utility room will serve as our master bedroom until the final bedroom wing is built onto the house. We need to make what is supposed to be the utility room work as a bedroom, laundry room and office until we complete the other side of the house. The plan is to rearrange the room, build cabinetry for storage, a desk for the computer and add countertops for folding laundry. It's a mess right now but once we are done the whole room will be transformed!
