a young woman recalls her relationship with her friend following her sudden disappearance

a big special thanks to both of my tremendous media teachers and all of my patient cast & crew who helped put together this passion project amidst endless assignments, sacs, tests, covid-19 lockdowns and year twelve in general - they endured shooting in 5 degree weather on a cold thursday night, filming in weird laneways in enclosed streets and suffered through my endless "lets do it again" or "you're doing it wrong" (incredible direction on my behalf) just to get the perfect take
but you know what they say, you suffer for your art....i think that's the saying?

the film isn't necessarily of the high standard i anticipated from the beginning of the year or what my friends & family would expect from me - an intense cinephile & aspiring filmmaker - but after several re-writes, re-shoots, deleted scenes, fired extras and characters who were left on the cutting room floor, i was still fortunate enough to have something to submit as my vce media final for media units 3 & 4 at all and that's something to be proud of!

congratulations to all year 12 students submitting their art, vis-com and media finals (all year twelves too) - i hope everything turned out in your favour
