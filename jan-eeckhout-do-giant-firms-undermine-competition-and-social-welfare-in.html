But wages are stagnant and prices are rising. Economist Jan Eeckhout shows how this is due to a small number of companies taking advantage of new technologies to charge ever-higher prices. The consequences are already immense, from excessive prices to fewer emerging companies that can compete, to rising wage inequality for most workers.
