Steady prices of spot aluminium augur well for the company. Prices of both alumina and aluminium are likely to be supported by the continued outage at Jamalco refinery with a capacity of 1.4MTPA, rising input costs and discontinuation of bauxite exports by Indonesia in 2022.

These developments are positive for Nalco, which has built an integrated business model, has healthy cash kitty and an attractive dividend yield.

Analysts remain positive on the stock which could reach levels of Rs. 126 over the next few months.
