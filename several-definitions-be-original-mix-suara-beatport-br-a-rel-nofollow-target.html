Several Definitions is one of those artists to watch. Last year was amazing for this swiss producer, with releases in labels like 'Stir Von Talent',' Selador' or 'Suara' among many others. 'Be' sounds even bigger and massive but still raw, with trippy harmonies taken by Andre Kronert for his classic techno remix. Last but not least we have 'Shadow Trains', a track plenty of arpeggios and acid feelings. A complete and up-to-date package.
