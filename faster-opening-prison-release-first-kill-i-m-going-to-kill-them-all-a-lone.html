A lone man recently released from prison, seeks out the people from his past who he believes betrayed him and killed his brother.

Dwayne Johnson plays Driver, a man on a mission of vengeance who has just been released from a 10 year prison sentence. Shot in the back of the head, the bullet bounced off his skull, traveled around the side of his head and existed out his cheek. Refusing to die, a metal plate holds the back of his head together.
