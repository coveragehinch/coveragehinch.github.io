00:00 - The first method is to find it at Wangshu Inn and Liyue Harbor's.
00:24 - The second method is to buy it from Verr Goldet at Wangshu Inn and Ms. Bai in Qingce Village.
01:07 - The third method is by Gardening in the Serenitea Pot.
