Decades of waiting have finally led us to this moment. Now, we are only a month away from witnessing history.

That is if the JSWT (James Webb Space Telescope) does, in fact, launch. Fingers crossed that it can finally begin its mission and leave our earthly world behind. But why has it taken so long to launch? And why are we now sure that it will be launched this December? Let’s find out in this video.
