Follow along on this 14-day Iceland Itinerary. We'll be driving around the famous Ring road as well as exploring Iceland's majestic highlands via the country's mountain roads known as F-roads. The video will highlight the most essential places to visit during an Iceland road trip including volcanoes, glaciers, waterfalls, and much more.
