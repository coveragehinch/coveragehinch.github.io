Local surfer Kopral showed a great amount of patience setting up the barrel in the opening scene of this video. As soon as he dropped in, it was all about setting up the tube section that he knew would eventually present itself. It’s a real testament to his knowledge of this reef and ability to perfectly read the wave.

The SE trade wind hasn’t been as reliable as we’d expect for this time of the year, but the funky winds backed down enough for some smooth conditions for this session a few days ago.
