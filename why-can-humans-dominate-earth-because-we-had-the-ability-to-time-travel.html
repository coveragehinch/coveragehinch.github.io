After the ants climb up the twig, the chimpanzee would eat the ant lollipop. Monkeys use stones to break open coconuts. A wide range of animals are considered to use tools. Some may then say that we have language. But animals also exchange information, such as through movements, sounds, or smells. None of these answers unlock the key to human’s domination of the planet.
