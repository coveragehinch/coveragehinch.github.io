Back to basics! How and when to use definite articles (el, la, los, las) and indefinite articles (un, una, unos, unas). This animated explanation is a basic lesson for beginners: it starts with what articles are in English and then explains how to use them in Spanish. It includes the exception for feminine nouns that begin with a stressed "ah" sound.
