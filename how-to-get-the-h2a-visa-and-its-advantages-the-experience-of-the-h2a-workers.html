I will talk about the H-2A VISA temporary agricultural workers program, supported by former President George W. Bush, often called the H-2A visa program - this VISA helps employers who anticipate a lack of available domestic workers to bring foreign workers to the U.S. to perform temporary or seasonal agricultural work including, but not limited to, planting, cultivating, or harvesting labor.

Depending on the type of work you need, temporary or seasonal agricultural work can happen on farms, plantations, ranches, nurseries, greenhouses, orchards, or other similar locations.
