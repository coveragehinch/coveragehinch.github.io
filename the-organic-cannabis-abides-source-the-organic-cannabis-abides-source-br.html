Source exists to transform the world - elevating the human experience.

The Source story stems from a desire to experience and grow the highest quality cannabis products. From their focus on accountability, commitment to adaptation, and team first philosophy, Source is intentional about what they ingest and put out into the world. Their North Star is instilling compassion through the unwavering quality of their offerings, service, and ethos.
