Well, this changes today, because we got some news on their next generation of GPU’s. Heck this will likely also be true for the Future AMD gpu’s too.
So, this comes from 2 leakers over on twitter, Kopite 7 kimi and greymon 55. First, came the Pinned tweet last week from Kopite saying “ Rest in peace PSU, I dont want to leak details now, let’s wait and see.
