RAISED in a conservative Christian home, Jordan Bovee', from Minnesota, looks nothing like the stereotypical 'church girl'. Throughout her entire life, people have been trying to convince Jordan to try a different style but she has never done so - until now. Today, Jordan is going to step into her sister's shoes for a day as she undergoes an extreme beauty queen makeover. She will also be seeing Angela for the first time in over a year - will Angela recognise her sister in a dazzling evening gown with a tiara?
