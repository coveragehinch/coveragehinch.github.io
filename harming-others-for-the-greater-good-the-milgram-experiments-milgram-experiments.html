#Milgram #Experiments #Authority Professors Alex Haslam and Steve Reicher revisited the famous Milgram Experiments which have been used for decades to understand why people will do harm to others when told by an authority to do so. Their determination is that it's not just authority that matters, but someone's relationship to the authority and a belief that their actions are accomplishing a greater good.
