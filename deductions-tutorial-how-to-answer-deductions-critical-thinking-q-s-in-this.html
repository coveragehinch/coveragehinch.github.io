In this deductions tutorial, our expert test developer talks through how to answer an assumptions question from a critical thinking test.

Candidates will be provided with a passage of information and candidates will need to evaluate a list of deductions made based on that passage.

If one cannot deduce a particular statement from the passage, then that deduction does not follow, and the candidate must select which deductions follow and which do not follow.

The answer must be entirely based on the statements made and not on conclusions made from one’s own knowledge.
