Back on Harvest knocking out acres… slowly. If I need to fix ruts that would be the field to harvest in as of now. I needed a ride back to the combine (hence the bike)… but someone forgot to check tire psi before loading it up!

Also figured out that one field on the bottom farm seems to be half tilled and half not tilled. You can definitely tell where there’s tile. Insane difference in yield. I may try and get some tilling done this fall!
