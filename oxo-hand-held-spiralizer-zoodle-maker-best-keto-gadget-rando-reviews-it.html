It's February...how's your New Year's Resolution going? Well, one of the things people on the KETO diet do is remove carbs by replacing them with healthy alternatives. Then someone said "Hey, Let's make fake noodles using zuchinni!"

You've seen Spiralizers for a long time and frankly, they're dumb. I wanted to try some zoodles using a simple, small gadget that wouldn't take up much space in my kitchen cabinet or drawer. So, I bought the OXO Hand-Held Spiralizer.
