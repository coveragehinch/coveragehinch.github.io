Top 5 Garlic Tricks that Unclog Arteries, Lower Bad Cholesterol & Prevent Heart Attack

Garlic is one of the few foods in this world that’s literally called a “superfood”. That’s because it is super powerful! Not only is this a root that tastes great and can be used to add lots of flavor to otherwise flavorless dishes, but it’s also an ingredient that can, on its own, help better your cardiovascular health! The reason why Garlic is so good for health isn’t just something that people believe for no reason, there’s actually scientific evidence behind it!

The first reason is that Garlic is full of antioxidants, which we almost always need more of. All India Institute of Medical Sciences, did an entire study on Garlic as an antioxidant, and concluded that as long as the right amount of Garlic is being used, it can actually be great for heart health! A Study from the 2019 issue of Experimental and Therapeutic Medicine also showed that Garlic is great to lower blood pressure! Reducing the risk of cardiovascular disease by 16-40%! Surprisingly, another study from the 2016 issue of The Journal of Nutrition showed that garlic can help clean “soft plaque” from the arteries, keeping them clear and promoting healthy blood flow!
