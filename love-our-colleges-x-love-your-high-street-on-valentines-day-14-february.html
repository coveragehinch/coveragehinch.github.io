On Valentines Day, 14 February 2019, Studio Egret West, an Architecture and Urban Design company launched their exhibition - Love Our Colleges X Love Your High Street. The exhibition explored the potential benefits that colleges and local High Streets might enjoy through enhanced interrelationships.

At the launch, New City College students were on hand to showcase their talents with creative design students, computer games design students and catering students present. Part of the exhibition was this brilliant video created by the team at Studio Egret West.
