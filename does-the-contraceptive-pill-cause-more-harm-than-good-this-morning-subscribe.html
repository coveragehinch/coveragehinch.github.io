For more than 50 years, millions of women have relied on the convenience of the contraceptive pill but is it doing more harm than good? Dr Sarah E Hill has been researching the darker side of the hormone-based drug, publishing her findings in a new book, ‘How The Pill Changes Everything’. She says that not only can it impair the way you manage stressful situations but that it can also dampen your sex drive, changing who you’re physically attracted to, which she claims can even end your marriage. She’s here today to tell us about her research, alongside Dr Sara who’ll be sharing her contraceptive advice and the other options out there.
