(http://www.diopsys.com/) The Enfant® Pediatric VEP Vision Testing System uses advanced Visual Evoked Potential (VEP) technology to assess the function of the entire visual pathway. The Enfant® helps pediatricians and eye care specialists detect visual deficits such as optic nerve disorders, severe refractive errors, amblyopia and even retinoblastoma.
