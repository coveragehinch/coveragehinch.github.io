Kajsa Lundborg talks about invincible filter bubbles. It is hard to find different perspectives be when everything on your screen is customized for you.

Kajsa Lundborg is a young entrepreneur and tech fanatic that grew up in a family of programmers. Kajsa has founded three companies within the IT-sector, done lectures for over 15,000+ people and been awarded by Microsoft as one of Sweden’s female role models within technology.
