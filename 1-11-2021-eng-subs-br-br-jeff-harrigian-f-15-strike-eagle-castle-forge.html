Greek-Turkish relations.
GREECE. TURKEY. USA.
The commander of the US, European and African Air Force, Jeff Harrigian, paid an emergency visit to the Larissa military airport, where F-15 Strike Eagle fighter jets have been hosted for several days which are taking part in the Castle Forge exercise.
The visit took place on the day of OHI, that is, on October 28, thus giving it an even greater importance, in terms of relations between Greece and the United States.
During his stay, the US commander met and talked with the commander of the Hellenic Air Force, Lieutenant General Themistoklis Bourolias, who welcomed him warmly, while he also met with the US personnel, to whom he pointed out the importance of this exercise, in terms of gaining experience.
Of course, he also made special reference to the purposes of the exercise, where one of them is to enable an air unit to develop rapidly in the theater of the respective war conflict.
In any case, the visit of the US commander really surprised us and we hope that it was for a good reason.
