Dr Bob brings you SCP Foundation Keter Class object, SCP-3899 The Night Hauler Animation.

SCP 3899 is, in appearance, a black Peterbilt 379 semi trailer truck, with attached trailer. SCP3899 has performance capabilities that exceed what is possible for conventional trucks and also displays the ability to selectively displace itself in space across short distances.
