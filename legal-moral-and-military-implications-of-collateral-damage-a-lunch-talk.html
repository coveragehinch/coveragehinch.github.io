Legal, Moral, and Military Implications of “Collateral Damage”: A Lunch Talk with Prof. Janina Dill

April 11, 2018

Dr. Janina Dill discusses her research concerning international law and ethics in international relations, specifically in war. Her current work investigates the legal, moral, and military implications of “collateral damage,” with a specific focus on U.S. military practices. Dr. Dill is the John G. Winant Associate Professor in U.S. Foreign Policy at the University of Oxford and a Research Fellow at the Oxford Institute for Ethics, Law, and Armed Conflict.

Sponsored by the HLS Program on International Law and Armed Conflict (HLS PILAC), the National Security & Law Association, and the National Security Journal.
