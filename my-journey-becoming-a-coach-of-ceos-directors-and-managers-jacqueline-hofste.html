Jacqueline Hofste shares her story right from the start of her journey as a professional coach and being in business for herself, through to gaining the confidence to coach directors, CEOs and general managers. Not only that, but also making a deep, lasting and substantial difference to them as human beings and leaders through her coaching work.

What does it take to coach and support high caliber leaders to achieve their top performance? Jacqueline uncovers what made the difference to her when it comes to dealing with the challenges that can come up through the coaching process when you are working with leaders on this level.

When it comes to the leaders that she works with, Jacqueline speaks about how the Being Profile® is useful in its remarkable precision and ability to direct the coaching conversation to save time getting to the root of important matters for her clients.
