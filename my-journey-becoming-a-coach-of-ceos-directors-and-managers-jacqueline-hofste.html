Jacqueline Hofste shares her story right from the start of her journey as a professional coach and being in business for herself, through to gaining the confidence to coach directors, CEOs and general managers. What does it take to coach and support high caliber leaders to achieve their top performance? Jacqueline uncovers what made the difference to her when it comes to dealing with the challenges that can come up through the coaching process when you are working with leaders on this level.
