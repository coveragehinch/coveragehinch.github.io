Welcome to the first tutorial video on the Mention Boolean Operators! In this tutorial series, we will teach you how to utilize the diverse operators offered within the Mention Advanced Alerts. For this video, we will cover 4 boolean operators that focus on the keywords you monitor and the sources you will fetch within your alerts. These operators are Proximity, Wildcards, NEAR, and Source.
