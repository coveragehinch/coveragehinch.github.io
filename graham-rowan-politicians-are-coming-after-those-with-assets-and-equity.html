Wealth commentator and Beaufort Society Chairman, Graham Rowan, talks about wealth and assets in a post-Covid19 world, and how people need to think about their tax position and becoming a "global citizen". He believes that it is the wealthy with assets and equity who are going to pay for the Covid19 economic fall out, and warns that anyone with equity in their portfolio is at risk.
