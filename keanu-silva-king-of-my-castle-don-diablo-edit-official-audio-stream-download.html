"I just started making music when the original version of "King Of My Castle" by Whamdue Project got released. This immediately sparked a lot of creative ideas in my mind, so I started working on a new version in my studio. Since that day the track has become one of the most requested ID's in my sets and we are excited to finally share it with the world!" - Don Diablo
