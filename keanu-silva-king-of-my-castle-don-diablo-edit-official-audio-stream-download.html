This immediately sparked a lot of creative ideas in my mind, so I started working on a new version in my studio. We ended up sending a few versions back and forth and then finally, after testing it out more extensively, we agreed on a version that we both loved. Since that day the track has become one of the most requested ID's in my sets and we are excited to finally share it with the world!" - Don Diablo
