Izinyane leSilo uNhlanganiso kaZwelithini (Prince Nhlanganiso Zulu) and leading health NGO Isibaya samadoda are hosting a double protection event for men with a focus on COVID-19 vaccination and medical male circumcision.

Both traditional leadership and the Department of Health will be coming together to promote men's health.

SABC News journalist, Nozintombi Miya attended the event.
