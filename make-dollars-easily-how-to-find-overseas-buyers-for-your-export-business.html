When you’re starting off, you might be a little lost as how you can look to find buyers. Finding buyers for your products can be especially tricky task to do with all the competition out there 😕

If this is a problem you’ve faced, we have brought someone in who can tell you where to start!

Meet Ranjan Hanchapola 👋

He is the founder and CEO of CAP organic, the CEO and MD of CAP green forest, and a partner at CAP engineering.

Today, Ranjan is going to guide you through everything you need to know about 🤔 How you can find initial buyers for your business 🤔 How you can maintain a healthy buyer-seller relationship 🤔 What strategies you can use to negotiate terms and prices with your buyer 🤔 How you can build long-lasting credibility with your buyers.
