From picking between the Stormcloaks and Empire to deciding whether or not to spare Paarthurnax, The Elder Scrolls 5 is a game that affords the player a large amount of agency over its many narratives. These opportunities to exercise our own judgement are by far some of the most thought provoking and interesting moments in the entire world of Skyrim.
