If you are studying business or starting a business, then its important to know the role business plays and what business exist to do. Basically, what is the point of a business? There are many roles businesses play but for the purpose of this video we are going to concentrate on the 3 most fundamental. Which are: to produce goods or provide a service, to meet the needs of the customer and finally to add value.
