In the 3 hours we were there we found some Indian pennies, a 1700's Chinese coin, an early 1800's half cent, and much more; so we knew it would be a great location for a full day of detecting. Well, today's the day and we go back with 6 of us for a full day of detecting at this beautiful Colonial stone home to see what was dropped over the centuries.
