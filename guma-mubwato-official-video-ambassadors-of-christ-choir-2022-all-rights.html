We are living in troubled times, times when many people have given up hope in Christ, others have stopped coming to church or any fellowship with believers because of the pressures, doubts and problems of the world. BUT worst of all HOPE is gone, and many have given up their faith in God, and others are considering JUMPING SHIP, and casting themselves into the stormy waters, but the waters are infested with devouring reptiles.
