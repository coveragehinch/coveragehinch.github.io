We are living in troubled times, times when many people have given up hope in Christ, others have stopped coming to church or any fellowship with believers because of the pressures, doubts and problems of the world. This prevailing pandemic has not made matters any easier; Folks have lost jobs, businesses have collapsed, many have lost loved ones to Covid-19, and our entire social support system hangs in balance.
