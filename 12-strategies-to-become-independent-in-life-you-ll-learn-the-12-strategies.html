In the following 12 strategies of keeping control of your life in your hands to become more Independent and less co-dependent:

1. Overcome Your Fears
2. Put in Extra Efforts and Do it Yourself
3. Stay motivated
4. Monitor Your Emotions
5. Do not Look for Validation
6. Become Your Best Mentor
7. Build and Maintain Strong Perspective
8. Come Out of Your Comfort Zone
9. Spend Some Time Alone
10. Seek Help Only When Necessary
11. No One Else Will Do it For You
12. Learn to Stay Happy with What You Do



We are human beings with different needs from everyone else. As much as it is easy to get things done with others' help, it is also not good for our personal growth.
