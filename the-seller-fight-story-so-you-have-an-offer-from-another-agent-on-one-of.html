So you have an offer from another agent on one of your listings. What's the goal? Easy, it's to persuade that agent's buyer to pay maximum price.

How? One technique is by relaying a genuine, powerful story about how hard you battled to convince the sellers to counteroffer at such a crazy low price.

Remember, buyers worry that they if they keep negotiating they may be able to beat the seller down more. A strong seller fight story relayed to the buyer by their agent may head that off.
