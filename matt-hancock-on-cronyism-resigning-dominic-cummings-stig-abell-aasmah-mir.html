Matt Hancock was live on Times Radio, answering questions from Stig Abell and Aasmah Mir in a wide-ranging discussion.

The former health secretary apologised for his actions after CCTV was leaked of him breaking social distancing guidelines with an aide during the Covid pandemic.

Stig Abell pressed him on claims of cronyism, after Mr Hancock had been accused of giving lucrative government contracts to friends who had little experience in providing medical equipment.

And Matt Hancock certainly didn't hold back when asked to comment about Dominic Cummings and his role in government.
