A UFO observed in the skies of France: intriguing videos question Internet users
A video posted on social networks shows a particularly intriguing luminous phenomenon in the sky this Tuesday, November 9. This strange light activity is said to have been observed near the Mediterranean coast.

On social networks, testimonies have been pouring in since the observation by several people of a particularly strange luminous phenomenon in the sky, Tuesday, November 9. Several inhabitants of departments close to the Mediterranean observed and filmed this intriguing activity. The meteorology site Météo Gard - Hérault has posted several enigmatic videos on Twitter

A bright rectangle, several videos
We can observe a rectangular luminous shape wandering in the dark night. According to our fellow meteorologists, the event was observed from Montpellier, Cournonterral, Palavas-les-Flots or even Sète. Reports have even been reported from Andorra, Narbonne and Perpignan.

Drone, satellite, lasers ... On social networks, Internet users are developing theories to explain the phenomenon.

A UFO already identified in Montpellier in September
On Wednesday September 15, a strange light activity had also hit the headlines. During a stormy evening, lights were observed in the sky over Montpellier. These were lights coming from projectors near the Halle Tropisme, report our colleagues from France 3 .

On the other hand, the anomaly observed by several dozen people on Tuesday, November 9 is difficult to explain. A new file which should not fail to interest the experts of Geipan , a service of Cnes in Toulouse in charge of unidentified spatial phenomena.
