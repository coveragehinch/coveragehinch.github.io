Colin Mickow of the Second City Track Club placed 6th at the Chicago Marathon 2021 in 2:13:31.

At the time of the Chicago Marathon the selection criteria for the 2022 World Championships in Eugene hadn’t been released.

The following week USATF released the selection criteria for the Marathon which stated that the team would be selected by top placings at the Chicago Marathon, the Boston Marathon and the NYC Marathon (also - automatic selection for Galen Rupp finishing top 10 at the 2020 Olympics).

Upon learning the criteria, Colin was ranked the highest (after Rupp) based on placings at Boston and Chicago.

Colin and Coach Michael Lucchesi had to wait on the results of the NYC Marathon to see if Colin would still rank top 2 (after Rupp).

Only Kibet placed higher than Colin at the NYC Marathon meaning Colin had qualified for the World Championships.

We joined Colin for a long run, 9 months before the World Championships.
