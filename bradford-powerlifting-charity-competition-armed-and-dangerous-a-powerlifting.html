A powerlifting charity competition was held at max out gym in Bradford Allerton, The group is called armed and dangerous, they were competing to see who was the strongest from between them.
The main aim was to raise money for great ormond street hospital charity. The team raised a healthy sum of £1665. Big respect to the contenders and every one who came down to support the cause, we also attended to support the lads.
