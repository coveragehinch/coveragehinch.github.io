Aaron McKeown, Head of Security Engineering and Architecture, Xero

Xero, a global leading cloud accounting platform, completed their migration of 59 billion records to Amazon Web Services late last year. Twelve months on, find out what they’ve learned and how they’re evolving their security principles. And how they’ve been able to maintain the cultural shift that enabled them to deliver their public cloud security strategy in an agile environment.

Learning Objectives:
1: Learn how to make security components accessible to developers in agile environments.
2: Challenges are everywhere: which ones will be the toughest to face and overcome?
3: Why sticking to your principles is necessary in building your security practice.
