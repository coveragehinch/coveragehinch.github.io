A #bikecrash ed into a clothing store at Raavi Chettu Bazaar in #Khammam city on Monday. However, the CCTV footage came to light on Wednesday. At the time of incident, two women and a man were present in the shop. Three of them were in shock for some time as speeding bike came towards them.

But no one was injured including the biker in the mishap. The police seized two-wheeler and registered a case. They are suspecting that bike rammed into the cloth showroom following #brakefailure. Now, the visuals are going viral on social media.
