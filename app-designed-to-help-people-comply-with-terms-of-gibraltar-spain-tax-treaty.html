A new app designed to assist people who need to ensure they are complying with the terms of the Gibraltar-Spain Tax Treaty has been launched.

OVRNITE helps the user keep a count of the number of days and nights spent in one territory or the other.

OVRNITE's Owen Smith said keeping a good record will be very important, given the cross border nature of our everyday lives.

OVERNITE is now available to download from the ​Apple store and ​Google Play.
