Today Cilla and Maddy are going shopping, but they are also going to hide, you (Cilla and Maddy) can buy anything unless I find you. How this works is that one of the girls gets a minute to hide, once that minute is over the seeker gets five minutes to find the one that is hiding. The one that’s hiding can pick one item that they want to shop for, if they get found before the five minutes is up, they must put the item back. The only way they get to keep the item is if the seeker can’t find the person that is hiding within the five minutes. We played this shopping challenge at Target, and it is a very big store. It was very hard for the seeker to find the person that’s hiding. Cilla however wasn’t that lucky. She was either too slow or she didn’t hide good enough. Maddy was very lucky in this challenge and was able to buy something in both rounds. What do you think she bought? If you want to know what they were able to buy, you must watch this video until the end. We hope you enjoy this video as much as we enjoyed filming it.
