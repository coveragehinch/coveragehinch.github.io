 (18m) was adopted by my parents (62m & 51f) when I was young. I’ve never known my biological family, nor do I want to. I was 17 when my parents adopted their second child “Cai” (6m). My mother had to start working more and I had to start contributing to rent as she no longer made enough to cover it with the extra mouth to feed. I was a freelancer, so I didn’t have a steady income and wanted to save for uni, but over 80% of what I made went to my mother. I also had to share a room with Cai, and he was not easy to live with
