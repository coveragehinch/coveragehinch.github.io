My mother knew his mother before she passed and wanted to prevent him falling to the care system, which is commendable, but we couldn’t afford this new addition to the family. My mother had to start working more and I had to start contributing to rent as she no longer made enough to cover it with the extra mouth to feed. I was a freelancer, so I didn’t have a steady income and wanted to save for uni, but over 80% of what I made went to my mother. I also had to share a room with Cai, and he was not easy to live with
