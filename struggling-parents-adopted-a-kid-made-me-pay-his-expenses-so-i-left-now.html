My mother had to start working more and I had to start contributing to rent as she no longer made enough to cover it with the extra mouth to feed. I was a freelancer, so I didn’t have a steady income and wanted to save for uni, but over 80% of what I made went to my mother. I also had to share a room with Cai, and he was not easy to live with
