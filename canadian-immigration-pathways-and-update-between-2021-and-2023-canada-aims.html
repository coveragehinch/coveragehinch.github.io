Between 2021 and 2023, Canada aims to welcome over 1.2 million new permanent residents (PR) from across the world. To meet these targets, Canada offers several immigration pathways and has programs in place to allow newcomers to study, work, and settle in Canada.

If you’re thinking about moving to Canada, the first step in your journey will be to identify the immigration programs that are best suited for you. Should you apply under the Express Entry or Provincial Nominee Program? Or would you have a better chance of getting a PR after completing a study program in Canada?

Join Clemence Leveau-Vallier, Head of Marketing, Arrive, Daniel Levy, Senior and ABC, Campbell-Cohen as they take you through the latest immigration updates for the PR process, including the application backlog, expected processing timelines for new applicants, and what to do if your Confirmation of PR (CoPR) has expired. Whether you’re getting ready to apply to an immigration program or are awaiting a response on your application, this webinar will address commonly asked questions about immigration to Canada.

You’ll learn about:
Recent immigration updates for permanent residence
Temporary foreign worker permit process
Study permit and pathways to PR
General guidelines to help you pick the best immigration program for you
