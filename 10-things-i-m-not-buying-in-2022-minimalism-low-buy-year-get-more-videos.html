It's almost a new year! Can you believe it? Today, I want to share with you 10 things I'm not buying in 2022. I've ever set no buy goals for them, or low buy goals. Minimalism has made me look at a lot of things differently, and has resulted in quite a few things I stopped buying. So let's talk about some of the things I don't buy anymore.
