88 Fortunes remains an iconic slot brand and I have so say I LOVE how it continues to evolve with new versions such as this one, Emperor's Coins. The base game remains fundamentally the same where you win by landing the same symbol on at least 3 adjacent reels from left to right, starting the leftmost reel and to help, wild symbols may land on reels 2, 3 and 4.

The novelties of this version are two-fold: an improved Jackpot Feature and variable free games based on a collection play mechanic, where special coins may land on reel 5 and increase the number of free games for that particular bet option.

The Jackpot Feature has been changed and I love the changes! It remains randomly initiated by landing one or more wild symbols although now, there is no Mini and the number of wild symbols you land dictates what Jackpots you may be awarded:
1 or more wild symbols: May award the Minor.
2 or more wild symbols: May award the Major.
3 wild symbols: May award the Grand.

The Free Games Feature is initiated by landing bonus symbols in a winning combination where you are awarded the number of free games for that particular bet option. Additionally, whenever the number of Free Games reaches 18, the feature is also initiated! Retriggers are possible for 8 additional games and the free games meter resets to 8 for the particular bet option after it's been initiated. Just like in the original classic, all non-premium symbols are removed from the reels during the bonus. Slot Vultures, here's another game for you!
