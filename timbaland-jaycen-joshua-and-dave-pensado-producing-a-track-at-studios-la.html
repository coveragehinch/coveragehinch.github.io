Revisit the magical Studios La Fabrique for a second series with mixing dream-team Jaycen Joshua and Dave Pensado alongside producer Timbaland! With the Pro Tools session open, Jaycen goes through the elements to apply processing, perform edits, re-arrange parts, and even add new material recorded on location!
