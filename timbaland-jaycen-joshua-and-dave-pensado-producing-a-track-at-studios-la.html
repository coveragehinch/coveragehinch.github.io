Revisit the magical Studios La Fabrique for a second series with mixing dream-team Jaycen Joshua and Dave Pensado alongside producer Timbaland! They present an exciting work in progress and recount how the project began with the teenage artist and was developed under collaboration with producer Scott Storch and others. After listening to the rough mix, suggestions are voiced by the guest speakers and seminar attendees. With the Pro Tools session open, Jaycen goes through the elements to apply processing, perform edits, re-arrange parts, and even add new material recorded on location!
