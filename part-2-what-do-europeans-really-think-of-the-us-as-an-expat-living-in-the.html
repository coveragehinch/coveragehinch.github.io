Everything from education in the US to health insurance to what we eat and how loud we can be! You know...I really can't disagree with 95% of everything that was said. I think like any where else it depends who you talk to. I don't think it's really Europe vs the US or living in the Netherlands is the end all beat all. I just think we have found a home here in the Netherlands and our love foe it continues to grow...and we're happy here!
