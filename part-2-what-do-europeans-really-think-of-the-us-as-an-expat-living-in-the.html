As an expat living in the Netherlands / Europe you start to realize how Europeans really feel about the US...it's interesting! I think like any where else it depends who you talk to. I don't think it's really Europe vs the US or living in the Netherlands is the end all beat all. I just think we have found a home here in the Netherlands and our love foe it continues to grow...and we're happy here!
