Did you know an Australian road train is a massive truck that can be up to 63m long with up to 4 trailers and over 100 tyres?
In this video we take you up close and personal with one of these incredible machines.

After that we head to the bush to tour Pyramid Station. A station is similar to a ranch. These enormous properties can run cattle or sheep and in some cases are larger than the smallest countries.
They can be millions of acres in size and graze thousands of cattle.

We have the opportunity to hang out with the caretakers of Pyramid and learn a little about station life whilst spending time on this beautiful land.

This video brings an end to our travels in the Pilbara region of Western Australia. Stay tuned though because the adventures are far from over 😉
