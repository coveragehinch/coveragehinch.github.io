Warren Buffett is interviewed by Charlie Rose. This is one of the best conversations there have ever been with the legendary investor. Mr. Buffett tells about the Rise of Berkshire Hathaway and shares his investment strategy & business principles he uses when buying or selling stocks.

He is considered one of the most successful investors in the world and has a net worth over 100 billion dollars. Buffett was born in Omaha, Nebraska. He developed an interest in business and investing in his youth and made truly incredible stock market returns over his career.
