We've reached the end of 2021, and it's time to reminisce about all the great indie folk songs released this year. Most of the songs focus on love and finding beauty in life and they have a certain dreamy and atmospheric feel to them, making them perfect for escaping daily tension and rush.

The lyrics of the first ten songs are displayed in the video, as well as the meanings of each song, throughout the entire video, as the song begins.⁠ We hope to include all lyrics from all songs in our videos in the future and can use all your support to make this happen.
