“We Met in Tokyo” is the title of the debut EP by Substantial and Funky DL. “We Met in Tokyo (A Mini Doc)” is an eight-minute documentary film where Funky DL and Substantial reflect on how they first met after working with the late Japanese producer, Nujabes. The two veteran emcees discuss their individual careers, the music they’ve created, and their latest project.

Directed and edited by Rachelle Etienne-Robinson
