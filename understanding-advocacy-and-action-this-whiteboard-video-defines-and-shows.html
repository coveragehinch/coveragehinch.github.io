This whiteboard video defines and shows the power of advocacy and action. By incorporating and infusing philanthropy education and service-learning into regular curriculum students learn there are many ways to make a difference; and that they have the strength and power to give time, talent, or treasure for the common good.

Discussion Prompt:
1. What does it mean to advocate for a cause?
2. Provide an example of doing research as a form of advocacy.
3. Talk about how action for the common good is good for all.
