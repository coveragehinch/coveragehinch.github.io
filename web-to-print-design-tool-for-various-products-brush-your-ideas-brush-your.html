Brush Your Ideas is an HTML5 Product Designer Tool to facilitate customized printing on any printable surface. This Web-to-Print software will help you to carve a niche for your business and raise the profits for your online store by offering personalization. Take a look at some of the features that our extension comes with:

1. Multiple Printing Methods
2. Responsiveness on any device
3. Printable in various formats
4. Clipart library of more than 1000 images
5. Texts, images and cliparts to inspire

Brush Your Ideas - Web-to-Print Design Tool is currently compatible with Magento and WordPress. The development is in progress for Shopify, OpenCart, Prestashop and CorePHP.

Let your customers be in charge of their creations through Brush Your Ideas. They can also make the most out of the ‘Print Color Management’ tool to choose colours, design, text and more. Enhance the creative edge of your customers by investing into - Brush Your Ideas.

Know more about features at https://www.brushyourideas.com
