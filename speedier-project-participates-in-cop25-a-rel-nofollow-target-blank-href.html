On December, 11, the SPEEDIER Project had the great opportunity to present its objectives and progress at the COP 25 hosted in Madrid (Spain).

Mariana Fernández, Communications Team Manager at Sustainable Innovations Europe (SIE) was the person in charge of presenting the speakers of the day: Jo Southernwood, Energy research engineer and consultant at IERC – International Energy Research Centre and coordinator of the SPEEDIER project; Germán López Lara, Energy and Environmental Technical Officer at CTA (Technological Corporation of Andalusia), and Licinio Alfaro, Head of Department of Sustainable Construction at ITeC – Institut de Tecnologia de la Construcció de Catalunya.

Jo provided a general overview of SPEEDIER, German spoke about the reasons behind the need for the SPEEDIER service and the framework the project is part of and, for his part, Licinio spoke about the tool that will be implemented within the project life.

This 2.5 year project aims to address the barriers faced by Small & Medium Enterprises (SMEs) that prevent them from undertaking energy audits and implementing the recommended energy-saving measures.
