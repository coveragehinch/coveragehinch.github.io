Crockpot Garlic Parmesan Chicken Pasta Recipe:
3-4 Chicken Breasts
8 ounce bottle of Buffalo Wild Wings Garlic Parmesan Sauce
8 ounces heavy whipping cream
8 ounces cream cheese
1/2 cup shredded parmesan
16 ounces of pasta

Dump all into the crockpot (except pasta) and cook on low for 6-8 hours
Shred Chicken and put back into crockpot
cook pasta and then mix in crockpot with chicken
add a little extra parmesan on top and serve
