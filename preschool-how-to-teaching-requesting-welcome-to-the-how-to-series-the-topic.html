Welcome to the How To series. The topic for this How To is teaching requesting. Teaching requesting is an important skill for students with autism so that they can get their wants and needs met. Some of the children you will see in the video will not have a diagnosis of autism, however the examples all depict strategies and teaching practices that can benefit all students.
