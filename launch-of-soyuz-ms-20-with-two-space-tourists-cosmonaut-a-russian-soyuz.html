A Russian Soyuz 2-1A rocket has successfully launched the manned Soyuz MS-20 capsule into orbit tomorrow morning at 02:38 Eastern, 07:38 UTC.

Onboard is Russian cosmonaut Alexander Misurkin and Japanese Space tourists Yusaku Maezawa and Yozo Hirano travelling via Space Adventures. The trio will spend 12 days in orbit and at the International Space Station before landing back on Earth on December 20th.
