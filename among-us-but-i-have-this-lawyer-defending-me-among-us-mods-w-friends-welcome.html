Welcome to Among Us! It's a simple deception game. As crewmates, we have to complete a set of tasks before the impostors can win. Once a body has been discovered or an emergency meeting is called, we can discuss our accounts of what happened. Then we try to vote the likely impostors off the ship. This game is SO MUCH FUN.
