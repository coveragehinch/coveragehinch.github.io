How to use colours?
There are seven days in a week, and each is allocated to a respective planet which further represents a colour. These colours are manifested/used in the form of clothes, accessories and gemstones, etc. As per a common belief, we simply wear colour on its respective day and expect results.

Wrong! The colour theory works slightly differently as explained in this video.

So, try to wear any colour of any planet on its day and in its specific Hora.
