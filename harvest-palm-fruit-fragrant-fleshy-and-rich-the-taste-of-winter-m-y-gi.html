Forest palm trees, they are planted around the house to serve daily life. Their leaves can be used for roofing or house cleaning tools. The trunk of the palm tree can be cut into segments and used as a breeding ground for honey bees. Their young core is sweet and delicious to eat. Palm trees flower in spring and it takes nine months for the palm fruit to ripen. They are used a lot in our culinary culture. Basically, they are very useful plants...
