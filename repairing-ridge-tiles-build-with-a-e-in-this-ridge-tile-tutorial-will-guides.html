In this ridge tile tutorial, Will guides us through his step by step process of conducting roof ridge repairs. He shows you how to repair ridge tiles and begins with making sure the ridges are clear of any old muck and moss. After, he reveals the tools and materials needed for the roof repair, as well as how the cement should look. Watch for tips, tricks and further information on how to re-bed ridge tiles.
