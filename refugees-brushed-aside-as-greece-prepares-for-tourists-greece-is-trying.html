Greece is trying to get tens of thousands of refugees off the streets before tourists arrive for summer.

It is building new camps on Athens' outskirts to house them. Tourism is key for the country's economic recovery but there are worries that the plight of asylum seekers are being ignored.

Al Jazeera’s Tony Birtley reports.
