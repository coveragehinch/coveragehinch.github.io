Painting Kitchen Cabinets DIY - How to paint kitchen cabinets with a sprayer, DIY edition! Painting kitchen cabinets can be intimidating for sure - Painting cabinets with a sprayer is the way to go to get a professional look and a durable finish. Using a paint sprayer on your kitchen cabinets it not as difficult as it seems, with a few easy preparations, you'll have your kitchen remodel looking fabulous in no time! Want to update those kitchen door knobs too? I'll show you the best method I've found to prevent chips in the paint and make your updated kitchen hardware last until it's time for a new style.

This video is the second episode in this Kitchen Makeover Series! Check back for more kitchen tutorials including a huge pantry makeover!
