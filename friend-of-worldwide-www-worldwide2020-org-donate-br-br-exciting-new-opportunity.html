Exciting new opportunity to be part of Bangor Worldwide Missionary Convention!Would you prayerfully consider becoming a ‘Friend of Worldwide’ and contribute monthly to support the Convention, which can cost more than £30K to put on each year (this does not include gifts which are allocated for and go direct to missions and missionaries).

Contributing £5, £10, £15 or an amount of your choice monthly would enable this ministry to continue. Should we find ourselves in the position of having all costs covered, excess gifts will go to missionaries/mission agencies.

Those who sign up as Friends:
Will receive our monthly prayer update by email – please make sure we have your e mail address
Invitations to special events
Pre-booking for the opening night with reserved seats.

Those who sign up for a minimum of £5 per month before 31st August 2020 will receive a free copy of Gary Millar’s book - Need to Know – Your Guide to the Christian Life (RRP £6.99)

We would be more than happy to answer any questions you may have before deciding. However, should you wish to proceed, please click this link. www.worldwide2020.org/donate
