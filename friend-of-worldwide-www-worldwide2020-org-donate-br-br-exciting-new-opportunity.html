Contributing £5, £10, £15 or an amount of your choice monthly would enable this ministry to continue. Should we find ourselves in the position of having all costs covered, excess gifts will go to missionaries/mission agencies.

Those who sign up as Friends:
Will receive our monthly prayer update by email – please make sure we have your e mail address
Invitations to special events
