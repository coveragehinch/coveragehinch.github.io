Coverage from all five Strongman events at the 2021 Rogue Invitational, as re-broadcast on the CBS Sports Spectacular. The competition took place October 29-30 at Dell Diamond in Round Rock, Texas, and featured 10 of the most accomplished Strongman athletes from around the world, including Martins Licis (USA), Mateusz Kieliszkowski (Poland), the Stoltman brothers (Scotland), Jean-Francois Caron (Canada), and Oleksii Novikov (Ukraine).



The CBS broadcast includes extended highlights from all five events over the two days of competition: the Elephant Bar Deadlift, Cyr Dumbbell Challenge, the Wheel of Pain, Timber Yoke and Log Medley, and Stones Over Hitching Post.

For additional highlights, final leaderboards, and more from the 2021 Rogue Invitational, visit https://www.roguefitness.com/invitational
