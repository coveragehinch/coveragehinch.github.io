Queen Elizabeth II's health has been a major concern in recent weeks, with much speculation centered around her overnight hospital stay and photographs of her walking with a cane. But what about the health of her son, Prince Charles? He may soon become the monarch, after all, and the oldest-ever to take on the role. The longest-serving British heir apparent recently turned 73. In November 2021, the Prince of Wales and his wife Camilla, Duchess of Cornwall, embarked on their first post-pandemic foreign tour. Pictures from their trip are provoking concern from many royal watchers. Here's why we're seriously concerned about Prince Charles' health.
