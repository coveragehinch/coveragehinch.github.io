Cecilia is diagnosed with schizophrenia and was first featured on SBSK three years ago. While filming she was symptomatic. Since then, she has worked hard to learn new coping skills and improve her mental health. Today, Cecilia is the founder of an organization named @Students With Psychosis that is helping people around the world who experience psychosis.

This was filmed in 2020 as part of our "How Are You?" series.
