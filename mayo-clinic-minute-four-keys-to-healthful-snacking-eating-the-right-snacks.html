Eating the right snacks at the right time could be the key to better managing your weight. Margaret Brown, a registered dietician with Mayo Clinic, offers four keys to healthful snacking.

In this Mayo Clinic Minute, Brown explains why knowing when and when not to eat, how long to go between meals and snacks, what kinds of snacks to eat, and how much water to drink can make it easier to lose weight or maintain a healthy weight. Ian Roth reports.
