One Thing is a song written by our very own Tishu (Addishiwot Feleke).

This song came from a place of emptiness and desperation to know God more and to stay in His presence. Out of everything in life, the only one who can satisfy us is Him. One thing we should seek and long for is to constantly dwell in Him and Him alone!

This is our everyday prayer, that we may reside in Him!
