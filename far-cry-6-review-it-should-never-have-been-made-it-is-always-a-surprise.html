(it's a horrendous, preachy, woke, incoherent abomination, with terrible combat, ridiculous weapon modelling, cliched mechanics, tiresome activities, obnoxious characters, a dreadful narrative, all cobbled together by the lowest outsourced bidders, using drool and chewing gum.
