It is always a surprise when a video game over-delivers, and Far Cry 6 disappoints in spades. It had an interesting core premise, but sadly the games ambitions were frustrated by minor setbacks. (it's a horrendous, preachy, woke, incoherent abomination, with terrible combat, ridiculous weapon modelling, cliched mechanics, tiresome activities, obnoxious characters, a dreadful narrative, all cobbled together by the lowest outsourced bidders, using drool and chewing gum.
