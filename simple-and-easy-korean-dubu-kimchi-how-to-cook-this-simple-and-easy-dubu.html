This simple and easy DUBU KIMCHI is a stir-fry kimchi and pork serve with sliced steamed, boiled or fried tofu. It is flavorful and tasty that makes all your love ones like it much.

DUBU KIMCHI
INGREDIENTS:
1 pack hard tofu slice into bite size
1/2 kg thinly sliced pork belly
2 tablespoon soy sauce
2 tablespoon sesame oil
1 teaspoon ground pepper
1 tablespoon minced ginger
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 pc. thinly sliced onion
1 tablespoon white sugar
2 tablespoon thinly sliced spring onion
2 and 1/2 cup kimchi
Also can add pepper paste and chili paste for your own preferences.
