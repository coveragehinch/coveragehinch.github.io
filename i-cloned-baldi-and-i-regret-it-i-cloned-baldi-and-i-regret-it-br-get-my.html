This game is not what it seems...
Inspired by creepy/bad edutainment games from the 90s (Sonic's Schoolhouse, I.M. Collect all 7 of the notebooks and escape the school, all while avoiding Baldi. Baldi has many friends in the school that will slow down your progress and put you at risk of being caught, so learn how each character works and how you can avoid their attention.
