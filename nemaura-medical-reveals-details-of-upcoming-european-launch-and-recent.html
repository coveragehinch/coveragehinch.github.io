Nemaura Medical Inc (NASDAQ:NMRD) Vice President of Strategy & Strategic Alliances Dr Fred Schaebsdau tells Proactive the medical technology company is targeting a commercial launch for its flagship SugarBEAT continuous glucose monitor in the UK and in Ireland in January 2020.

Dr Schaebsdau adds that recently, the Nemaura team presented positive feedback from its first patient-user experiences at the 55th Annual Meeting of the European Association for the Study of Diabetes.
