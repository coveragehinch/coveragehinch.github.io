Feng Li graduated from the China Academy of Art in 2016 and stayed on as a teacher at the School of Chinese Painting and Calligraphy. He was determined to become a calligrapher since he was very young. Now at CAA, Feng not only plays the role of calligraphy teacher for his students, but also acts as their life mentor. He has a steadfast determination to preserve traditional Chinese calligraphy.
