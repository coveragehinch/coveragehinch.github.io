/ˈin(t)ərvəl/
noun
1.

“after his departure, there was an interval of many years without any meetings”
synonyms: interim, interlude, intervening time, intervening period, meantime, meanwhile;
interregnum

synonyms: intermission, interlude, entr’acte, break, recess, pause, gap; More

“United led 3-0 at the interval”
