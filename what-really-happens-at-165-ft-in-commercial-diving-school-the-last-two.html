The last two weeks of training and certification to be a commercial diver at CDA Technical Institute take place at our Deep Water Training Facility in Ocala, Florida. This is when the students get a chance to experience a real world work environment and test out what it is like to really go deep water diving in a dry suit. While we would like to claim that it is all hard work to be a commercial diver we wanted to show you what really happens at 165 ft at the CDA Deep Water Training Center in Ocala, Florida!
