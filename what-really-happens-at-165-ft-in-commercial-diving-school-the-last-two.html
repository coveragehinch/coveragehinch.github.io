The last two weeks of training and certification to be a commercial diver at CDA Technical Institute take place at our Deep Water Training Facility in Ocala, Florida. While we would like to claim that it is all hard work to be a commercial diver we wanted to show you what really happens at 165 ft at the CDA Deep Water Training Center in Ocala, Florida!
