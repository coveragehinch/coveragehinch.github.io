After a year of dropping heavy video parts, winning X Games medals and being crowned “Rider Of The Year”, Felix Prangenberg delivers one hell of an encore performance to end 2021 with a bang.

One of the strongest work ethics in BMX, Felix got to work on filming his first Monster Energy part from the day he joined the team. Filmed across Europe during the course of 2021, URGE delivers some of Felix’s most progressive and original riding to date.
