The world of Open Source brings a sense of freedom, to express, develop and propagate ideas. Watch as our speaker Neel Gala puts forth the benefits of an Open Source approach to Customized Hardware, manifested in the form of the Shakti Processor Project which gave birth to India's first 64-bit microprocessor.

The move to Open-Source technology extends the dimensions of flexibility and customization while increasing its accessibility. The contributions made in this endeavor could significantly reduce India's electronic imports dependency.
