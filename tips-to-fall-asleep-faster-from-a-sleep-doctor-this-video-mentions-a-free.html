How can I fall asleep faster? Can you learn to fall asleep faster? From eating dinner to setting that morning alarm, clinical psychologist Dr. Shelby Harris gives her top sleep tips to fall asleep quicker at night.

Have you tried any of these better-sleep hacks?
1: Wake up at the same time every day (even on weekends).
2: Limit your screen time, especially late at night.
3: Choose a (screen-free) wind-down activity like reading or journaling, and do that before bed.
4: Limit rigorous exercise and food three hours before bed.
5: Meditate during the day.
