In this video, we will explore What is Checklist.
Mistakes are common in our personal and professional life, so if you want to reduce them then you can use this simple, but effective support tool.

Checklist is a type of job aid used to reduce failure by compensating for potential limits of human memory and attention.
It helps to ensure consistency while carrying out a task and making sure that nothing important is skipped.
A basic example of a checklist is your daily to-do list that you prepare to ensure that you do all the things that need to get done.

Third - Professional diving - checklists are used in the preparation of equipment for a dive, and to ensure that the diver and life support systems are fully prepared before they enter the water.
These are all serious operations and they rely on the checklists to help them do their job and avoid potential errors.
