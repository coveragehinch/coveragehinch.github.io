Mistakes are common in our personal and professional life, so if you want to reduce them then you can use this simple, but effective support tool.

Checklist is a type of job aid used to reduce failure by compensating for potential limits of human memory and attention.

A basic example of a checklist is your daily to-do list that you prepare to ensure that you do all the things that need to get done.
