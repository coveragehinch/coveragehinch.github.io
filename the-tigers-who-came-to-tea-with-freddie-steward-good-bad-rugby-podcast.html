Ellis Genge brings his mate Freddie Steward to meet Alex and Tinds this week. They celebrate Freddie’s sensational player of the match performances against both Australia and South Africa, and hear how his feet are firmly grounded living in student digs in Loughborough, listening to 80s disco.

They also get into Leicester Tigers’ phenomenal start to the season, the culture of the club and Ellis’ leadership.
