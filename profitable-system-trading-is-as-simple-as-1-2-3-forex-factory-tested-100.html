Indicators
Trade Session Indicator

Long Entry
Entries are only in the direction toward the daily open.
Wait for a candle to make a new dialy low.
Enter when price retraces above bar that made the daily low, as long as that candle did not make a new daily low.

Short Entry
Entries are only in the direction toward the daily open.
Wait for a candle to make a new dialy high.
Enter when price retraces below bar that made the daily high, as long as that candle did not make a new daily high.

R:R
3:1

Where to place stop-loss
Shorts - Above candle making new daily high (minimum 5 pips)
Longs - Below candle making new daily low (5 pip minimum)
