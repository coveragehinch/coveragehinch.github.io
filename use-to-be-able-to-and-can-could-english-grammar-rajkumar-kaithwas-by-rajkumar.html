Watch this video and solve the sentences.

1) He can run fast.
2) She could swim well .
3)They can manage it.
4) We could do something for them.
5) Rohan cannot pass the examination.
6) Police could not catch the thieves.
7) Even God cannot save them .

9)Can you read it.
10) Could he make it?

12) How can he stop it .
13) When can you afford the things?
