We need to be wise during these unsure times and have food, water, and back up heat sources in your home. Still live life, but we also need to be watching what is going on around the world. A new variant, social unrest, talks of possible war, are all reasons to get prepared. But ALSO, if there is a personal job loss, or a bad winter weather wise. If you are prepared with a prepper pantry of food, have water and/or water filter, and a source of heat gives a sense of peace.
