The first year of the DISTINGUISHED GENTLEMAN'S DRIVE has been run around the world, however due to Lockdowns, Sydney and Wollongong DGD was run last weekend.

I started in with the Wollongong Distinguished Gentleman's Drive at the Australian Motorlife Museum https://www.australianmotorlifemuseum.com/

We drove up to Stanwell Park to the Bald Hill Lookout, from here we jumped onto the Sydney Distinguished Gentleman's Drive travelling to the Southern Highlands to have a delicious lunch at the beautiful Centennial Vineyards in Bowral https://www.centennial.net.au/

The DGD is not only a fantastic event, it also raises funds for MOVEMBER
https://au.movember.com/
