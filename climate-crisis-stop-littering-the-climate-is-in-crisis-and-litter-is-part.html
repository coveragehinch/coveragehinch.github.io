The climate is in crisis and litter is part of the problem – harmful waste such as chemicals and plastics are spread on the ground and in lakes and seas.

If we don’t do anything about it, our oceans could end up containing more plastic than fish by the year 2050! But you and other children and young people around the world can make a difference and become the No Litter Generation. Watch and share this animated video to be inspired for change!

Climate Action and No Litter Generation is an initiative from the World’s Children’s Prize (WCP) and Håll Sverige Rent (Keep Sweden Tidy). with support from the Swedish Postcode Lottery.
