Imagine being ill with a very horrible disease, one which either no one has even heard of or else they have very wrong ideas concerning it. The latter is most often the case. This brief video is to shine light on the many wrong beliefs surrounding ME/CFS and to respond with the truth from the experts, the researchers and clinicians who have studied this disease.
