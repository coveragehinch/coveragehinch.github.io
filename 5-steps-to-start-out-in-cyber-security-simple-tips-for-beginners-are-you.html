I am Venicia, founder of Cyber Queen, and I help you with basic tips and guidance to succeed in cyber security.

In this video, I share the 5 steps that helped me start my cyber security career. 👩🏽‍💻

1. Do "Focussed" Research - the cyber security field is huge, do your research to understand the options available to you. ✍🏽

2. Choose a focus domain - Chose a focus domain early on, this can help save you time and money.
These resources can help you understand the different domains in cyber security 👇🏾
ISC2: https://www.isc2.org/Certifications/CISSP/Domain-Refresh-FAQ
NIST NICE Framework: https://niccs.cisa.gov/workforce-development/cyber-security-workforce-framework

3. Start with Free Training Options first - Check out my guide for my recommended free and low-cost training options. 📚
Guide: https://www.cyberqueen.org/post/free-and-low-cost-cyber-security-training-for-beginners

4. Practise your skills - while Degree and certification programs are required and help you get the relevant knowledge and accreditation, you still require hands-on demonstratable skills in order to do the job. 🛠

5. Find a Coach or Mentor - This has been the most influential step in my personal journey!👩🏽‍🏫
