In a thriller that went down to the last shot, Zenit St Petersburg recorded its third consecutive win by downing Maccabi Playtika Tel Aviv 73-71 at Sibur Arena on Thursday. Alex Poythress led the winners with 14 points. Billy Baron added 12, including the game-winning three-pointers, while Arturad Gudaitis and Dmitry Kulagin each had 10 for Zenit. Jalen Reynolds led Maccabi with 24 points and 11 rebounds. Derrick Williams added 14 points for the guests.
