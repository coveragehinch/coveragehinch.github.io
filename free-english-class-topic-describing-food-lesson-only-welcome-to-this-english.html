Welcome to this English lesson where I'll help you learn how to describe food. If you haven't noticed, food is all around us and there is so much variety. Describing food in English requires quite a bit of vocabulary. In this free English class I won't be teaching the names of different food as I'm sure you know most of those already, but instead I'll help you learn words and phrase that you can use to talk about those foods when you have an English conversation.

In this English lesson you'll learn words and phrases like, crispy, crunchy, bland, undercooked, burnt, sticky, and more!

Remember this is much more than a simple vocabulary lesson. It is important to listen to the words and phrases I use as I talk about each word as you'll be able to pick up many common English expressions along the way.

I hope you enjoy this English lesson about describing food!
