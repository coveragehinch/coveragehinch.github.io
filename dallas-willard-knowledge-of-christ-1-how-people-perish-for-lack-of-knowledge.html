#DallasWillard is known for saying, “Reality is what you run into when you’re wrong.” That’s good for a laugh, but what does it mean? In this session you will learn that knowledge is the key to being able to avoid “running into” reality. You’ll also learn:
- the difference between knowledge, truth, belief and commitment,
- the Four Great Questions of Life,
- how lack of knowledge affects our worldview and our education system,
- the gospel of Jesus in this context.

Key Scriptures: Hosea 4:6; Daniel 12:4; John 17:3; 2 Peter 3:18
