There’s hope when your congregants’ eyes start to glaze over! You can help your church members get all they can from the message. You’ll get step-by-step examples to help your congregation follow the text’s flow of thought and discover the Bible’s deep truths. Pastor Lutzer offer’s a method to make your sermon preparation simpler, as well as insights from his years of experience developing and delivering messages.
