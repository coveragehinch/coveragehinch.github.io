We form the Past Simple Tense and the Past Participle of Regular Verbs by adding -ed at the end of the infinitive. We use the Past Participle to form all Simple Perfect Tenses in English and the Passive forms of the verbs (Passive Voice). Sometimes, some Past Participles of certain verbs can be used as adjectives before nouns.
