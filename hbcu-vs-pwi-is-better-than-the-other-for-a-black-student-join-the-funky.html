However, in the midst of the push, discord and division have also been sewn between Black people who attend/attended HBCU's and those who attend/attended PWI's. At the very end of the day, a Black person with a degree is a good thing, not matter where the hell you got it from. Let's discuss some of the pros and cons of both types of institutions and the factors that lead students to their respective higher education destination.
