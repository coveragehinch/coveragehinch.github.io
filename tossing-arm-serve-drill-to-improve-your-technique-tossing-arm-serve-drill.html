Tossing Arm Serve Drill To Improve Your Technique
In this serve training video, I share a powerful drill to improve your tossing arm and off hand position, two essential components for developing an elite tennis serve technique.

🎾🔥 Tossing Arm Serve Drill Step By Step 📖:

✔️ 1- Slide your off arm up the fence
✔️ 2- Reach as high as you can and then side bend
✔️ 3- Get the racquet vertical into the trophy position
✔️ 4- Spread your fingers and get the off hand facing towards the side fence
✔️ 5- Hold this position for 20-30 seconds
