Shridhar Chillal from Pune City, India had grown his nails for 52 years before finally getting them cut off and stored in the Ripley's Believe It Or Not! museums in New York City and Amsterdam.

At the time of measurement when his record was confirmed in 2014, Shridhar's nails measured: Little finger - 179.1cm, ring finger - 181.6cm, middle finger - 186.6cm, index finger - 164.5cm, thumb - 197.8cm.

We tell the story of Shridhar, from why he began to grow his nails to how living with them throughout his life has changed and what obstacles he has had to overcome.
