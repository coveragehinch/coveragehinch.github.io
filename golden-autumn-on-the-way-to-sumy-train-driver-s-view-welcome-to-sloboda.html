Welcome to Sloboda Ukraine (or Slobozhanshchyna)! Is is a historical region, now located in Northeastern Ukraine. Meet one of the most atmospheric railway lines on diesel traction of eastern Ukraine - Kharkiv - Sumy - Vorozhba railway. There is a whole mix of quite unique things here: searchlight signals, manual switches at stations, and all this on a double-track diesel railway (of which there are very few left in Ukraine) with beautiful nature and very measured atmosphere, which is difficult to describe. In this part we travel from Trostyanets-Smorodyne station to Sumy station - the regional center of Sumy region in Ukraine.
