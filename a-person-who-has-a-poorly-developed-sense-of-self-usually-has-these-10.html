People that have a poorly developed sense of self don't even know that they have such issues; rather, they are comfortable with the way their life is. They tell you, they don't want to stress themselves much in this life because they were not born to come and suffer. Some of them feel that they owe it to another person for their happiness, so they derive happiness in doing things for other people, thereby forgetting to take care of themselves and their needs.

The following are ten signs that you have a poorly developed sense of self, watch this video till the end, and if you find that you notice any of these signs in your life, then you sure need to develop your sense of self.
