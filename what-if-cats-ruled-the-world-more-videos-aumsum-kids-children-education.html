Fourthly, Dogs and Cats generally do not get along, if Cats ruled the World, dogs may have to move back to the jungle.
Fifthly, cats are known for their cleanliness, if Cats ruled the World, we may have to keep everything super clean.
Lastly, if Cats ruled the World, some people may start dressing and acting like Cats in order to get more benefits.
