Tesla Giga Berlin chronicler Tobias Lindh yesterday spotted the first Model Y Performance at Giga Berlin with a made-in-Germany VIN number.

"I saw a Model Y Performance today at #GigaBerlin. I've had a look at the VIN. It is made in Germany," Tobias wrote on Twitter sharing some images.

Keep in mind that the existence of a VIN from Giga Berlin and a license plate don't imply that this Model Y Performance is production unit, rather than a test unit. Tesla can't sell this vehicle. Also, this doesn't imply that the actual mass-production has started at Giga Berlin.

Some people in Germany state that they expect their Model Y EV crossovers to come in 2 weeks. Does this mean they are coming from Giga Berlin or Giga Shanghai from China?

Tobias says "I could imagine that Tesla hopes to be able to deliver from Giga Berlin in 2 weeks. Does anyone know whether ships from China are already sailing to Europe with performance models? They also need more than two weeks to get here."
