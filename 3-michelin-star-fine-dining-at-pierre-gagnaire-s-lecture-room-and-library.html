Full fine dining experience at the Lecture Room and Library in Sketch, London.

Always at the top of any best restaurants in London list, this is one of only 7 three Michelin star restaurants in the UK and one of the world's best restaurants.

The restaurant is hidden away from the other three busy restaurants in Sketch - The Parlour, The Glade and The Gallery. Once upstairs, you're made to feel like royalty, with your every, tiny need catered for by the waiting team and the food taken care of by head chef Johannes Nuding.

Everything from the faultless service, incredible food and breathtaking interiors make it one of the most unbelievable meals of our lives.
