Prostate Inflammation Signs & Symptoms (& Why They Occur)

Prostate inflammation (prostatitis) is a condition involving a variety of causes of prostate inflammation. These include bacterial infections and urinary obstructive causes (including benign prostatic hyperplasia). There are four types of prostatitis, each of which has particular pathophysiological mechanisms and signs and symptoms. In this lesson, we discuss the signs and symptoms of all types of prostate inflammation.
