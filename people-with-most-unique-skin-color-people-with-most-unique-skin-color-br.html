People with Most Idiosyncratic Skin Color

Not too long ago, people with unique skin color were considered outcasts and made to feel like they are not a part of this beautiful world. While we may be in the 21st Century, we are still far from embracing the differences among us without being judgmental. But, in recent years, the tables have slowly turned and people with unique skin have constantly used their skin color as a blessing in disguise, embracing their difference and rising above the cruelty!
