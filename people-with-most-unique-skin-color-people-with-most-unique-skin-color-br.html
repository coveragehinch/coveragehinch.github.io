As someone once said, everything has its beauty but not everyone sees it. Such, is the color of our skin. Not too long ago, people with unique skin color were considered outcasts and made to feel like they are not a part of this beautiful world. While we may be in the 21st Century, we are still far from embracing the differences among us without being judgmental.
