People with Most Idiosyncratic Skin Color
In a world of over 7 billion human beings, it would be absurd to expect everyone to look the same. It is our many differences that make the world an interesting place to be. Not too long ago, people with unique skin color were considered outcasts and made to feel like they are not a part of this beautiful world. While we may be in the 21st Century, we are still far from embracing the differences among us without being judgmental.
