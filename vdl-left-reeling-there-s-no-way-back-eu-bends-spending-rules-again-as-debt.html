BRUSSELS is poised to bend its spending rules to protect EU27 members already saddled with high levels of debt from another round of austerity. 5 countries pay off their debts to another 22, the eu cannot and will not be able to continue in its current form.
The UK should be grateful for its decision to leave the EU - because otherwise Britain would face the prospect of taking on the EU's debt. If we were still in Eu, we would have had to follow the rules, while everyone else 'bended' the rules.
