After the clean up and fitting adjustments of the Kerckhaert Comfort Sport shoe, Bobby gets ready to nail it on the right front. He chose the new Liberty Cu 4 Combo Slim nails. The Cu Liberty nails are a great option, especially in wetter conditions as the copper coating keeps them from rusting during the shoeing cycle.
