Home Affairs Minister Karen Andrews says "our government has never lost sight" of the fact there are many challenges to our safety and security in a very changing world.

"It's without doubt that the threat environment is changing, and it's changing very rapidly," Ms Andrew said during Question Time on Thursday.

Ms Andrews said Australians can "be assured" that the Morrison government takes "very seriously the issues of national security."
