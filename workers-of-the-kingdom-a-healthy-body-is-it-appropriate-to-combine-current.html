Is it appropriate to combine current events and the Jesus story? Pastor Allen Jackson shows examples from the Bible of how God’s Truth, declared in the light of current circumstances, moves peoples’ hearts towards God. Pastor Allen also discusses communion and how to make it a meaningful part of our lives at home, at work, and within our communities.
