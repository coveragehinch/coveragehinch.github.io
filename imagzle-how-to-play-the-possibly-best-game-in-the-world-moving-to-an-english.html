And then ... let's move on to the tips on how to solve the levels!
First of all: if you use a search engine like DuckDuckgo that does not do profiling, you will not be "listed" as an Imagzle player and therefore the game will be more difficult because you will not have your own bubble. This is not to say that you can't use a search engine other than Google, I'm just warning you that it will be more difficult.
In any case: use google or any other search engines correctly! When you discover a new level, start by putting the first words that come to mind on Google images and shake the images, sometimes a mechanism is triggered that will help you a lot in the solution. Use quotes to search for words together, use minus to remove words that don't interest you and that proliferate in your search results. If it gives you too many synonyms or declensions of a name, then you must select Verbatim in the search options and it will only give you results with exactly what you are looking for.
Then, once you've hit the search engines, why don't you take advantage of your friends and family as well? Because Imagzle is also this, sharing, socialization and discovering.
