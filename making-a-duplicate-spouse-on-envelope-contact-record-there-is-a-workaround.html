There is a workaround for getting the Mr & Mrs or Partners names merged from the contact manager into both the card and onto the envelope. This video touches on a few key principles for doing so. Forgive a few sound hiccups - I didn't want to spend a lot of time on cleanup when it is good enough.

Here's a link to a step-by-step for getting this to work for you:
https://goo.gl/ip7mQG

Yes, this is an "inelegant" solution to the problem by creating near-duplicate records in the account. In larger contact lists this could cause a user to top out their storage limits and prematurely force them to move to a premium package. And there will still be contacts that will not fit easily into this workaround. Still, it is a way to get around the "first name" field printing one spouse name on the envelope when it is addressed to two.

You have to realize that the dynamic is that some spouses will be offended by and will not open mail addressed to only their spouse.
