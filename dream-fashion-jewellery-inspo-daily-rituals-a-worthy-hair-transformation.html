Then, we ask some of our favourite inspiring women to talk us through their top wellness habits and rituals, from nutrition to exercise. Plus, we're back with another instalment of SheerLuxe reader makeovers! This time, celebrity colourist Nicola Clarke (hair stylist to Kate Moss) gives one SL reader a stunning hair transformation.
