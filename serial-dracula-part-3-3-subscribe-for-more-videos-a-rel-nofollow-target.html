Plot:
Dracula, one of the special series of Nowruz 1400, tells the story of the rich and influential family of "Kamerva". Kamran Kamerva, his wife Jaleh and their son live in a world full of wealth and luxury. They are so immersed in wealth and prosperity that they do not have the slightest understanding of the life and conditions of other classes, especially the lower classes of society! But suddenly, their lives full of happiness collapse with the occurrence of a shocking event and become an introduction to the subsequent events of the series.
