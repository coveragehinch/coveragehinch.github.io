Want to know a cheap way to make your fireplace or wood stove look brand new? Paint it! Over time, even high-quality fireplaces can start looking dull and the finish can rust or chip. Special high-temperature paint is a great way to make it look young again. This video will tell you the dos and don'ts of painting your fireplace or stove.
