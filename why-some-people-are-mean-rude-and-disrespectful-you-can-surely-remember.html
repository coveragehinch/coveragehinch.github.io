You can surely remember a time when people were thoughtless, disrespectful, selfish and mean. Rude people are everywhere, and rudeness is on the rise. But what causes rude behavior and a mean mentality? Why are people so nasty toward others?

Well, various causes make people mean, disrespectful, and inconsiderate - from cultural differences to insecurity and low self esteem to learned behavior and low emotional intelligence. Personality disorders and mood disorders can also make people come across as somewhat offensive.
So let’s dive deeper into human psychology to learn more about the root causes of toxic behavior!
