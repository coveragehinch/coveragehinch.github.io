Stressing that democracy is a common value shared by all mankind, Wang Yi, Chinese state councilor and foreign minister, said no one is in a position to lecture others on democracy.

Wang made the remark during a year-end interview with Xinhua News Agency and China Media Group. The Chinese foreign minister shared his views on the international situation and China's diplomatic work in 2021.

He said some forces have attempted to impose their will and standards on others and use excuses such as rules, human rights and democracy to smear other countries. "We can't compromise on this. We can't retreat, and we must face up to it," said Wang.
