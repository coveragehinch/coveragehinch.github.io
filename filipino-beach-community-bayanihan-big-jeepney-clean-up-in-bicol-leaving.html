I AM LEAVING THE COMMUNITY IN CATANDUANES... BUT FIRST IT WAS AWESOME WATCHING THEM DO A BEACH BAYANIHAN CLEAN UP!

This is my last day in Puraran. I check up on the boat house building, take a few moments to soak up some awesome beach vibes, and then head over to the Municipality of Gigmoto so see the Bayanihan beach clean up! I absolutely love the big jeepney that everyone was rocking. Bicol and Catanduanes certainly is beautiful! It is so wonderful seeing the beaches be cared for, and I am excited for the future when everything gets even more beautiful and blooming!
