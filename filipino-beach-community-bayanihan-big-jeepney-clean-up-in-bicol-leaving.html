This is my last day in Puraran. I check up on the boat house building, take a few moments to soak up some awesome beach vibes, and then head over to the Municipality of Gigmoto so see the Bayanihan beach clean up! I absolutely love the big jeepney that everyone was rocking. Bicol and Catanduanes certainly is beautiful!
