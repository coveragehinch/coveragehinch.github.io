However, the problem isn't the issuance of subpoenas, it's the enforcement of congressional subpoenas.

More than two weeks ago, Congress referred Steve Bannon to the Department of Justice for criminal prosecution for contempt of Congress. Every day that Bannon goes unindicted is another day the DOJ sends the signal that witnesses need not take congressional subpoenas serious.
