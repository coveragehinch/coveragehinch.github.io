Alex and Hana deny any wrongdoing and claim it’s their mother who has transformed into a “mean, anxiety-riddled mess.” But BillieSue says she’s finally had enough of their “disgusting” behavior, cut them both off, and evicted both her children from her homes. Can Dr. Phil help repair this broken family? Find out! (20050)
