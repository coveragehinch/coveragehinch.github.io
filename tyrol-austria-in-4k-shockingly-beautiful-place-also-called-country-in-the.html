==== THE STORY ====
I stayed for a few days in Austria "by accident". Literally, my car broke down in Austria in a middle of nowhere, and I used this time to look around and experience this beautiful Alpine region called Tyrol. I did not even realize that I am in Tyrol until I tried to pay with a credit card and one of the restaurant employees said "we don't accept it many, you know it's Tyrol". So make sure you have some cash on you if you are visiting this area (especially in the mountains).
For several days I have been hiking around different trials and enjoyed some pure mountains, the rivers, animals and other wonders of the nature. Incredible views. I will post another 2 short videos from Alps and then a longer one covering my entire journey which took several weeks. If you would like to see it, do not forget to subscribe. Thank you again for your support!

==== THE LOCATION ====

"Gasthof Pension Aschlandhof"
Address: Aschland 117, 6416 Obsteig, Austria

No stores and no real civilization within around 40 minutes walking distance. Which is perfect but not when you need some food and your car is dead... Still managed to take a long walk to the store to get food for a couple of days. I have seen lamas, I have seen cows, I have seen ship and and other mountain creatures. So nice and calming to see animals.
