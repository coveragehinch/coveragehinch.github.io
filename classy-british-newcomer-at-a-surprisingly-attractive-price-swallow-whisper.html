At the 2021 Southampton Boat Show, Motor Boat & Yachting editor Hugo Andreae took the chance to tour the Swallow Whisper 300, which was making its global debut.

Swallow Whisper 300 specifications

LOA: 30ft 6in (9.32m)
Beam: 9ft 4in (2.85m)
Engines: 2 x 70hp Yamaha outboards
Top speed: 26 knots
Starting price: £150,000 (inc. VAT)
