This patient's foot type is cavoid which means that his arch is higher than normal and therefore when he walks, all of his body weight gathers on the outer line of his foot. Due to the prolonged standing the area is also traumatised and as it can be seen when debrided there is a small bruise on the center of the corn. In this case orthotic insoles are going to be very helpful.
