Are you trying to cope with a narcissist and just finding it hard to think about anything else? Or maybe you want to know how to survive or overcome the trauma of living with a narcissist in your life? Self care when dealing with narcissistic abuse can be tricky. Whether you're dealing with a covert narcissist or a regular narcissist, the constant barrage of gaslighting, lying, manipulating, intimidation tactics and so much more can leave you feeling exhausted and just a shell of your former self. Self care is one thing when you are in a normal situation but when you're dealing with a narcissist in divorce, or just every day life, it is even more critical.
