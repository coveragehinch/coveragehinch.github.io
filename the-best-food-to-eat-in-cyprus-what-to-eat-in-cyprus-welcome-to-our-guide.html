If you’re coming to Cyprus, I’m sure you’re going to be thinking about all the delicious food you’re going to eat. So here are a few of our top tips where (and what) to eat whilst in Cyprus. For breakfast, grab something fast and delicious to take on the road. There’s so much to see, and not a second to waste! The bakeries here are incredible, everything’s freshly baked and huge! Perfect car snacks, along with great coffee to go. Mini pizzas, savoury pies or perhaps you’d prefer to indulge your sweet tooth with the fabulous biscuits and pastries on offer... fruit filled to the classic, decadent baklava... heaven! For lunch, make it a stop on your adventure, and take the chance to go somewhere with character. Our tip for finding the best food - look for locally owned taverna style restaurants, and you’ll definitely be onto a good thing! They’re more likely to serve traditional food and purchase locally sourced ingredients, putting your money back into the local economy. We had a great lunch at Oniro By The Sea, a taverna looking onto a shipwreck. It has traditional food on the menu but also safe bets like burgers - if you’re travelling with the family. For dinner? Give yourself a chance to wind down... and take the best shortcut EVER to trying loads of different kinds of Cypriot cuisine, by ordering a mezze! Where you’ll get a selection of smaller dishes. So we’ve found a restaurant along the seafront and we’ve ordered mezze. And I love ordering mezze because it means you’re going to get loads of different flavours from the Cypriot menu. Here we’ve got the salad, we’ve got their traditional bread which has cinnamon in it, got some tzatziki, aubergine dip, obviously halloumi, and there’s gonna be more and more coming. Apparently we’ve got 20 dishes to try, so I’m going to get stuck in! Have you got a favourite Cypriot dish, or place to eat? Let us know in the comments below! And don’t forget to subscribe for more on Cyprus and loads of other amazing destinations from all over the world. See you next time!
