A chunk loader will keep the chunks around loaded, this way, some of your farms will not stop working, what's will boost the yield significantly, this is the best friend of iron farms.

It's really easy to build and very cheap, everyone can do it :D

I also provide some valuable tips about chunk loaders, but if you have any doubt or problem with this tutorial just let me know in the comments, we will help you out!

I tested it in few minecraft version, so it will work in Minecraft 1.18 / 1.16 / 1.16.5 / 1.17 to name a few, but probably works on every version since observer block was introduced in the game.

So, if you play Minecraft, build a chunk loader, you will not regret about that!
