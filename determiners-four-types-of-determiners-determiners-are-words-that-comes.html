Determiners are words that comes at the beginning of the noun hence basically introduces a noun.
They determines noun, that is whether a noun is specific or general.

Now, determiners are basically divided into four types :
1.) Articles : which are a,an,the
2.) Demonstratives : which are words like this, that, these, those
3.) Possessives : which are words like my/your/his/her/your/their
4.) Quantifiers : which are words like some/few/little/many/much/any
