Little One was written while watching an episode of one of my favorite TV shows, AMC's Walking Dead. The song and video tell the story of a romantic relationship that is not only infectious but consumingly toxic in all the right ways. Sometimes we find ourselves in love with the wrong person but for some reason we love the way it hurts.

Filmed across various locations in Washington State including The Rendezvous Jewelbox Theatre in Seattle and the NWFitco Company in Puyallup.
