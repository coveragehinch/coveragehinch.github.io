This interview was filmed on December 16, 2021.

​Why didn't gold experience more momentum in 2021, and will 2022 be any better? Heading into the new year, these are the questions many investors are asking.

Alain Corbani, head of mining at Finance SA, shared his thoughts, saying that in order to perform the yellow metal needs a weak US dollar and negative or declining real rates.

In 2021, the market got the opposite — a stronger US dollar and higher real rates. But "what we had in 2021 is not going to be repeated in 2022," he explained.
