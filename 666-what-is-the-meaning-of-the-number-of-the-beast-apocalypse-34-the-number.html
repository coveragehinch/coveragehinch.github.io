The number 666 has been feared, calculated and speculated upon as a number that symbolizes the presence of the deepest evil. Yet, how can we understand it in the context of it's inclusion in the Book of Revelation, what are its implications and effects, and what hope is there to counter such deep evil.

Fr. Bill discusses possible meanings to the number, possible counters to the evil it represents as well as other areas of awareness we perhaps overlook where the number 666 is concerned.
