If you are applying for an open work permit as a spouse or common-law partner of an international student of Canada, you are required to submit many supporting documents in your application.

In this video, I am going to show you how to write an effective letter of intent that you need to upload in the 'Purpose of Travel' section found in GCKey. This is the most important element for the approval of your open work permit application.
