This went from a joyous moment in pursuit of wild food in what I thought was a sure thing. Only it didn't work out that way. I made shot on a nice buck which turned into a 2.5km 4 day long blood trailing that eventually dwindled out. For most of it, I kept my camera off; part of that was because of the emotional shame I had felt about it and part of it was because the filming felt secondary to the task at hand which deserved my full attention. That being said theirs a gap in the footage as I catch you up on my difficult experience and lesson in humility.
