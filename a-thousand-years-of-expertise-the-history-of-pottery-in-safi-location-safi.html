At least since the arrival of the Phoenicians -- over two thousand years ago --- Moroccan pottery has been closely intertwined with the coastal city of Safi. The abundance of rich clay drew the early settlers to the area and ever since, numerous civilizations have come to settle here leaving their own mark on the craft and the city.
