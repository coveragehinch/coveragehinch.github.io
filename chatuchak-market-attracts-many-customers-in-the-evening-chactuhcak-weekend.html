Chactuhcak Weekend market is the largest outdoor market in Bangkok.
It attracts a lot of visitors.
The market is open in the morning.
In the evening, stores outside the pet zone and the main street are closed for business.
However, the cooler weather brings more customers to the Market.
You can buy casual clothes and items.
The cooler weather allows you to browse the stores at your leisure.
