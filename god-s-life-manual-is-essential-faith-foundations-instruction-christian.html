Faith Foundations: In life, we often find ourselves trying to figure out everything on our own. We throw away the IKEA manual, we wander around the grocery store refusing to ask where the chips section is, and we don't ask for help when life takes a turn. Well luckily for us, God has provided a manual for how to live life in the Bible! God's Word is filled with guidance, instruction, and direction for our lives. We just need to take the time to open it up and read it.

This is Faith Foundations hosted by Tammy Kanei. In this series Tammy will take us through the most basic, foundational aspects of our faith as Christians.
