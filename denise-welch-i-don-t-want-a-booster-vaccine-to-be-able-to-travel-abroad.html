The government is urging millions of Brits to get their booster vaccines before Christmas to counter waning immunity from their first and second doses.

Travellers can now also prove they have received a booster jab using the NHS Covid Pass app.

02:00 "I have asked two epidemiologists if I needed the booster and they both said no".
03:18 "I don't want to have a booster to go away to exercise my fundamental right to go on holiday".
06:34 "If have Covid I can go somewhere with double-vaccination proof and get in, but my unvaccinated friend who doesn't have the virus can't get in"
07:47 "There were times when my sister and I alone kept my dad alive because of the NHS' broken system"
