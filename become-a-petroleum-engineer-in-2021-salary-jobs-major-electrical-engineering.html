Petroleum engineers design and develop methods for extracting oil and gas from deposits below the Earth’s surface. Petroleum engineers also find new ways to extract oil and gas from older wells.

Become a petroleum engineer in 2021? Petroleum engineers are in charge of making sure that there is enough supply of oil and gas in an area. They use cutting-edge technology to discover oil and gas sources without ruining the environment and the lives of the inhabitants in that area.
