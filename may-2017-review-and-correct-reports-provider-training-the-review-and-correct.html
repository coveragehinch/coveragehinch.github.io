The Review and Correct Reports Provider Training was webcast live on May, 2, 2017. The focus of the training was to assist providers from the following care settings in better understanding how Review and Correct Reports fit within the overall Quality Reporting Program: Inpatient Rehabilitation Facilities, Long-Term Care Hospitals, and Skilled Nursing Facilities.
