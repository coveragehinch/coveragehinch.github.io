They can track where your bike, how fast you ride (including your top speed), and how long you've been cycling once they're fitted. Cadence, heart rate, and calories burned are all tracked by a good bike computer.

Modern bicycle computers also include navigational tools, such as turn-by-turn directions to your destination. The best bike computers nowadays are wireless, tiny handlebar-mounted devices with a GPS chip, Bluetooth, ANT+, WiFi, and a variety of other features.
