10 Paradoxical Experiences The INFJ Dreads Every Single Day. INFJs hold on to their irrevocable beliefs. But unavoidably, there will always be paradoxical experiences that can prove their pre-existing beliefs wrong. These paradoxes can either discourage them or give them the greatest life lessons.

INFJs unconsciously drag themselves into their paradoxes every time they ponder and reflect on their experiences and decisions. Nevertheless, the more they mature, the more they're able to adapt to these surprising turns of events. As an INFJ, what other paradoxes do you fear the most? Have you experienced all of these?
