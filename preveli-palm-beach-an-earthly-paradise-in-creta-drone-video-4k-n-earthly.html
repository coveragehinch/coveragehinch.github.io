Αn earthly paradise!
The palm forest with the lake / river, the steep rocks and the beach of Preveli, create a rare beauty landscape in the south of Rethymno that will charm forever any lucky traveler found in the place.
The favorite destination - after Matala - the hippies of the 70s and not accidentally because in this heavenly environment the hippies found the absolute harmony between nature and man ...

From the Cretan mountains with the Kourtaliotiko gorge begins the ''Grand Potamos'' that reaches Preveli to create a lake (river) between the palm trees of "Theophrastus" and end in a parallel course with the beach before it expels In the South Cretan Sea.
Even aerial images from drone are too poor to describe the feeling of greatness of creation that you only feel when you are there ...
