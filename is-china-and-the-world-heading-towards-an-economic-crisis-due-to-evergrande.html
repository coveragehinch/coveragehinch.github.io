07:45 What are the problems Evergande is facing? 300 Billion in debt they can't pay back; 80% loss in value; selling assets at discount.
09:53 Which systemic problems does China have? Land sale, property speculation, little write downs of bad investments, and crossing the red lines of real-estate liabilities

22:09 Will Evergrande set-off a crisis in China or globally? Unlikely, as this is not Lehman brothers or Japan crisis of 90s, but also because Beijing has been preparing for this and is not bailing out Evergrande.
