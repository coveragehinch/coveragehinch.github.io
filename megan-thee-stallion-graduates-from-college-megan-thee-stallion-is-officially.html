Megan Thee Stallion is officially a college graduate. The Grammy Award-winning rapper, whose real name is Megan Pete, received a Texas Southern University Diploma with over 840 other graduates. She earned a bachelor of science degree in health administration. The singer told Entertainment Tonight in 2020 that graduating from college was something she was doing to honor her mother and grandmother, both of whom have passed away.
