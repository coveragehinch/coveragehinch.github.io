Three influential and prominent industry representatives, who have have been instrumental in business aviation’s humanitarian and policy efforts, will serve as keynote speakers for EBACE2016. The event’s Opening General Session features presentations by Bernard Kouchner, former French minister of foreign and European affairs and authority on the Middle East, geopolitics and globalization; Bassim Haidar, group founder and CEO of Channel IT Group; and Patrick Ky, executive director of the European Aviation Safety Agency.
