In this video I demonstrate my approach to isolating out an injured or strained soleus muscle. Uphill running and long distance running can cause this overuse injury. However, it can be confused for a gastrocnemius muscle injury unless you isolate it out during the examination. My demonstrated technique explains how to do that.
