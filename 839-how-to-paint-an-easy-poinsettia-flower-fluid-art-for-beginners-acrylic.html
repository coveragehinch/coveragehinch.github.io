In today's video I will show you HOW TO paint an easy POINSETTIA flower.
This Fluid art technique is really easy for beginners, so if you are new to acrylic pouring, this is something that you can do too!

PROCESS:
- Pour your base on canvas
- Make puddles with your colours
- Draw some petal pattern
- Put down damped paper napkin or towel
- Lift the napkin
- Torch
- And here is your flower

COLOURS used for this Acrylic pour painting:
- Amsterdam Oxide black
- DecoArt Extreme sheen Garnet
- Amsterdam Cadmium red
- Arteza Prussian green
- Amsterdam olive green light
- Pearl white

My pearl white mixture:
1 part Vallejo pearl medium
1 part titanium white
4 parts pouring medium
