Dan and Maiyah show you how to use the power of music to create super engaging video content in the latest episode of Tips And Tricks.

Music can make or break a video. Pick a good tune, and you can quite literally create a mood for your viewers. A bad one? Your video will be off quicker than you can say “wow, that song is REALLY BAD!“.

In this video, Maiyah and Dan explain some of the theories behind why music works (and why it doesn’t), and show you how to choose a track that works for your content, plus much more.
