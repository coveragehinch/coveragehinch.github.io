Back in Sri Lanka means more adventures! But before we start the adventures, we need to do our part and do a 14 days quarantine. #SriLanka #Avelovinit #QuarantineLife #VisitSriLanka

Here are the travel requirements fore tourists before going to Sri Lanka:
-Negative RT-PCR test taken not more than 72 hrs before the flight
-Vaccination card (14 days after 2nd dose was taken)
-Sri Lanka visa
-online Health declaration: https://airport.lk/health_declaration/index
- travel insurance covering covid
-PCR test upon arriving at the hotel
- Sri Lanka visa should be applied before arrival. There’s no more visa upon arrival option at the airport: https://eta.gov.lk/slvisa/
Applicants who are not fully vaccinated should book an accredited level 1 hotel quarantine for 14 days
