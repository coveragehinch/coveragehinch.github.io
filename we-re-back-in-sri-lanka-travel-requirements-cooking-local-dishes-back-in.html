-Sri Lanka visa
-online Health declaration: https://airport.lk/health_declaration/index
- travel insurance covering covid
-PCR test upon arriving at the hotel
- Sri Lanka visa should be applied before arrival. There‚Äôs no more visa upon arrival option at the airport: https://eta.gov.lk/slvisa/
