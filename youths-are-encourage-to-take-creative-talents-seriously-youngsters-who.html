Youngsters who have a talent for writing and the performing and theatre arts are being encouraged to harness their skills.

The encouragement comes from coordinator of the Theatre Arts Festival committee Sean Frederick who said the Ministry of Culture can provide guidance on how they can take the next step in developing their talent.

Frederick further spoke on how the creative and performing arts have contributed to the growth in the local tourism industry.
