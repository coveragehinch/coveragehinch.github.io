Dr. Lefkothea-Vasiliki Andreou is a Senior Teaching Fellow at the Department of Biological Applications and Technology, at the University of Ioannina, Greece. She holds degrees in Biochemistry, Biotechnology and in English Language and Literature. Her PhD is in Auditory Neuroscience and her PhD research involved psychophysics and brain imaging. She has worked as a domain expert and editor at a global leading publisher of science and health information. She is also a visual artist with international screenings. She is a member of the local panel of judges (FameLab Ioannina) of the international science communication contest, FameLab. Her research work and publications in peer-reviewed journals and book chapters involve an array of subjects such as neuroscience, molecular biology, human-computer interaction, history, and education. Undoubtedly, she will broaden our horizons with her story and ideas!
