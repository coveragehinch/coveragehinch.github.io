There are some cultures where it is usual for a husband to not leave his family for his wife but bring his wife into his family. In some cultures, the wife literally moves in. It always boils down to the same thing. Now I'm also going to tell you at this point that you should subscribe to this channel and learn a lot about marriage because that's really what's going on here. We don't learn about marriage. But this particular issue is one that you can do something about. You see when a husband gets married, he has a subconscious expectation which is not unrealistic, I might add, that his wife will give him something that he only can get from a woman which is unconditional love.
