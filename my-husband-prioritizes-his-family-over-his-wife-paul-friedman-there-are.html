Now I'm also going to tell you at this point that you should subscribe to this channel and learn a lot about marriage because that's really what's going on here. We don't learn about marriage. Husbands don't learn how to please their Wives and so there's a very high divorce rate in the western world, and there would be a really high one in the eastern world if it were less frowned upon. But this particular issue is one that you can do something about. You see when a husband gets married, he has a subconscious expectation which is not unrealistic, I might add, that his wife will give him something that he only can get from a woman which is unconditional love.
