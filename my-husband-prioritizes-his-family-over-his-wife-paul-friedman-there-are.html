There are some cultures where it is usual for a husband to not leave his family for his wife but bring his wife into his family. In some cultures, the wife literally moves in. But this particular issue is one that you can do something about. You see when a husband gets married, he has a subconscious expectation which is not unrealistic, I might add, that his wife will give him something that he only can get from a woman which is unconditional love.
