This is an English vocabulary game to teach beginner EFL / ESL students about 'places around town'. Includes places and buildings vocabulary such as, hospital, restaurant, park, airport, etc. This activity is great to introduce or review vocabulary with beginner ESL students. To play this game, simply play the video in class and ask students to try to guess what the building or place is. Pause the video when students are ready to guess.
