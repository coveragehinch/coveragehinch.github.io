Did you enjoy this new show? Be sure to let us know in the comments!

In addition to the "Blackout" mode in Call of Duty: Black Ops 4 (2018), Warzone is the second main battle royale installment in the Call of Duty franchise. Black Ops 4 relies more on equipable gadgets while Warzone focuses on accumulating an in-game currency called 'Cash' as opposed to equipable gadgets. Battle Royale and Plunder are the two key game modes in Warzone. It supports up to 150 players in a single match, which is more than what is typically seen in battle royale games.﻿
