'Err… I’m not supposed to tell you this, but…'

This is one example of a phrase which can make someone really listen to what you have to say, or heed advice you have to give. There are many ways to get people to open themselves wide to new learning, and mimicking everyday speech like this can help us make our clients really listen.

In this video I share a few more examples like this that you can adapt for your clients, and I talk a little about peas bouncing off a balloon (...this is explained in the video!)
