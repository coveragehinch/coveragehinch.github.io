Wondering what is the best Figma plugin for organization? You found it! Zeplin is a Figma plugin designed to help you keep track of your files, your design systems, and more importantly help others view your designs in a coherent manner. Zeplin is great for small and large teams and can be used with Figma, Sketch & Adobe XD. In my opinion, there's no other system you need to keep yourself organized!
