Release Info:
Morttagua brings to Timeless Moment what could be one of his strongest tracks to date in the brand new single “The Mantra”.

“The Mantra” is a track built around a beautiful worship mantra for the lord Shiva. Mahadeva better known as Shiva is one of the principal deities of Hinduism. He is the Supreme Being within Shaivism, one of the major traditions within contemporary Hinduism.

With melody themes and atmospheres surrounding it’s amazing vocal work, ‘The Mantra’ shines for its supreme hypnotic dance floor appeal. With Morttagua’s trademark melodic riffs and astonishing sound design coupled with a strong percussion work this track is a true candidate to become an anthem in this summer season.
