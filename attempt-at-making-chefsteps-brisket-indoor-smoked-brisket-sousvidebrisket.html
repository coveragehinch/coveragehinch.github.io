This is my attempt at making Indoor Smoked Brisket by Chefsteps.
The result was much better than I anticipated, yielding one of the most tender briskets I have ever made.
Pink salt was added 3 hours prior in order to give it the smoke ring.
I cooked the brisket at 60C for 48 hours instead of 68C for 24 hours.
