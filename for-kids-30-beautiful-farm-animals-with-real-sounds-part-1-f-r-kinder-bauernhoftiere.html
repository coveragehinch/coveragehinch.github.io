This video, for children and their parents (and all others who love farm animals aswell) includes 30 different breeds of farm animals, which I have filmed in the last years in various european countries.
Their is no music in this video, only real natural farm sounds, so the kids can get a feeling for the farmlife and its animals.
Most of the breeds in the video are very rare, only a few of them are common breeds:
White Baroque donkey, Danish landrace pig, Austrian Bantam chicken, Brown Swiss cattle, Frisian Gull chickens, Royal Palm turkeys, Peacock goats, African geese, Mangalica pigs, Haflinger horses, Simmental cattle, Pomeranian ducks and much more are waiting for you.
