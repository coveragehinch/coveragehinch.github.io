DIY Gift Card Envelope Measurements:
Cut cardstock 5 ½” x 5 ½”
Score one side at 1 ½”, rotate and score again at 1 ½”
Rotate cardstock 180 degrees and score one side at ½”, rotate and score again at 1 ¼”
Fold at score lines
Trim out each corner
Watch the video for final assembly
Finished envelope 2 ½” x 3 ¾”
