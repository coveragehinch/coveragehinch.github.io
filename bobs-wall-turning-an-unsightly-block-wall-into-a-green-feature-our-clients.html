our clients had a problem. There lovely rear deck area & outdoor living space was spoilt by an ugly concrete retaining wall...they asked us for ideas

-we suggested hiding concrete wall with simple green-wall panels, not just attached to wall face, but wrapping over top of wall to create an illusion of a thicker 'hedge' rather than a green 'veneer'

-the transformation was both startling & immediate, the lush green-wall lifted the whole area, providing a wonderful, natural-looking transition from deck to gardens
