Unfortunately, his horrible neighbor continued to mess up his perfect parking plan.

Because AngryAussie lived in a large apartment building, he had no idea which tenant was blocking him in. His first solution to the problem was to peacefully get his neighbor to stop taking his guest spot and blocking him in.

Finally, AngryAussie decided to teach his neighbor a lesson. A rude neighbor kept blocking his parking space, so he got the perfect revenge.

He was excited by the thought of his neighbor searching the parking garage for his car.
