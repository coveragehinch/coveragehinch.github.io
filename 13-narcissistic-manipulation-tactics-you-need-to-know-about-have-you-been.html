Have you been the victim of narcissistic manipulation tactics? You may not even be aware that someone close to you uses emotional manipulation and psychological control on you. Instead of providing happiness and encouraging success, these people often become obstacles to achievement.

Since changing a manipulator’s behavior is pretty much impossible, it’s important to learn about the narcissist’s favorite manipulation and punishment tactics so that you can protect yourself from their narcissistic abuse.

The narcissist uses gaslighting or crazy making communication to manipulate how you think. However, when you learn to recognize the psychology of how a narcissist manipulates you, you can outsmart them and protect yourself from further harm.
