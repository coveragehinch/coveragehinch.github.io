Reading aloud to children is a critical component of supporting their oral language development. When teachers regularly engage children in interactive read-alouds, including holding conversations before and after book-reading, children have opportunities to practice their oral language skills.
See https://www.easternct.edu/center-for-early-childhood-education/oral-language-development/interactive-read-alouds.html for additional resources and related videos.
