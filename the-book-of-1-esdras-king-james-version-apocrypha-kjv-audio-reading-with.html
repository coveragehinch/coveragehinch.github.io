An audio reading with text of of the book of 1 Esdras from the King James Version Apocrypha (Deuterocanon).

This book is similar to the book of Ezra with notable additions being King Jossiah's passover and a brief telling of the fall of Judah, and the introduction of Zerubbabel with the tale of the three guardsmen.



This book is considered canonical by Eastern Orthodox churches, and deuterocanonical (apocryphal) by the Roman Catholic and Protestant churches.

In the King James Version, the book of 1 Esdras was included as a book of the Apocrypha.
