Hi Everyone,

With the death of the brilliant Stephen Sondheim, we’re experiencing the end of an era of songwriting that will never come again.

Here’s a gorgeous song that he wrote for the ending of an early musical he wrote called “Anyone Can Whistle”. The song is “With So Little To Be Sure Of”. It feels like a goodbye song to someone who was loved.

I loved singing and playing it. He was really something.

Hope you like it too.

Barry
