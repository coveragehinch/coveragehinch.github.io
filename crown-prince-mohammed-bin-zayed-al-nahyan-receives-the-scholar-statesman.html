On the occasion of receiving The Washington Institute's Scholar-Statesman Award, Abu Dhabi Crown Prince Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan discusses his country's motivation for making peace with Israel with Institute Executive Director Dr. Robert Satloff (English and Arabic with subtitles). The conversation took place in Abu Dhabi in October 2021 before the November 18, 2021 Scholar-Statesman Award event in New York City.

SHEIKH MOHAMMED BIN ZAYED (ARABIC, TRANSLATED TO ENGLISH): Why did we make this big decision for peace? We saw this as a unique opportunity. We decided that there would be a diplomatic relationship. For us, this is an important decision for many reasons. First, for the Palestinians themselves. Second, to send a clear message to the world and the region that we are striving for peace. Third, it is a mission that we have inherited from our founder, the late Sheikh Zayed, God rest his soul. He was a man of peace and compassion, since the days of the UAE‚Äôs inception.
