On the occasion of receiving The Washington Institute's Scholar-Statesman Award, Abu Dhabi Crown Prince Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan discusses his country's motivation for making peace with Israel with Institute Executive Director Dr. Robert Satloff (English and Arabic with subtitles). The conversation took place in Abu Dhabi in October 2021 before the November 18, 2021 Scholar-Statesman Award event in New York City.

SHEIKH MOHAMMED BIN ZAYED (ARABIC, TRANSLATED TO ENGLISH): Why did we make this big decision for peace? We saw this as a unique opportunity. We decided that there would be a diplomatic relationship. But the rewards are an incentive, and the outcomes we will achieve together are far greater than the drawbacks. When we decided on this step, we were looking forward to a level of cooperation that goes beyond just peace itself. The UAE aspires to a greater peace, one that is for the benefit of all.
