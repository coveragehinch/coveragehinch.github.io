Spacious Primary bedroom with walk-through closet & 4 pc ensuite includes a new shower & 2 sinks on a quartz counter. Heated underground garage: – 1 parking spot owned, 1 storage locker, wash-bay access. Security with front doors locking after 7pm. No smoking building. Pets under 25lbs allowed. Condo has updated: roof, windows, AC/Furnace, garage, elevator & balconies in past few years. A great place to call home. This one won't disappoint, and won't last!!
