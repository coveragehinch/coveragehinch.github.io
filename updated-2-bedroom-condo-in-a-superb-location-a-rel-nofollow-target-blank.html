This bright & spacious 2 bedroom condo apartment is in a prime location just outside the heart of the downtown core & steps to the: Rideau Sports Centre (fitness & tennis facility), footbridge leading to Sandy Hill & Ottawa U, river, local parks, bike & walking paths. Spacious Primary bedroom with walk-through closet & 4 pc ensuite includes a new shower & 2 sinks on a quartz counter. Heated underground garage: – 1 parking spot owned, 1 storage locker, wash-bay access. Security with front doors locking after 7pm. No smoking building. Pets under 25lbs allowed.
