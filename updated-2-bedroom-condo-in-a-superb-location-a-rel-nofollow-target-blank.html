Welcome Home! This bright & spacious 2 bedroom condo apartment is in a prime location just outside the heart of the downtown core & steps to the: Rideau Sports Centre (fitness & tennis facility), footbridge leading to Sandy Hill & Ottawa U, river, local parks, bike & walking paths. Kitchen upgraded with quartz counters, backsplash, pantry, ample white cabinets, all appliances included. Convenient in-unit stackable laundry. Additional bedroom with window overlooking shared courtyard. 4 pc main bath with new bath fitter tub/shower & surround walls & quartz counter. Heated underground garage: – 1 parking spot owned, 1 storage locker, wash-bay access. Security with front doors locking after 7pm. No smoking building. Pets under 25lbs allowed.
