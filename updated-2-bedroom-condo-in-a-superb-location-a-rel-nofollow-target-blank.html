Welcome Home! This bright & spacious 2 bedroom condo apartment is in a prime location just outside the heart of the downtown core & steps to the: Rideau Sports Centre (fitness & tennis facility), footbridge leading to Sandy Hill & Ottawa U, river, local parks, bike & walking paths. Minutes to transit, groceries & other amenities. Beautiful finishes, including gleaming hardwood & tile floors, upgraded baseboards & crown moulding, renovated baths & kitchen. 1300 sq ft floor plan well designed so the living area is separate from the bedrooms for privacy. Enter through the foyer with 2 storage closets to the open concept living/dining room with bright sunny window & patio door. Kitchen upgraded with quartz counters, backsplash, pantry, ample white cabinets, all appliances included. Convenient in-unit stackable laundry. Spacious Primary bedroom with walk-through closet & 4 pc ensuite includes a new shower & 2 sinks on a quartz counter. Additional bedroom with window overlooking shared courtyard. 4 pc main bath with new bath fitter tub/shower & surround walls & quartz counter. Heated underground garage: – 1 parking spot owned, 1 storage locker, wash-bay access. Security with front doors locking after 7pm. No smoking building. Pets under 25lbs allowed. Condo has updated: roof, windows, AC/Furnace, garage, elevator & balconies in past few years. A great place to call home. This one won't disappoint, and won't last!!
