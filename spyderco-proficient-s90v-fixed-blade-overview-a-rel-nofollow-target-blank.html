Description
The Spyderco Proficient fixed blade hits the major points of the traditional bushcraft knife and kicks them up a notch or two. Designed by Bushcraft UK wilderness expert Chris Claycomb, the Proficient boasts a full tang of premium CPM-S90V stainless steel with thick contoured carbon fiber handle scales. The scales are carefully machined to ensure maximum comfort during prolonged cutting tasks. Razor sharp blade has a non-traditional full flat grind for reduced friction cutting and a stellar satin finish. Stainless steel pin construction with lanyard hole. Includes a black leather belt sheath with hard plastic insert for secure carry. The Spyderco Proficient is brand new direct from Spyderco Knives.
