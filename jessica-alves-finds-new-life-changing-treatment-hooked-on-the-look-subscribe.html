HAVING spent over £700,000 on her look, trans woman Jessica Alves has finally found the treatment of her dreams. The 38-year-old is currently living in her hometown of São Paulo, Brazil, where she is immersing herself in Brazilian culture and enjoying her latest beauty regime. Jessica told Truly: “I want to look ageless and flawless forever for as long as I can with the help of technology. This anti-ageing treatment I am doing here in Brazil has been helping me look younger. Actually, I am ageing backwards!” Today, Jessica will be going for her sought after treatment. “The anti-ageing treatment will accelerate the natural production of collagen. I’m going to be having a facelift effect without going through surgery." Her friend, Rafaela Manfrini, will also be joining Jessica to get a better understanding of how the treatment works. “Rafaela and I have a lot in common. We love beauty. We love fashion. She’s really confident and beautiful and always gives me good advice about how to be a woman.”
