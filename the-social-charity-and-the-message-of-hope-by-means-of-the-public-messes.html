The social charity offered to the needed heal the wounds of the financial crisis and sends a message of hope thanks to the public messes provided by the Church of Greece. They are plenty those who offer what they can squeeze off their poor income so that homeless and poverty-stricken people could find a hot plate of food. The Church of Aghios Sostis --thanks to the financial help of their parishioners and the assistance of Father Georgios—provides daily 180 meals to people in need. Among them Maria, a Law School undergraduate and mother of an underage child and Manolis, a former businessman.
