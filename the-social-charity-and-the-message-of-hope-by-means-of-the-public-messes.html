The social charity offered to the needed heal the wounds of the financial crisis and sends a message of hope thanks to the public messes provided by the Church of Greece. The Church of Aghios Sostis --thanks to the financial help of their parishioners and the assistance of Father Georgios—provides daily 180 meals to people in need.
