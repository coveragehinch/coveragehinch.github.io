Writing a good outline will definitely help you write faster and more efficiently. Outlines are the blueprint of your writing, and they help you gather your ideas so you only have to focus on the writing when you're behind your laptop. In this video, I discuss what an outline is, how you can create an outline, and I show you how you can write an outline for an essay.
