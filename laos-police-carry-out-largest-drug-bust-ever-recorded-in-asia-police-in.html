Police in Laos have carried out the largest drug bust ever recorded in Asia after authorities located more than 55 million methamphetamine tablets and more than 1,300 kilograms of crystal meth.

Authorities intercepted a truck carrying beer crates in Bokeo, leading them to a nearby house where two men were detained.

Laos has been a gateway for drug traffickers who are known to transport meth shipments into Thailand and Myanmar as part of "the golden triangle region".
