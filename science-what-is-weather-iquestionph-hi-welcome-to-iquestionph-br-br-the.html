The today's lesson is about 'The Weather'

☔The weather is the short-term condition of the atmosphere of a particular place.

❄️The condition may change within a short period of time, sometimes in a matter of minutes, and sometimes, in a matter of hours or days.

🌞Weather can be classified as sunny, windy, cloudy, rainy, snowy, or stormy.

There are factors that affect the weather.
