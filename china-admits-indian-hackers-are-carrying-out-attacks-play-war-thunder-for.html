Many countries have been at the receiving end of Chinese cyber espionage

Earlier, China obtained more than 50 terabytes of data from U.S. defense and government networks, notably the Joint Strike Fighter’s stealth radar and engine secrets, through cyber espionage.
The NSA or National Security Agency of the US estimated that the Chinese had conducted more than 30,000 cyber attacks as part of the massive defense industrial espionage and that more than 500 attacks were significant.
