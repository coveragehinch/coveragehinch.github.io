The NEW updated Forester is here for 2022 and on top of that Subaru brought a special off road Wilderness Edition. The Forester being a compact SUV puts it up against the Toyota RAV4 TRD. On the outside you will discover a special color called Geyser Blue, 17in wheels with rugged off road tires, unique roof rails and body cladding. On the inside there is an 8in infotainment system, gold accents, and plenty of passenger room. Under the hood is a 2.5L flat-4 that produces 182HP & is mated to a revised CVT. Is the NEW 2022 Subaru Forester Wilderness a better SUV than a RAV4 TRD?
