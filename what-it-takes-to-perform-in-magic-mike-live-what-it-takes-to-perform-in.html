To perform in the cast of "Magic Mike Live," it takes more than being a world-class dancer and having a spectacular physique. Chief video correspondent Graham Flanagan went behind the scenes of the show based on the hit movies starring Channing Tatum. The cast performs up to eight shows a week at Sahara Las Vegas where they also rehearse, work out, and recover between performances.
