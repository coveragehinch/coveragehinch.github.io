The American Meteor Society (AMS) received several reports of a fireball seen over Michigan and surrounding US states on October 20, 2021. It was likely the reentry of a defunct Russian satellite known as Kosmos-2551. Full Story: https://www.space.com/russian-spy-satellite-kosmos-2551-fireball-video
