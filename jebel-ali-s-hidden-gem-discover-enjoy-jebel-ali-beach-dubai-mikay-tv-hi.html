Hi, Guyz! Let me show you around another beach which we discovered only while on our road trip few weeks ago.

Most of the Dubai residents go to Jumeirah open beach (including Kite Beach, Lamer & JBR area) for its pristine crystal clear water and fine sands. Considering also the accessibility of these beaches to public transportations, you will see a lot of beach goers whether residents or tourist all around.

However, if you want to spend a relaxing day hearing only the sounds of the waves and wind, you may want to visit Jebel Ali Beach. This is totally a hidden gem, I must say. Take note, it's a bit inconvenient for those who will take public transportations but if you can drive, then hop in! It's a perfect place to unwind.

The beach is open from 6:00am to 6:00pm daily and open to campers with caravans at night. Only those with caravans who secured permit are allowed to stay overnight to camp. No tents allowed!

A food truck is open at night for campers who would like to unwind under the stars with comfy bean bags and pallet seats. It could be a fun night, I guess!

So come and visit this place! Let's swim and enjoy with the fish that freely swims along the shore...
