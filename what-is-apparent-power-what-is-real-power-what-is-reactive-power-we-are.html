And how it is produced.

If the load is pure resistance there will not be any reactive power over the system.
So according to formula
S=P+jQ
At this time the power is fully concussed by load and also source is not get disturbed until it get over load
Let us considered other case
When the load has more inductance or capacitance over it then reactive power will come into picture
