When you are getting closer to ovulation, it’s normal to have some signs of ovulation or ovulation symptoms. However, only a very small percentage of women experience all the signs & symptoms of ovulation.

In today’s video, Dr. Schehrezade explains:

1. Signs & Symptoms of Ovulation
2. The science behind the following Ovulation Symptoms:
- Heightened Sexual Desire
- Cervical Position
- Cervical Mucus or Ovulation Discharge
- Ovulation Pain
- Basal Body Temperature or BBT

Many women don't get these signs of Ovulation or no Ovulation Symptoms at all. If you are one of them, you can measure your hormone levels with the Inito Fertility Monitor.
