Architectural Justice is a full service design and remodeling center offering almost every project imaginable for the home including granite, marble, quartzite and other natural stones, cabinetry, flooring, kitchen & bath products, ornamental iron, cast stone, and custom furniture & accents.

Our 20,000 square foot showroom is truly one of the area's top destinations in design. We are one of the largest stocking stone fabricators in our area with over 3,000 slabs of natural stone on site plus over 100 selections of quartz in the showroom. Our on-site services include granite and stone fabrication, custom cabinetry, furniture and mill work, architectural metal design and fabrication, cast stone fabrication, as well as design and remodeling services.
