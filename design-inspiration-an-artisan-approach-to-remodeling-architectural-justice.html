Architectural Justice is a full service design and remodeling center offering almost every project imaginable for the home including granite, marble, quartzite and other natural stones, cabinetry, flooring, kitchen & bath products, ornamental iron, cast stone, and custom furniture & accents.

Our 20,000 square foot showroom is truly one of the area's top destinations in design. Our on-site services include granite and stone fabrication, custom cabinetry, furniture and mill work, architectural metal design and fabrication, cast stone fabrication, as well as design and remodeling services.
