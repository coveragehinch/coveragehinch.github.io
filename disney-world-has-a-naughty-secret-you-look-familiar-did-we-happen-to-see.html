You look familiar, did we happen to see you on the dance floor at Mannequins in Disney World back in 1996? You were wearing Bongo jeans, and we locked eyes for a split second, but we were halfway to the door on our way to Eight TRAX because we had a hankering for a Leisure Suit.
If that really was you, then you will love this Nightlife episode of RTT. What do you mean you weren’t born yet in 1996? Well you’re in for quite an education today because Disney World Has A Naughty Secret.
