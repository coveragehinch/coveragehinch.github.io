A light offshore breeze blew all night to produce an extremely groomed and smooth morning lineup at Keramas the other day. This was the second day in a row of good waves on the east coast, with the size about the same as the first day of the swell (see the previous video).

Much like the previous day, there were a few of barrels, but it was mostly clean walls with a couple of sections to unleash some turns. The wind never blew onshore, but the early morning definitely dished up the best conditions of the day.
