Today I show you how to make homemade chicken nuggets. Chicken nuggets are an extremely popular meal choice for both kids & adults. Their delicious, mouth watering taste is most likely the reason. One of the best things about making homemade chicken nuggets is that you know exactly how much yolk is going into them! Because of this you know they are healthy and don't contain nasty parts of the chicken! Enjoy!
