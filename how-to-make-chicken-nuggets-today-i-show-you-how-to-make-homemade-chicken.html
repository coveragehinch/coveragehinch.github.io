Today I show you how to make homemade chicken nuggets. Chicken nuggets are an extremely popular meal choice for both kids & adults. Their delicious, mouth watering taste is most likely the reason. Because of this you know they are healthy and don't contain nasty parts of the chicken! Enjoy!
