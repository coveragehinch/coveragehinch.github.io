This is a whole different type of interactive television, a tasty kind. A prototype of something called Taste the TV, or TTTV, is being tested in Japan. After telling the device what you want to taste, it plays video of the food while it whips up a concoction of liquids to strike just the right flavor balance and then sprays it on the screen to be licked off.
