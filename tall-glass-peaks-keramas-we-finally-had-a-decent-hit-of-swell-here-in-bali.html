We finally had a decent hit of swell here in Bali, and with a firm west wind blowing it was the east coast's turn for some waves.

Glassy conditions and an incoming tide made the first half of the morning the best pick. There weren't any really deep barrels, but it was still good enough for a couple of good hits to the channel.
