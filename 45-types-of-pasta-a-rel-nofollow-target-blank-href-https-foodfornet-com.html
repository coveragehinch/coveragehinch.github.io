Some are layered like lasagne, or are variations on long noodles like tagliatelle or bucatini. To be Adding sauce variations like pesto, tomato, or cream based you could probably have pasta every day of the week and never run out of dishes.

I tried my best to find as many variations as possible, but I know I left some out. What did I miss, and what's your favorite type?
