As part of the WeThe15 campaign, The Valuable 500 will be hosting a panel conversation on Friday August 27 at 1pm, exploring the many links between Paralympic sport and business.

The panel will be moderated by Stephane Leblois, Partnerships Director at The Valuable 500 and will hear from former paralympic athletes now working in Valuable 500 businesses: Anna Johannes, Senior Account Executive at Porter Novelli, Danny Nobbs, Corporate Responsibility Consultant at Aviva, and Tucker Dupree, Workplace Colleague Engagement Lead at bp.
