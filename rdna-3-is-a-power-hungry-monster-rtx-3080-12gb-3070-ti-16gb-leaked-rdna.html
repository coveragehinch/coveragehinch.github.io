RDNA 3 is a Power Hungry MONSTER | RTX 3080 12GB & 3070 Ti 16GB Leaked

According to the latest leaks, RDNA 3 is a power hungry MONSTER, as new details have leaked on the power consumption for RX 7000 for AMD's next generation GPUs, as well as some details on the RTX 40 Lovelace from Nvidia power consumption. There have been a lot of leaks surrounding the specifications, performance improvements for gaming and ray tracing for both RDNA 3 and RTX 40, as well as significant changes in the architecture for the next gen graphics cards compared to the current gen. But according to the new rumours, both RDNA 3 and RTX 40 Lovelace have a hugely high power consumption / TDP, and price / cost to go along with the huge increase in performance and specs.

Nvidia are allegedly planning on releasing more GPUs to combat the current GPU stock shortages, specifically the RTX 3080 12GB and RTX 3070 Ti 16GB from the current RTX 30 Ampere range, and even a RTX 20 Turing card - the RTX 2060 12GB. But with many gamers left frustrated with the current graphics card shortages, will gamers pick up these new RTX 30 cards or wait for the next gen? Not much is known about the specs improvements these new cards will bring, nor the price / MSRP they will be set at.
