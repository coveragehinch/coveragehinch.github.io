Welcome to Inspire Time! In this video, Top 20 Appreciation, Gratitude, and Thank You Quotes. Let us take a moment to be appreciative of what we already have in our lives. Being thankful and expressing your appreciation are essential components of living a happy life. That desire for more might cause us to lose sight of what we already have—and for which we should be grateful.

Finding the right words to express gratitude for others' kindness, or even reminding ourselves of how much we have to be grateful for, maybe rewarding all year.

Memorize a few and keep the rest handy as a reminder. Practicing gratitude is one of the best ways you can start appreciating what you have. The goal is to focus the mind on the more positive things in life. It takes just a couple of minutes each day and can have a huge impact on your happiness.
