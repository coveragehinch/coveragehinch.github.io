A selection of new games for the video today, and the first time I have made a video since the removal of the autoplay functionality. Talking of potentially tough to get bonuses, I was pleased today to see that the new BTG slot was available to play, although given some of their recent releases was concerned it might be another dud. Not the case from what I saw in play though, and the bonus itself looks to have an interesting mechanic whereby it awards the starting free spins and multiplier.

It has the standard multiplier build up that you get on these games, plus a mechanic that can drop wilds into the reels. Looks interesting, I will commence play on it and resume the video when the reels unlock.
