A selection of new games for the video today, and the first time I have made a video since the removal of the autoplay functionality. Talking of potentially tough to get bonuses, I was pleased today to see that the new BTG slot was available to play, although given some of their recent releases was concerned it might be another dud. Looks interesting, I will commence play on it and resume the video when the reels unlock.
