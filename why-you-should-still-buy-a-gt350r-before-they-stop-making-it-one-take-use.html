We drove the Shelby GT350R back in 2016 when it was new, and it was game-changing for what the SVT team was able to achieve both with the Voodoo engine, a once-in-a-generation mill, and also the agility, the best ever for a front-engined Ford. Now that Ford has announced the end of this generation of GT350, we took the opportunity to take the last California press car for a proper blast! The 2021 Ford Mustang GT350R follows Porsche's GT3 playbook to a T, and offers not just one of the most rewarding experiences available in an American car, but one of the most rewarding experiences available in all cars.

Ford provided the vehicle, insurance, and a tank of gas for this review.
