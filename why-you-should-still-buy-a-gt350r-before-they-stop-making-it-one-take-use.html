Now that Ford has announced the end of this generation of GT350, we took the opportunity to take the last California press car for a proper blast! The 2021 Ford Mustang GT350R follows Porsche's GT3 playbook to a T, and offers not just one of the most rewarding experiences available in an American car, but one of the most rewarding experiences available in all cars.
