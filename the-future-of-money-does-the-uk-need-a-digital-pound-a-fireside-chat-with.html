Joined by the Bank of England's Head of Future Technology, William Lovell, members of the Digital Pound Foundation discuss the potential benefits of a digital Pound and whether the UK would benefit from a Central Bank Digital Currency (CBDC).

Hosted by the Digital Pound Foundation on 10 November 2021, this webinar featured some of the Foundation's originating members, as well as representatives from each of the Foundation Members, including Ripple, Quant, Electroneum, Avalanche, Billon, Accenture, CGI, Herbert Smith Freehills, and The Realization Group.
