Their "A Kind Of Hush" showed signs of breaking nationally in January 1967, however an expedient cover by Herman's Hermits was released in the US in February 1967 to reach the Top 30 of the Billboard Hot 100 in three weeks and proceed to a peak of #4.

Peter Noone and his Hermits entered a lot of charts worldwide with "There's a Kind of Hush, with #2's in Canada, Malta and Singapore.
