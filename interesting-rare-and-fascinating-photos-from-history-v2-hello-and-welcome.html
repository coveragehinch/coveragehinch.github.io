It is called Punt Gun and can kill between 50 and 100 birds in one shot. As the population of waterfowl fell to a dramatic level, at some point US government had to step in and ban this practice.

He was just 21 years old when sheriff Pat Garrett finally caught up to him in New Mexico, in July 1881.
