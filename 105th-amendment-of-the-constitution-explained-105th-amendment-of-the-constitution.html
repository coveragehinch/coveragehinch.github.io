105th Amendment of the Constitution has been passed and notified in the gazette.
127th Constitution amendment Bill OBC Bill
The Rajya Sabha may soon pass the 127th Constitution amendment Bill aka the OBC Bill turning it into 105th Constitution Amendment, overturning one aspect of the Maratha Reservation Judgement and giving states their power back to make own lists of SEBCs. This lecture explains the objective of 127th Constitution amendment Bill.
