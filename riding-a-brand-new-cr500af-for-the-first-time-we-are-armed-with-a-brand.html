We are armed with a brand new 2021 Honda CR500AF and our man Neville Bradshaw is ready and raring to let it rip! This is going to be fun...

We've teamed up with Andre and Brett to make some magic happen, they lent us their brand new Ally Frame CR two stroke for the day and we called up Mr. Bradshaw to take her for the ride of her life! Enjoy guys :-)
