Learn how to decrease in knitting using the “center double decrease” technique in this beginner knitting video from Annie’s. The center double decrease is a method of decreasing the number of stitches in your row or round while working a knitting pattern. This method is abbreviated in knitting patterns as “ccd” or "s2kp2". Learn how to center double decrease by watching this tutorial video from Annie’s.
