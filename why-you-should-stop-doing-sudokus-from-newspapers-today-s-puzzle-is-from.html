However... this is also an absolutely stunning solve and another example, if you needed it, of why humans should have had a key role in constructing all the puzzles you spend your time on. Normal sudoku rules apply. In cages, digits must sum to the small clue in the top left corner of the cage. Digits cannot repeat within a cage.
