Today's puzzle is from channel favourite Joseph Nehme. It's called The Odd One (probably a description of Simon) and it's an "ordinary" killer sudoku. However... this is also an absolutely stunning solve and another example, if you needed it, of why humans should have had a key role in constructing all the puzzles you spend your time on. Rules:
Normal sudoku rules apply. In cages, digits must sum to the small clue in the top left corner of the cage. Digits cannot repeat within a cage.
