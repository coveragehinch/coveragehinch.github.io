Pattaya has many nice beaches nearby and one of those is Dongtan Beach in Jomtien.

Interestingly, many visitors to Jomtien who are wanting to escape the Pattaya madness, miss Dongtan Beach when they visit! Many places available to sit back and relax as well as many people come along this stretch of beach to exercise.

If you want a peaceful change from Pattaya city then Dontang Beach may just be what you are looking for. If not Jomtien beach is just a few minutes walk away. Take a look and see for yourself what is literally just a few minutes away from Pattaya. It is definitely worth a visit!
