Welcome to the dark side! Or the brown side? #BrownHair
I am beyond over bright hair colours and decided to start my natural series with a classic: Chocolate brown hair 🤎🍫

Dye details:
Starting with a colour correction:
Schwarzkopf Chroma ID mask 'Blue'
Arctic fox 'Purple Rain'

Applied on the pink half of my head and then very briefly on all my hair

Brown dye:
Schwarzkopf 5-67 57g
Schwarzkopf 5-68 20g
Schwarzkopf 6-88 15g
Schwarzkopf 0-77 20g

Schwarzkopf 20 vol developer 1:1 ratio 110ml

Applied all over, left on for 40 minutes and let airdry to 80%.
Then I blowdried it all, went in with some finishing oil and ✨Voila ✨ Brown hair

This formula is very specifically formulated for blue hair base, to recreate it, avoid adding in ny booster (0-77)
