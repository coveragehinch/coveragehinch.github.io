About 1,000 vulnerable households in the South West district are set to receive some help. Each household will get up to $4,000 a year to help cushion the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic. A $1-million care fund was launched on Nov 30 at a Community Development Council meeting. Mayor Low Yen Ling also shared that South West CDC helped more than 600,000 residents through employment and assistance schemes in 2020. It also worked with corporate partners, donating over $10 million in cash and in-kind to help residents.
