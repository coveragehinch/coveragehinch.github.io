On September 22, 2021, Eddie Ndopu, disability advocate and co-collaborator on The Harkin Institute's DI Dialogue series and Rose Kirk, Verizon’s Chief Corporate Social Responsibility Officer, talked about the value creating possibilities of disability inclusion.

Their conversation covered important topics such as:
- Reimagining the discourse pertaining to “building back better” in a way that centers disability justice and intersectionality?
- Is there any through-line between DEI, environment, social, and governance investment practices and the UN Sustainable Development Goals? If so, can “it” be leveraged to drive systems change?
