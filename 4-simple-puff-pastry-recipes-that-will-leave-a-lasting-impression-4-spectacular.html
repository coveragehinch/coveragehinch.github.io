1. Puff Pastry Eclair Cups

Yield: 3
Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cooking Time: 15 minutes
Inactive Time: 30 minutes
Difficulty Level: Easy

You'll Need:

- 3 ice cream cups
- 1 puff pastry sheet, cut into strips
- 2 whisked egg yolks + milk for the egg wash
- ¾ cup vanilla pudding
- 3 round cookies
- 7 oz semi-sweet chocolate, tempered
- ½ cup shredded coconut
- ¾ stick butter

3. Egg & Cheese Puff Pastry Pockets

Yield: 2
Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cooking Time: 20 minutes
Difficulty Level: Easy

You'll Need:

- 1 puff pastry sheet
- 4 cherry tomatoes, halved
- ⅓ cup grated cheddar
- 3 egg yolks
- 1 whisked egg yolk + milk for the egg wash
- chopped chives for the garnish

* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *

4. Lemon Meringue Puff Pastry Boats

Yield: 8
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cooking Time: 30 minutes
Inactive Time: 15 minutes
Difficulty Level: Easy

You'll Need:

For the lemon curd:

- ⅓ cup lemon juice
- ¼ cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 tbsp corn starch
- ¾ stick butter

For the puff pastry boats:

- 1 puff pastry sheet
- 2 whisked egg yolks for the egg wash
- lemon zest & mint for the garnish

For the meringue:

- 2 egg whites
- 1 pinch of salt
- ½ cup sugar

Extra:

- ovular lid
- culinary torch
- piping bag

These 4 puff pastry recipes are all amazingly quick & easy ways to serve delicious snacks using puff pastry dough. The possibilities with puff pastry are truly endless!
