About the Unbound Cordless Curler:

•Powered by lithium-ion batteries that are recharged via USB for up to 45 minutes of cordless styling.

•Made with Ceramic technology to guarantee an even, consistent heat throughout, helping to get rid of frizz and lock in shine as you style.

•LCD control to choose your curl style from tight curls to barely-there waves and even change the curl direction to suit your style
