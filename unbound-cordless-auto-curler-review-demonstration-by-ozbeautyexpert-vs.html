Watch Bonnie from @ozbeautyexpert break down our newest Unbound Cordless Auto Curler and show you how you can create three different kinds of curls looks anywhere!

Starting with a quick how-to, including how to charge the Unbound Cordless Auto Curler for cord-less styling, choose your heat setting, curl direction and create your desired curl look, whether that be tight ringlets, beachy waves or big, soft glam curls.

About the Unbound Cordless Curler:

•Powered by lithium-ion batteries that are recharged via USB for up to 45 minutes of cordless styling.

•Made with Ceramic technology to guarantee an even, consistent heat throughout, helping to get rid of frizz and lock in shine as you style.

•LCD control to choose your curl style from tight curls to barely-there waves and even change the curl direction to suit your style

•Choose from 3 heat temperatures: 160°C - 200°C

•Automatic curling system takes the guesswork out of creating gorgeous waves by gently drawing the hair into the curl chamber for the fastest, easiest & most perfect curls or waves.
