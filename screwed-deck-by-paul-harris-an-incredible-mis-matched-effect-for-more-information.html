Craig will be performing magic from the creative mind of Paul Harris. Introducing Screwed Deck. One of the best opening tricks you will ever see, this routine uses very little sleight of hand and works great in any venue. What's the solution? Simple. The magician gives the deck another twist and it becomes an ordinary, examinable pack of cards. Immediately afterwards you can begin your next card trick.
