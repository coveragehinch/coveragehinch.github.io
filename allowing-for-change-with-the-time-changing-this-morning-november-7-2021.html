With the time changing this morning (November 7, 2021), it reminded me of how I sometimes dig in my heels in an attempt to try and resist something that I do not want (like it getting darker earlier!). But nothing is permanent. Change is always afoot.

Whether we say we want it or not, change offers us incredible opportunities to gently let go of the old and powerfully embrace the new.
