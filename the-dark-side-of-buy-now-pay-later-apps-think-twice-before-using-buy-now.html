Think twice before using Buy Now Pay Later apps. (Timestamps below) There are no free lunches. Find out how expensive it is to offer (for a merchant), how that can increase prices, and how using other methods to pay might be better for you (and also for lower income households!)

***Correction: As pointed out by the (great) blog "Michael James on Money", I should have factored in that the BNPL company collects a riskless $25 payment upfront. So really, they lent out $69 over 6 weeks. That increases the effective APR on their loan from 55% to 76%. For a more detailed look, visit: https://www.michaeljamesonmoney.com/2021/03/buy-now-pay-later-apps.html
