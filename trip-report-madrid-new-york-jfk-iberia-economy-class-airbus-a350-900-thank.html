Thank you for joining us on this trip report!

Come and join us on this Iberia flight to New York!
It’s my first time flying with long haul with them since 2010, when I flew the A340-300 to Miami. While I love the A340, theirs had no IFE or entertainment on board besides the general screen.

Much had changed since then I am very happy to share that with you!
