Without any doubt, ‚ÄôDo not let your hearts be troubled‚Äô is an outcome of a sudden inspiration. In fact, the work was written only in two days. Musically the work pays a tribute to the composer Johann Sebastian Bach (1685-1750), well-known for his great religious works, St. John Passion, BWV245, St. Matthew Passion, BWV244 and Mass in B minor, BWV232.
