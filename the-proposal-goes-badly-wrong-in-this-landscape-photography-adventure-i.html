This jam-packed landscape photography vlog has it all. Thirty-three minutes of inspirational photography adventures, romance, fashion, and of course an abundance of hilarity.

Both Brent and I manage to bag some spectacularly colourful panoramas. We even discover a new viewpoint of Abraham Lake that most people have never seen before.

Will Brents new mullet be accepted and what does he have to say when confronted with my suspicions about 'Karen'? Will I ever be able to get those hip waders off and how on earth do I make it through a full video without mentioning my book even once?
