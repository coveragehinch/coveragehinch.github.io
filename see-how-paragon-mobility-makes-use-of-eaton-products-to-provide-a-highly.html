Paragon's Nano Pod is a flexible energy station, easy and quick to deploy and redeploy, which combines different services, such as fast car charging, with the integration of Eaton Green Motion's RANGE XT22 DC fast charger, and energy storage, with the integration of Eaton xStorage Compact.
This all-in-one charging solution, together with the recently EASA - approved SKYCHARGE charger from Green Motion and Pipistrel, which is powering the electric aircraft at this event, provides a reliable charging infrastructure that is essential to powering a project like ELEKTROPOSTAL.
