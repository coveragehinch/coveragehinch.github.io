Do you ever feel like you don’t belong? You’re not alone. Most people feel like they don’t fit in at one time or another. Social isolation can make you feel lonely and even more withdrawn from the world - which is detrimental to your mental health.

Feeling isolated is not exclusive to introvert problems. Most of the time, though, the feeling like you don’t belong is temporary. It’s often a result of a significant life change - such as moving away from friends and family. And living abroad, in particular, can make you feel isolated and alone at first.

Thankfully, dealing with social isolation and loneliness is not an impossible task. But first, you have to get to the root of the problem. There are reasons you don’t fit in and feel like you don’t belong!
