Thomas Juneor Andersson Interview: Founder, primary writer and guitarist of the incredibly successful and popular Swedish blues rock band, Kamchatka, Thomas has been playing guitar since age 7. They have toured all over Europe in addition to touring throughout the US, as the opening act for Clutch… and Thomas has his own Signature Model Ibanez Guitar, called the Beard Bender...



Thomas Juneor Andersson Interview: During this conversation, Thomas discusses a severe accident he had in 2017, which nearly took his life… the 10-year commitment he made made when he started Kamchatka, and what he sacrificed to make sure it all worked out… 2 valuable lessons his parents imparted… Telecasters, Stratocasters… raising children, and more...
