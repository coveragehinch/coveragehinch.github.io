Pattaya is not really well respected for it's beach. However, just 10 minutes in a taxi and you can be relaxing on Cosy Beach in Pratumnak near Pattaya.

This lovely stretch of beach is often overlooked or missed out by people who visit Pattaya. Many people are totally unaware of this beach and have no idea how to even get there.

If you visit Cosy beach near Pattaya you are sure to return time and time again. take a look at this hidden gem and see for yourself why Cosy Beach just has to be on your agenda for place to visit. Those that know, well they know, those that don't sadly have missed out!

Cosy Beach near Pattaya, a very nice and enjoyable place to spend your time relaxing and soaking up the sun!
