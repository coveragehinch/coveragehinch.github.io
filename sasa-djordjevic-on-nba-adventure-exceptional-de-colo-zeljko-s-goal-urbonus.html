Topics:
- Intro & playing with Larry Bird (00:00);

- Crazy Djordjevic numbers & radical change (9:05);
- Scorer’s advice to solve Fenerbahce problem (12:59);
- Teodosic tip on Fenerbahce leader (15:59);
- Inspiring Serbia NT locker room story (19:05);
- Tough goal to repeat for Zeljko (25:57);
- Djordjevic phone calls with his dad (29:46).
