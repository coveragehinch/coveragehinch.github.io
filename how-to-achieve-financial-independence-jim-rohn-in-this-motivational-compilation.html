In this motivational compilation Jim Rohn and Zig Ziglar share their wisdom on achieving financial freedom. Jim Rohn says that in order to become financially independent you must turn part of your income into capital; turn capital into enterprise; turn enterprise into profit; turn profit into investment and finally, turn investment into financial independence. A major factor in setting a good sail towards achieving this objective will be your personal philosophy as told by both Jim Rohn and Zig Ziglar.
