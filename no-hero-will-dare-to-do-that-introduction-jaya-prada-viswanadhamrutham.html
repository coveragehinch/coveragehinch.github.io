Jaya Prada is a very beautiful and popular south Indian film actress. Her real name Lalitha Rani, she worked in Tamil, Telugu, Hindi, Malayalam, Bengali, Marathi and Kannada Movies. Jaya Prada made her screen debut in Telugu Movie Bhoomi Kosam (1974) and her First Tamil Film Manmatha Lelai (1976). Jaya Prada has worked many popular films such as Simhasanam, Veta, Meghasandesam, Sagara Sangamam and many more, and received several Awards and Nominations.
