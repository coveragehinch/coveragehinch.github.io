Some highlights from a couple of weeks bouldering in Leavenworth, Washington, during an unseasonably hot April 2016. This meant some very early starts and plenty of resting.

Leavenworth is truly a world class bouldering destination with easy access, top quality rock, a stunning setting and close-by amenities...watch out for the rattle snakes though!
