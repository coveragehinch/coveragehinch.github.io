If your goal is for your horse to become a "happy athlete," Equitopia's new online course is for YOU! This course was developed with Gerd Heuschmann, world renowned veterinarian, master rider, and acclaimed author of "Tug of War: Classical vs. Modern Dressage," "Collection or Contortion," "Balancing Act," and the DVD that shook the horse industry in 2009 "If Horses Could Speak." Based on a lifetime of study and experience integrating compassionate training techniques for the physical and mental welfare of horses, Dr. Heuschmann examines the basics of classical riding, focusing on the first three steps of the training scale: rhythm, suppleness and contact. He goes through each step in detail to explain how to produce a happy horse in balance.
This course will give you the tools you need to understand the components of true self carriage. You'll learn about the history of classical riding and the philosophies behind its evolution. By the end of the course you will have "trained your eyes" to recognize the characteristics of truly balanced and happy horses as well as identify the features of tenses horses in false collection.
