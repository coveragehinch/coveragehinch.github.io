1 large onions
2 tatashes and 6-8 scotch bonnet peppers (peppermix)
1 red bell pepper
salt to taste
2 bayleafs

seasoning powder to taste
6 pieces chicken
1/2 cup cooking oil
3 cups basmati rice
2 carrots

1 teaspoon black pepper
