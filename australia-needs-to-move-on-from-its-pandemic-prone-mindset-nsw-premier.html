NSW Premier Dominic Perrottet has been “absolutely exemplary” in facilitating his state’s transition towards a post-pandemic future, according to the Institute of Public Affairs Senior Fellow Dr Sherry Sufi.

Premier Perrottet has significantly relaxed COVID-19 restrictions, arguing the health system will be able to cope due to the state’s high vaccination rate.

Dr Sufi told Sky News Australians can begin to look forward to regaining their freedoms and liberties in 2022.

“We can’t forever remain in this pandemic prone mindset, where we are in this state of emergency where desperate measures have to be pursued,” he said.

“We have to start shifting out of that.”
