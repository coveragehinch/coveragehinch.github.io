A while back we were scouring our local Big 5 Sporting Goods and we came across this beast of a board. It's called a SK8 SURF! With a set of normal trucks in the back and a swivel (surf) truck in the front, you're meant to have one of the most radical rides you can find! The Braille Team gives this board their best shot with the help of Andy Anderson himself! What board should we skate next?
