You will often hear the term “accreditation” mentioned when choosing the college or university that might be right for you. But what does it mean when a college or university is accredited and how should this factor into your decision? Accreditation within higher education means that a college or university has undergone a review and has met a certain level of quality. College accreditation is important because it lets you know that the college meets certain standards for the education they provide. Attending a college with the right type of accreditation for your future goals is a big step in ensuring your higher education experience is the right one for you.
