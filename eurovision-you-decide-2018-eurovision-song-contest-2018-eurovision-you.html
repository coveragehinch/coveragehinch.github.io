Eurovision: You Decide is back for 2018! Join Eurovision superfan Mel Giedroyc and winner of the Eurovision Song Contest 2015 Mans Zelmerlow live from the world-famous Brighton Dome, the site of ABBA’s 1974 Eurovision Song Contest win with Waterloo. This 90-minute UK Eurovision extravaganza gives you the opportunity to vote for the act you want to represent the UK at the biggest song competition in the world! The selection show sees six shortlisted acts take to the stage in a bid to impress the viewers at home and a jury of music professionals. An expert panel are on hand to offer their thoughts on how the songs could be made to look and sound on the stage in Lisbon. After the telephone and on-line vote has closed, the public votes are combined with the results from the jury vote and the winning song is revealed! The spotlight is on and the stage is set, the countdown to find our next UK act has started – but who will win? You decide!
