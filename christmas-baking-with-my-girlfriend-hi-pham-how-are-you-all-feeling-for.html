With all the stresses of the holidays AND buying gifts AND travelling, there has been ONE thing that’s kept us sane: Paige’s FreeStyle Libre 2 sensor! This sensor is a flash glucose monitoring system which basically means she can check her glucose levels ANY time (and I’ll even get a notification, too!)

It is really important for both Paige and I to manage her levels as best we can, and her FreeStyle Libre 2 makes it so much easier. The system even sends a push notification to both of our phones*** when Paige’s glucose levels breach her pre-set ranges so we can quickly correct and keep her on the ball to buy me the perfect Christmas present 😉
