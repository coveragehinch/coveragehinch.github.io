PSMF BREAD RECIPE -
1 cup of egg whites
2 tsp of cream of tartar
Mix in mixer till you get stiff peaks.
In a small bowl combine,
1/3 cup of egg white protein powder
1 Tbsp of nutritional yeast
1 tsp of Swerve confectioners sugar OR 1 Tbsp of Allulose
1/8 of tsp of active yeast
1 tsp of xantham gum
dash of salt
MIX
Add dry ingredients slowly while your mixer is on, mix for approx 2 mins. Make into a loaf, or anything really!!
Bake 325 for 30 mins turn off oven and leave In for addition 10 mins.
