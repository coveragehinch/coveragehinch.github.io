Becoming an entrepreneur in the online space can be difficult, especially if you’re not sure what kind of business to start with or how to actually start one. Some common ideas that you can come across on this topic are things like drop shipping. But there are DEFINITELY better ideas.

There's actually never been a better time for entrepreneurs to start online businesses than now. And if you asked me, one of the most viable things I'd probably do myself if I was starting out would be becoming a virtual assistant.

Virtual assistance involves providing online support services like customer support, simple tech support, email marketing, and simple website updates, which can help a lot of small online businesses out there who need a hand.
