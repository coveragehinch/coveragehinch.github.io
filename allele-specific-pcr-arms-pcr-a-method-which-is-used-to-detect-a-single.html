PASA .... PCR Amplification of Specific Allele

AS- PCR ...for Allele-Specific PCR. 


In all these acronyms, everytime we are using the word “Allele”...

the reason being is its specific amplification capacity for a specific allele or DNA sequence. 


So,
What is ARMS or Allele-Specific PCR? 

As we know Alleles are alternative forms of a gene.

When a mutation occurs, it give rise to, two different alleles for a gene;

one type is wild or normal and onother one is mutant.


The Allele-specific PCR has the ability to detect this specific allele.
