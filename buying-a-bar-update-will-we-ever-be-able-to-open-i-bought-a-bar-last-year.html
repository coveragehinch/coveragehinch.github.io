I bought a bar last year with hopes of doing a quick remodel and opening it up again soon. Well, that idea quickly went by the wayside as we have run into a number of issues. Covid did not help but the real culprit was me!

I decided to change the floor plan too much and the city made us bring in an architect and engineer. Those two issues have added months to the timeline. We are hoping to be close to getting papers from the engineer and architect but until then it is a waiting game.

Don't worry, we have still had some drama at the bar while we wait!
