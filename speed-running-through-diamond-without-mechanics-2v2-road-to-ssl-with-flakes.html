It should be noted though, that I will be able to utilize more mechanics the higher rank we get, this is because if I just do nothing we of course won't win in the highest ranks! I will try to keep it as simple and easy so that you guys can hopefully use the strategies and easily rank up yourself! Thanks for watching and any feedback is always greatly appreciated!
