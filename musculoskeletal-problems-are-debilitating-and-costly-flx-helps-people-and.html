Musculoskeletal disorders (MSDs) are a major health issue and a leading cause of occupational ill-health in Britain, accounting for 30% of sick days.

They can have a life-changing effect on the health and wellbeing of employees and an adverse effect on the commercial performance of businesses too. But many MSDs can be avoided, or their symptoms reduced, with exercises which improve the way the body moves.

FLX app, is a world-first digital health product proven to help employers both manage and prevent musculoskeletal problems in the workforce without a referral to a healthcare professional.

FLX app empowers employees to take responsibility for their own health. It creates a personalised experience for each user and, whether they’re looking to reduce pain or improve their musculoskeletal health, it responds to suit their progress over time.

Informed by research into MSDs involving over 4,500 people over a 15-year period, FLX app is based on algorithms proven in real-world clinical settings with impressive results. It reduces pain levels and helps employees get back to work reducing the costs and business disruption MSDs can cause.

A healthy workforce is a happy and productive workforce. The FLX app allows businesses to take an active approach to their employees’ musculoskeletal health.
