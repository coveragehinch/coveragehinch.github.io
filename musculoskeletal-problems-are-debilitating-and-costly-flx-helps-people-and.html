Musculoskeletal disorders (MSDs) are a major health issue and a leading cause of occupational ill-health in Britain, accounting for 30% of sick days.

They can have a life-changing effect on the health and wellbeing of employees and an adverse effect on the commercial performance of businesses too. But many MSDs can be avoided, or their symptoms reduced, with exercises which improve the way the body moves.
