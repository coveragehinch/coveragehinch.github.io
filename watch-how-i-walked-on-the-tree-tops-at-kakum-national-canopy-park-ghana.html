In this video is a documentary or vlog I made on my hike at the Kakum national canopy park in the Central region of Ghana about 175km from Accra. The Kakum national park is a forest reserve where we can find the canopy walkway in Ghana.
This ecotourism we made at the Kakum canopy walkway was super amazing experience since I was scared of height yet I was walking on tree tops ranging from 11 feet to 40 feet high. But before then, me and my team made our bookings, gathered our hiking kits, paid the entrance fees and the tour begun.
