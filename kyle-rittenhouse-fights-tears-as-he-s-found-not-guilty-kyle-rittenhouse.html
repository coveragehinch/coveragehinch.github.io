'Kyle Rittenhouse FIGHTS Tears as he's Found NOT GUILTY'

Kyle Rittenhouse has been cleared of all charges relating to the shooting to death of two men, and wounding of a third during protests in Kenosha, Wisconsin.

The 18-year-old fought back tears as he was found not guilty after the jury deliberated for days.

Rittenhouse had gone to Kenosha alongside other self-described militia members armed with an assault rifle.
