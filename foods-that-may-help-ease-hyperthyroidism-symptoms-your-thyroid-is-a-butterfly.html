Your thyroid is a butterfly-shaped gland at the front of your neck. It produces hormones that regulate your metabolic rate, heart and digestive function, muscle control, brain development, mood, and bone maintenance.

You have hyperthyroidism, or overactive thyroid, when your thyroid gland produces more hormones than it should.

Treatment is necessary because an overactive thyroid may cause heart problems, bone loss, and a variety of other health disorders.
