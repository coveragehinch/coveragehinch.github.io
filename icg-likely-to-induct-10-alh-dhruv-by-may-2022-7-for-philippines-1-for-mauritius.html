The Maritime version of Dhruv cost around Rs110 Crore. It includes an on-board weather radar, an electro-optical pod, a searchlight, a tracker beam, emergency flotation gear, a VHF homing device, a traffic collision avoidance device, a rescue hoist that is anchored just below the main rotors, and a slithering device for marine commandoes.
