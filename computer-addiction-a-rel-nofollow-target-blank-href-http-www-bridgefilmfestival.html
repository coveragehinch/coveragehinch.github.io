The dangers of computer addiction. This clever, funny and all too true, mixed media PSA depicts the symptoms of spending way to much time on the computer. How about Carpal tunnel syndrome? 0:18

Computer addiction symptoms.
Are you up all night playing computer games? Are you falling asleep in Class? What about homework? Are your grades slipping? Are you lying about game playing? Choosing the internet over spending time with friends? Are you irritable when not on the computer? Are you not eating or bathing regularly? Are you experiencing carpal tunnel syndrome?
