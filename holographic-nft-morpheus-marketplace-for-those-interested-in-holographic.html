We are tokenizing history & cultural NFT using 3D scans on the Morpheus Project marketplace.

2. Anyone with the 3D model can join our platform and upload the model on our software to generate holographic content.
More on this link: https://www.community.morpheus.art/post/making-your-first-holo-nft-art

3. Yes, we do have agreements with museums and other organisations.
We have the Holo-MUSEUM platform: https://www.holo-museum.com/ for museums' artefact collection online exhibition. Besides preserving history, the Morpheus Project is a financial tool for museums and galleries to support their digitisation projects.
