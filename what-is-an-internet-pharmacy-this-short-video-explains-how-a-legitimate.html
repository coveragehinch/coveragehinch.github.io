This short video explains how a legitimate Internet pharmacy, such as White Pharmacy, works. It demonstrates clearly the straightforward process and shows how convenient it is to use an online pharmacy for your prescription only medicines. UK doctors review the medical information supplied and, if appropriate, write an electronic prescription.

White Pharmacy follows all UK regulations and only uses UK approved products.

The pharmacy was inspected by the GPhC on 19th May 2014 and found to be compliant in all aspects.
