When a young woman saw a dog roaming her neighborhood, she called it over. The pit bull seemed friendly, but when her little dog, Max, came outside, all hell broke loose. All of a sudden, an Amazon delivery driver appeared, putting herself in harm's way and blocking the pit bull so the lady and her dog could get inside. Inside Edition reunited the heroic driver and the woman and pup she rescued.
