The Israel Philharmonic celebrates 85 years and the IPO Foundation and the American Friends of the IPO present a world-wide Virtual Birthday Celebration!

Sunday, December 26, 2021 At 21:00 Israel time - and at the same time around the world.
Never before seen archive material with musical legends Arturo Toscanini, Leonard Bernstein, Zubin Mehta and Isaac Stern, featuring IPO Music Director Lahav Shani and presented by Golden Globe and Emmy Award nominee, Israeli actress Shira Haas ("Unorthodox").

The event will be held in English and will be broadcast on medici.tv and on YouTube.
To register and to receive a reminder, please click here: https://gala2021.ipo.co.il/
Access to the Virtual Birthday Celebration is free of charge.

This special Birthday Celebration will bring together the worldwide IPO family of friends and supporters and help raise funds to secure the Orchestra's future.
During and after the event it will be possible to donate online to the Orchestra.
Contributions are recognized for tax purposes under section 46 of the Income Tax Ordinance
