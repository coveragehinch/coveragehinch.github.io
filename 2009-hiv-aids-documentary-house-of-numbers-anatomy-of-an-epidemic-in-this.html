The film follows the director Brent Leung going on a journey across the world exploring the so-called HIV/AIDS epidemic. Brent gets first hand interviews with all of the major players involved, including the founders of HIV, the founder of the PCR test, and presidential health advisors. These candid interviews shine a light that many establishment figures would rather sweep under the rug.
