Jenna Lyons, ex-Creative Director of J.Crew, designer and commentator, opened her doors to ELLE UK, as part of our new video series My Style, My Space, for a guided tour of the spacious SoHo loft, so that we might all learn a thing or two from its arrangement.

From a mantlepiece laden with impressive works of art to a walk-in shoe wardrobe, Lyons' home is unsurprisingly one we dream of.
