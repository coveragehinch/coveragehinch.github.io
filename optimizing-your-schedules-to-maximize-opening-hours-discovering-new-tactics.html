-Discovering new tactics to stretch employee’s hours in the most efficient, labor-cost effective way has been on top of mind for operations and managers over the past 12 months. Technology-driven solutions have quickly become the best, most sought after method in combating the staffing shortage and ensuring doors stay open during peak business hours.

This webinar covers:

-The in’s and out’s of modern scheduling practices.
-Input definitions of data-driven scheduling.
How forecasted data can drive ROI and lower labor costs.
