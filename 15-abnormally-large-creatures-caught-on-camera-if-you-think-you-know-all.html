Big Jake - World's Tallest Horse

Horses are pretty humongous as it is, but Big Jake is a horse unlike any other. When Big Jake was born, he was about 240 pounds which isn’t that unusual for a horse. However, he just kept growing. And growing... And growing. Big Jake reached a height of 6 feet, 10 inches, and weighed nearly 2500 pounds as an adult - which is about 2 times the size of an average horse.

Big Jake broke the Guinness World Record for the tallest horse to ever live in 2010 and held the title ever since. Big Jake passed away in June 2021 at the age of 20 with no horse coming close to his size to this date.

Chinese Giant Salamander

Salamanders are pretty tiny animals - only going up to 6 inches at the most. However, the Chinese Giant Salamander is a fascinating cross between a lizard and a frog going up to - not six inches, but six feet in terms of their length.

These reptilian creatures are roughly the size of a dog with their huge tails making up for most of their bodies. There are about 50,000 Chinese Giant Salamanders left in the whole world, meaning that these animals are officially a critically endangered species.
