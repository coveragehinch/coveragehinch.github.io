Based just outside Swansea, Jordan explains how valuable Property Training was to him because he wanted to live life on his terms by replacing his salary. After attending Property Networking meetings such as the Swansea property investors network (pin), and reading Simon Zutshi’s book Property Magic, this led him to decide to invest in his own property education by joining the Property Mastermind (MM28).

Find out more about Jordan’s main Property Investing Strategy of ‘Solving People’s Problems’. As a result, he has been involved with Luxury Co Living, HMO Property Investing (House of Multiple Occupancy) and has learnt How to Do Lease Option Agreements.

His first deal, a 7 Bed HMO Property from a Motivated Seller, was sourced using Landlord Letters.
