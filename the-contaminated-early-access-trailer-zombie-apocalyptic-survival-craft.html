"The Contaminated" is an open world survival craft base building game, made by a single developer, set on a zombie-apocalyptic world.
The game map is seamless and procedurally generated.
You need to craft items and weapons, upgrade your technology, and eventually build a plane to escape.
Good luck, survivor.
