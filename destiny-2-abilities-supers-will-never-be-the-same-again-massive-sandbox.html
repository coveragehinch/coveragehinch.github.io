Bungie is making one of the biggest changes they've ever made to the sandbox: they are breaking up ability cooldowns. No longer will all grenades be on the same cooldown, all melee on the same cooldowns. Not only that, but significant changes are being made to super energy and how we'll generate it. PvP is targeted as well, with Bungie hoping to promote primary gun battles and reduce ability spam. Lots to go over, so grab a seat.
