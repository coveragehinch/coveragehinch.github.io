Airbus’ first A321XLR has entered the final assembly phase in Hamburg. With manufacturer serial number 11000, the aircraft will act as a test and certification aircraft ahead of the type’s expected entry into service with Middle East Airlines in 2023. The first flight of the plane is expected next year. Unfortunately, Airbus has only made photos publicly available. Nonetheless, let’s take a look at this exciting new jet being prepared by Airbus!
