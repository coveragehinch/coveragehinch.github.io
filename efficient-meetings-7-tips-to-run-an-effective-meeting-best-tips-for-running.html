BEST TIPS FOR RUNNING AN EFFECTIVE MEETING // HOW TO RUN AN EFFICIENT MEETING

I'm sharing 7 of my meeting management tips to increase the productivity of your next meeting. Follow these meeting tips to make your next business meetings are as efficient and effective as possible.

MEETING PREPARATION
- Ban update meetings (this is the number one time waster)
- Prep documentation in advance
- Have a meeting box (have supplies at your finger tips)

MEETING
- Arrive early to set up the meeting
- Use tools so participants know whats expected (Meeting Rules / Time Outs / Parking Lots)
- Summarize action items at end of meeting

POST MEETING
- Send meeting summary within 24 hours of meeting
