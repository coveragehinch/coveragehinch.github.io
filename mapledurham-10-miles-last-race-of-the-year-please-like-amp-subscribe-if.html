Continuing our family tradition, last week I took part in Mapledurham 10, a 10 mile cross-country/trail race near Reading. As you may know I've not been running a lot recently, so I was a bit nervous about getting out - originally I planned to do the 10k but stepped up to the 10 miles challenge and loved it!
This year hasn't been plain sailing when it comes to running, but I'm so pleased I did this race and most importantly, LOVED IT!
