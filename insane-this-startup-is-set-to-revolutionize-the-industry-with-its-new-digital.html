The amazing technology we've just spent the last thirty seconds or so describing is going to be built by a company named StoreDot.

The batteries they are working on are a lot more interesting. StoreDot, today, is a pioneer of extreme fast-charging battery technology for electric vehicles. The company has been very involved in developing ground-breaking battery technologies based on the design and synthesis of both organic and inorganic compounds.

Over the past few years, StoreDot has managed to revolutionize the conventional Li-ion battery by designing and synthesizing proprietary organic and inorganic compounds.
