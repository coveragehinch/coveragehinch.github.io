Many young drivers have made their debut on the track in recent years, racing with the greatest of the best in the world, but if you had to pick one driver whose career has exploded since the beginning, it would be none other than Max Verstappen. The 23-year-old has 15 grand prix victories, eight pole positions, and 50 podium finishes under his belt, and if things go his way, he may win the championship this year. Max has broken numerous records in just six years, owing to his early achievements in the sport. His strong performances have earned him the respect and adoration of fans all over the world. Only one race remains in the 2021 season, and the battle between Lewis and Max is still very tight. With such cut throat competition and a much awaited outcome to see what happens on Sunday in Abu Dhabi, it’s not surprising that many drivers have opposing viewpoints when predicting the 2021 season’s outcome. Many support Hamilton but a fair share have their bets on The Pride of the Dutch, Max Verstappen.
