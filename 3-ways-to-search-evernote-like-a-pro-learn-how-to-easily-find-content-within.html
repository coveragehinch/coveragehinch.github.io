Learn how to easily find content within your Evernote account’s thousands of notes. You can search for handwritten content, text within PDFs and images.

Filter your search by notes from within specific spaces or notebooks, created by specific work colleagues, containing images, Google Drive attachments, office documents, audio files, YouTube links as well as specific tags.

Use the advanced search syntax with operators that swiftly narrow down your search to text from only within the note’s title (intitle:), contains either of the search terms (any:) or even notes that aren’t tagged (-tag:*) and more!

BONUS: learn how to save searches and add them to shortcuts, so you can find what matters with a couple of clicks.
