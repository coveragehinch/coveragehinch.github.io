It's a new year and many are contemplating what kind of new and improved version of themselves they want to be in the new year. Some ladies have asked us how to be more feminine when they just "don't feel like it" or they might be "exhausted" at practicing femininity. Well ladies....we are here to explain why you might feel this way and why you are wearing what we like to call a "femininity mask".

Today we get nitty gritty as to why you might be inconsistent with femininity and 8 tips to become a feminine woman at your core. Stop wearing a femininity mask!
