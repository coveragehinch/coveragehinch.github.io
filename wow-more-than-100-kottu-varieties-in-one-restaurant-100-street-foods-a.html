KOTTULABS is a restaurant situated in Nawala which first opened its doors to Sri Lankan customers in February 2018 as a shared idea of four friends. What KOTTULABS does differently is to offer customers the option to choose exactly what they want in their Kottu.

The customers are able to custom design their meal; they can choose up to 112 different Kottu dishes. Customers follow 5 simple steps:
* Select a size (medium, large, family, party)
* Pick a Protein/Proteins (Chicken, Beef, Mutton, Seafood, Mushroom, Fish, Babath, etc.)
* Select a Carb (Rotti, Chickpea, Pasta, String Hopper, Pittu)
* Choose 5 veggies (Carrot, Leek, Cabbage, Tomato, Capsicum, etc.)
* Set the spice level (Mild/ Spicy/ Extra Spicy)
